### Miele Washing Machine (WWD 660 WCS TDos, 8 kg)

An 8 kg front-loading washer with **TwinDos** auto-dispense.

- **TwinDos** two-cartridge auto-dispense (UltraPhase 1 for whites + UltraPhase 2 for colours): no need to add detergent each cycle.
- **CapDosing slot**: drop in a Miele detergent cap for sportswear / wool / down-fill loads.
- **AddLoad**: pause mid-cycle and add a forgotten item — door unlocks briefly when water level allows.
- **Programs**: Cottons / Synthetics / Delicates / QuickPowerWash / Wool / Sportswear / Outerwear (down).
- **Wash2Dry**: pairs with the Miele dryer to auto-select the matching dryer programme.
- **Capacity**: 8 kg max — don't overload.
