# Welcome

Dear Guest,

Thank you for choosing to stay at 100 Risinghurst Terrace. We trust your stay here will be more than enjoyable. For your convenience we have prepared this Guidebook to assist you during your stay. Should you experience any problems or difficulties during your stay please do not hesitate to contact us via the booking platform — your hosts (Bethan & Paddy) live in the attached unit and are very accessible.

### The Home & Neighbourhood

100 Risinghurst Terrace is a spacious family home in Lower Shotover with a peaceful mountain-view setting. The grey house sits on an elevated section with a garden, a private outdoor area, and a BBQ. Lower Shotover is ~10 minutes by car to Queenstown Airport and ~12 minutes to the town centre.

### Bedrooms & Sleeping

- **Bedroom 1 (Master):** King bed
- **Bedroom 2:** Queen bed
- **Bedroom 3:** Queen bed
- **Maximum occupancy:** 6 guests

# House Rules

- Consumable items are provided free of charge on a fair-use basis (cooking basics, tea, coffee, soaps).

### Security & Responsibility

- Please look after your valuables and secure your accommodation. Hosts are not liable for loss or damage.

### Check-In & Check-Out

- Check-in after 3pm. Check-out before 10am.
- Self check-in via lockbox on the right-hand side wall of the garage, above the bins. The key opens the front door.

### Access

- Look for postbox #100. Follow the driveway up and around to the left to the grey house straight ahead.
- Lockbox code 2604 — also sent via the booking platform before arrival.
- Garage is accessible from inside the house — used for laundry, cleaning supplies, and ski storage.

### Quiet Hours & Outdoor Spaces

- Garden area available between 7am and 10pm.
- Please keep noise levels considerate between 10pm and 7am.

### Parking

- 1 vehicle on the driveway — park in front of the front door / garage door, on the left-hand side by the rose bush.
- Leave room on the far right for the flat tenants in the attached unit (100B).
- Street parking on the main street for additional vehicles.

### Smoking, Vaping, & E-Cigarettes

- Smoking or vaping is NOT permitted inside the home.

### Pets

- Pets are NOT permitted.

### Damages

- Please respect our home as if it were your own. Notify the hosts immediately of any damage.
