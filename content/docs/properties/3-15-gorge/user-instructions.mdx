# User Instructions

### Internet / WiFi

The internet WiFi can be accessed by:
* **Network:** Unit 3
* **Password:** provided on the welcome card at check-in

The Wi-Fi modem is in the bedroom by the chair. If you have connectivity issues, switch it off and on again (unplug for 20 seconds, plug back in).

### Access & Security

* **Entry:** Walk past Whistler Apartments reception, through the corridor on the left-hand side. Unit 3 door is on the left.
* **Door Lock:** Digital lock — code provided in your check-in instructions.
* **Locking up:** Push the middle button on the door knob to lock the door on exit.
* **Note:** Unit 3 is privately managed — please do not use Whistler Apartments reception for guest services; contact us directly.

### Layout

One-bedroom apartment with an open-plan living/kitchen area and a balcony.

### Bed Configuration

* **Bedroom:** Queen bed with electric blankets (remote controls attached to each side of the bed).
* **Living area:** Sofa bed that folds out to a double bed — must be requested in advance, additional $50 charge.
* **Travel cot** available on request (stored in the wardrobe). Please notify host before arrival.
* **Highchair** available on request.

### Heating

* **Heat pump** in the living area — heats the whole apartment. Recommended setting: 23°C.
* **Panel heater** in the hallway.
* **Underfloor heating** in the bathroom — on/off switch and temperature dial on the bathroom wall.
* **Portable heater** available for the bedroom (stored in the cupboard).
* **Electric blanket** on the bed — remote attached to the side of the bed, one per side.
* **Fan** in the cupboard for summer use.

### Hot Water

* Hot water is managed by the building manager and should be instant.
* If there are issues with hot water supply, call the building manager on **03 409 0394** or use the phone by reception.

### Kitchen

* **Hob:** Electric. Mains switch at the far right of the kitchen wall (shared with the oven).
* **Parmco oven (electric):** Three knobs — temperature, timer, and function. **The timer must be turned on for the oven to heat properly.** Function settings include: top & bottom heat, top shelf with fan, light only, fan heat.
* **Haier dishwasher:** Rotate the knob to select a programme, press the centre button to start. Eco mode recommended. Tablets are under the sink.
* **Insinkerator:** Switch on the wall at the right-hand side of the kitchen, by the kettle.
* **Kettle and coffee plunger** (instant coffee only — no coffee machine).
* Tea, sugar, and basic cooking supplies provided.
* Rubbish bags under the kitchen sink.
* **Tap water is safe to drink.**

### Bathroom

* Shower.
* **Heated towel rail** — wall switch to turn on/off. Please turn off when checking out.
* **Underfloor heating** — on/off switch + temperature dial.
* **Washing machine** (top loader) is in the bathroom. Washing powder provided. Drying rack provided; can be set up near the heat pump if needed.

### Living Area

* **TV** with two remotes:
    * **Larger remote** — on/off and source selection. Set to HDMI 1 for the streaming device.
    * **Smaller remote** — navigation. Press Home for app selection.
* **Freeview app** for NZ channels, and Netflix (log in to your own account).
* **Spare lightbulbs** are in the TV cabinet.
* Black house manual on the kitchen table or TV cabinet drawer has quick-reference info.

### Balcony

* Mountain views — available for use 7am–10pm.
* **No laundry on the balcony** (body corporate rule).
* **Nothing stored on the balcony** (body corporate rule).

### Laundry

* **Washing machine** (top loader) in the bathroom. Washing powder provided.
* **Shared dryer** in the complex's communal drying/storage room — costs $4 per load (2 × $2 coins, pushed in to activate). The access code for the drying room is on the back of the apartment keychain.
* Drying rack available on request.

### Ski, Bike & Luggage Storage

* **Ski storage:** Ski/dryer room below the apartments. Use the ski lock provided.
* **Bike rack:** Outside the drying room in the car park. Use a bike lock.
* **Luggage storage** (pre-check-in / post-check-out):
    * Storage room in the garage area below reception.
    * Walk down the steep driveway from reception, turn right into the garage.
    * **Door code:** C8743
    * Alternative: Bounce.com for luggage storage in town.

### Utility Infrastructure

* **Fuse box:** In the cupboard along the hallway.
* **Flashlight:** Bottom-left drawer of the kitchen.
* Check the Aurora Energy Queenstown outage page for area-wide power information during any outage.

### Getting Around / Transportation

* **Bus:** By the Four Square in town, $5 cash or Bee Card (cheaper). Use the Transit app or orc.govt.nz for timetables.
* **Airport bus:** Number 1 bus from opposite Super Liquor on Stanley Street — $4 cash only, goes directly to the airport.
* **Uber** available in Queenstown. **QT Taxis:** 03 450 3000.
* **Queenstown Airport:** ~10–15 minutes by car.

### Nearby Shops

* **Fresh Choice** — ~5 min walk (turn right, across the road). Open until 10pm.
* **Raeward Fresh** — ~5 min walk (deli and butchers).
* **Pak'n'Save** and **Countdown** — Frankton, ~20 min drive (full supermarkets, best prices).
* **Four Square** — in town centre (more expensive but closest).

### Mid-stay Clean

* Available at additional cost on request — please contact the host to arrange.

### Check-out

* Ensure the apartment is returned to its original condition.
* Dispose of rubbish in the bins (see House Rules).
* Load the dishwasher.
* Switch off lights and heated towel rail.
* Leave bed linens in place and towels in their original rooms.
* Push the middle button on the door knob to lock the door behind you.
