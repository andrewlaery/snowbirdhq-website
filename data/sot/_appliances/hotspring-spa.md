### HotSpring Spa (Hot Tub)

Hot tub on the top deck — digital controls, dual jet systems, lighting, and temperature control.

#### Using the Hot Tub
1. Check water temperature on the **digital display**.
2. **Remove the cover completely** before entering — never sit on the cover.
3. Use **jet controls** to adjust water flow.
4. Use **lighting controls** for ambiance.

#### After Use
1. **Replace the cover securely** to retain heat + keep debris out.
2. Ensure the **filtration system is running** (it's automatic; just don't unplug it).
3. Don't add chemicals yourself — we manage water chemistry.

#### Etiquette
- Sound carries to neighbours after dark — please keep voices low after 10pm.
