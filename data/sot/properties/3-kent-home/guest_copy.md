### The Home & Neighbourhood

3 Kent Street is a substantial two-level home in central Queenstown — an over-engineered 80s build with character throughout. The whole-home configuration gives you both the upstairs apartment (4 bedrooms, best views) and the downstairs apartment (2 bedrooms + extra-bed nook, floor-to-ceiling windows backing onto the Queenstown Hill walking track).

Underfloor heating runs throughout the home, set to a constant 20°C for an even, warm temperature. The kitchen has a large pantry and two dishwashers (one upstairs, one downstairs). Texture-rich, wallpapered, with thoughtful detail throughout.

The garden has fruit trees (quince, apricot, apple) and direct access to the Queenstown Hill walking track — recommended if you'd like a walk straight from the house.
