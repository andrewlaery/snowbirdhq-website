### Portable Electric Heaters (Bedrooms)

Each bedroom has a portable electric heater stored in the wardrobe.

- **Plug into a wall socket**, then turn on at the unit.
- **Thermostat dial** — start mid-range, adjust to comfort.
- Helpful on cold nights when the lounge heat pump can't reach.
- **Don't drape clothes / towels** over the heater; keep clear of bedding.
- **Switch off** when leaving for an extended period.
