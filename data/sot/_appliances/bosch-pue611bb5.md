### Bosch Induction Hob (PUE611BB5)

[User manual (PDF)](https://media3.bsh-group.com/Documents/9001867174_A.pdf)

Bosch induction cooktop that heats pots and pans directly using magnetic technology. Faster, more energy-efficient, and safer than conventional cooktops. Located on the kitchen bench.

#### Starting the hob

1. Touch the main power switch to turn on the hob.
2. Select a cooking zone by touching its corresponding circle symbol.
3. Within 10 seconds, set your desired power level (1–9) using + or − (Level 1 = lowest heat for melting/gentle warming; Level 9 = high heat for quick boiling/searing).

#### While cooking

- The hob automatically detects if suitable cookware is placed on a zone.
- Adjust power level any time by selecting the zone and pressing + or −.

#### Turning off

1. Specific zone: select it and use − to reduce to 0.
2. All zones: touch the main power switch.

#### Notes

- Hob auto-shuts-off if all zones remain unused for 20 seconds.
- Don't place metal objects (e.g. utensils) on the cooking surface.
- Use only induction-compatible cookware (a magnet should stick to the base).
