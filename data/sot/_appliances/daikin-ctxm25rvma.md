### Daikin Heat Pumps (CTXM25RVMA) — Top Floor x3

Three Daikin heat pumps on the top floor (TV nook + Bedrooms 2 & 3) — heating + cooling.

- **Remote controls** are mounted **on the walls** in each room.
- **Cooling Mode**: power on, select cooling, set temperature (22–24°C), adjust fan speed.
- **Heating Mode**: select heating, set temperature (21–23°C), allow time to reach setpoint.
- **Switch off when leaving** the property or finishing for the night.
