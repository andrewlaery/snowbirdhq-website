### Canon Multifunction Printer (FTC3147403)

Canon multifunction printer/scanner with wireless connectivity.

#### Printing
1. Connect your device to the house Wi-Fi.
2. Select the printer from your device's print settings.
3. Choose your print options.
4. Send the print job.

#### Scanning
1. Place document on the scanner bed.
2. Use the printer panel or the Canon app to scan.
3. Select scan quality and format.
4. Save or send the scanned document.
