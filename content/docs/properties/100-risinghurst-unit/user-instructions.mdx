#### Important — gas hob / oven power switch

* The gas hob and oven have a **power switch located above the washing machine**. This must be turned **ON** before either will ignite.
* **If the stove or oven won't light:** check that switch first. This is the most common source of "gas isn't working" reports.
* **Oven ignition:** after the power switch is on, hold down the clock symbol and the symbol next to it simultaneously for a few seconds until it lights up and stops flashing.
