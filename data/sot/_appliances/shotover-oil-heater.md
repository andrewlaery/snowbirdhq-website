### Oil Column Heater (Bedrooms)

A portable oil-filled column heater in each bedroom for additional warmth on cold nights.

- **Plug into the wall socket**, then turn the dial / switch on the unit.
- **Thermostat**: usually a numbered dial — start mid-range, adjust to comfort.
- **Slow to warm up** — oil-filled units take 10–15 minutes to reach full output. They also retain heat after switching off.
- **Safety**: don't drape clothes / towels over the heater; keep at least 30 cm clearance from bedding.
- **Switch off when leaving the room** for an extended period.
