### Washer + Dryer

A washing machine and a dryer in the laundry.

- **Washing machine taps** must be **turned on** before running a cycle.
- **CRITICAL — known electrical issue**: the laundry power point is faulty and trips under combined load.
    - **Do NOT run the washer and dryer simultaneously** — this trips the circuit.
    - Use the **extension cord provided** to the power point on the **opposite side of the laundry** if you need to run both.
    - **If the circuit trips**, there's a **red reset button on the plug** — push it to restore power.
- Washing **powder is provided** (may run low on longer stays — let us know and we'll top it up).
- Additional soaps and supplies are in the **big box in the cupboard by the main entrance**.
