### Panel Heater (Bedroom)

Portable electric panel heater stored in the **bedroom cupboard** — additional heating for the bedroom on cold nights.

- Plug into the wall socket, then turn on at the unit.
- Adjust the dial for desired warmth.
- **Don't drape clothes / towels** over the heater.
- **Switch off when leaving** the room for an extended period or at check-out.
