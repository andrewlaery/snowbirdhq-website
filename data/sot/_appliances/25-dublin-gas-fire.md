### Gas Fire (Upstairs Lounge)

The gas fire is remote-controlled. Remote is on the wall in the same room.

#### Operating

1. Press the **top button** on the black switch directly under the remote (this powers the gas fire).
2. Press **Power** on the remote. Confirm the temperature appears on the digital display (if not, press Power again).
3. The fireplace takes a few seconds to ignite.
4. The gas fire is on a **60-minute timer** — repeat the steps to reignite if needed.

> **See House Rules → Upstairs Gas Fire** for safety policy (turn off when leaving, child supervision, clearance from flammables).
