### Bosch Dishwasher (SMU2ITS01A)

[User manual (PDF)](https://media3.bsh-group.com/Documents/9001720420_A.pdf)

Bosch dishwasher with multiple wash programs. Located in the kitchen under the bench.

#### Starting

1. Open the door and load:
   - Cups, glasses, small items in the top basket
   - Pots, pans, plates in the bottom basket
   - Cutlery in the cutlery basket, sharp points down
2. Add detergent to the dispenser (15–25 ml or 1 tablet).
3. Close the door firmly.
4. Press the ON/OFF button.
5. Select a program:
   - Eco Wash — everyday, most efficient
   - Auto Wash — adjusts to soil level
   - Quick 1h — lightly soiled, fast
6. Press Start.

#### End of cycle

- Wait until the display shows 0h:00m.
- Crack the door for 30 minutes for cool-down/drying.
- Unload bottom basket first, then top.
- Press ON/OFF to turn off.

#### Tips

- Don't pre-rinse — just scrape food off.
- Use rinse aid for better drying.
- Leave the door slightly ajar between uses to prevent odours.
