## Property Orientation

### Internet / Wi-Fi

Network and password are shown above in the Quick Reference.

### Heating & Cooling

BaseCamp has a **Daikin FVXG50K2V1B Nexura** floor-standing reverse-cycle air conditioner (~5.0 kW, heating + cooling) with a front radiant heat panel for fast, draught-free warmth. **A second heat pump in the hallway** provides additional heating; a **wall-mounted panel heater** and a **portable plug-in heater** are also available for extra warmth on cold nights. Adjust your clothing first; contact the host if anything feels off. See the Daikin Nexura entry under Appliances → Heating for operating details.

When leaving the house in cooler weather, please close all doors and windows so the home stays at a comfortable temperature for your return.

### Kitchen

A fully-equipped gourmet kitchen — Miele dishwasher (AutoDos), Beko electric oven, Bosch induction cooktop, Beko built-in microwave + grill, Beko side-by-side fridge/freezer with external water + ice dispenser, electric kettle, blender, baking sheet, full cooking utensils. Cooking basics (oil, salt, pepper, tea, coffee) are provided. **Tap water is safe to drink.** See the Appliances → Kitchen section for per-device operating instructions.

### Bathrooms

Three bathrooms, each fully stocked with towels, shampoo, conditioner, body wash, and hand wash. Hair dryer in the master ensuite. Heated towel rails in the main bathroom — please switch off when checking out.

### Laundry

In-unit Miele washer + heat-pump dryer. Drying rack and iron / ironing board provided. See the Appliances → Laundry section for operating details.

### Outdoor

Private deck overlooking Lake Wakatipu and The Remarkables. Manicured garden with lawn and cypress trees. Outdoor dining furniture and a gas BBQ — see the Appliances → Outdoor section for BBQ operating instructions.

### Utility / Power Outages

Check the [Aurora Energy Queenstown outage page](https://www.aurora.energy/outages) for area-wide power information during any outage.

### Getting Around

The Queenstown CBD is approximately 5–7 minutes by car. Frequent public bus services run from the bottom of the hill — check the Orbus Queenstown timetable for current routes. Allow 15–20 minutes for a leisurely walk into town.

### Rubbish & Recycling

- Red bins (rubbish): collected weekly.
- Blue bins (glass) and yellow bins (mixed recycling) alternate weekly.
- Verify the schedule at [QLDC rubbish + recycling collection](https://www.qldc.govt.nz/services/rubbish-recycling/rubbish-recycling-collection/) or check which bins the neighbours have out.
- Place bins on the street curb by 7am or the night before. Collect them off the street by collection-day evening.

### Children's Amenities

Travel cot, high chair, and a small selection of toys are available on request — please ask the host before arrival so they can be set up.
