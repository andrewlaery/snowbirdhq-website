### Bosch Dishwasher
Built-in Bosch dishwasher.

- **Isolation switch** is at the **bottom rear of the left-hand cupboard**.

#### Operating
1. Open the front.
2. Press the **power button**.
3. Select wash cycle.
4. Press **START**.

⚠ **Use only the dishwasher tablets provided.** Using washing liquid will cause a fault and **repair fees will apply**.
