### Electric Blanket (Queen Bed)

A dual-control electric blanket on the queen bed in the bedroom.

- **One remote per side** — attached to each side of the bed.
- **Settings**: typically 1 (low) to 9 (high). Pre-warm the bed for 10–15 minutes before getting in, then drop to a low setting overnight.
- **Switch off** when getting out of bed in the morning.
- **Don't fold or sit heavily on the blanket** while it's running.
