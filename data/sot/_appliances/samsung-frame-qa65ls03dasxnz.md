### Samsung Frame TV (QA65LS03DASXNZ) — Living Room

[Samsung support](https://www.samsung.com/nz/support/model/QA65LS03DASXNZ/#downloads)

Samsung Frame TV — works as a regular TV, and as an art display when not in use (Art Mode).

#### Power

- Press the power button on the remote to turn on.
- Press once to switch into Art Mode (low-power art display).
- Press and hold to fully turn off.

#### Watching

- Channel buttons (∧/∨) to change channels.
- Volume buttons (+/−) for sound.
- HOME button (⌂) for apps and features.
- SETTINGS (⚙) for picture and sound adjustments.

#### Smart features

- Voice Assistant — press and hold the microphone button, then speak.
- Smart Hub — HOME button accesses Netflix, YouTube, etc.

#### Remote

- USB-C charging plus a solar panel — leave face-up when not in use to keep it charged.

Full guide available in-TV at *Settings → Support → Open User Guide*.
