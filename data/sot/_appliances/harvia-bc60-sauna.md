### Alpine Saunas Barrel Sauna with Harvia Vega Heater (BC60)

Outdoor barrel-style wooden sauna with panoramic windows.

#### Before You Start — Three Things Must Be On
1. **Isolation switch** — on the **right side of the sauna unit**, ground level.
2. **Light switch** — at the **top of the stairs inside the house**.
3. **Timer dial** — inside the sauna (see below).

If the sauna won't heat after turning the timer, the isolation switch or light switch is usually the cause.

#### Preparing the Sauna
- Turn on the sauna **30–45 minutes before use**.
- Inside the sauna, two control dials labelled **1 TIMER** (left) and **2 HEAT** (right):

![Sauna timer and heat dials (Harvia), labelled 1 TIMER and 2 HEAT](/properties/6-25-belfast/sauna-dial.jpg)

- **2 HEAT (Temperature Dial)**: controls the heat level. For normal use, turn **to HIGH**.
- **1 TIMER (Timer Dial)**: controls when and how long the heater runs.
    - Turn the timer into the **WHITE zone** to start heating immediately.
    - ⚠️ If turned **past white into the BLACK zone**, this sets a **start delay** — useful if heading out and you want the sauna ready when you return.
- The sauna typically reaches **80–90°C** within **15–30 minutes** once started.
- Ensure the sauna door is closed properly to maintain heat.
- Have water available for hydration.

#### Adding Steam (Optional)
- Pour a **small amount of water** over the heated stones using the ladle.
- Add water **gradually** to avoid sudden steam bursts.
- Never pour excessive water at once.

#### Using the Sauna
- Enter once the sauna has reached a comfortable temperature.
- **Sit on a towel** for hygiene.
- **Limit initial sessions to 10–15 minutes.**
- **Exit immediately** if you feel dizzy or unwell.
- **Please turn the sauna OFF** once you have finished using it.
