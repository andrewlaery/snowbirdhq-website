### Bathroom Underfloor Heating

Electric underfloor heating in the bathroom.

- **On/off switch + temperature dial** on the bathroom wall.
- **Slow to warm** — allow ~30 minutes for noticeable warmth on the floor; ~1–2 hours for full effect.
- **Switch off when checking out** — operator request, both the underfloor heating AND the heated towel rail.
