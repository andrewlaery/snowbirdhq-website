### Daikin Nexura Floor-Standing Reverse-Cycle AC (FVXG50K2V1B, ~5.0 kW)

A floor-standing reverse-cycle inverter air conditioner with a front radiant heat panel — heats and cools.

- **Radiant heat panel**: the front face of the unit warms like a radiator for fast, draught-free heating. Useful when you walk into a cool room and want immediate warmth.
- **Hot Start (heating mode)**: waits until the unit is warm before circulating air, so you don't get cold draughts when starting from cold.
- **Modes**: Heat / Cool / Dry / Auto / Fan. Use the remote to switch.
- **Econo mode**: caps peak power consumption — useful overnight or in mild weather.
- **Whisper-quiet mode**: drops fan noise by 2–3 dB — for sleeping rooms.
- **Setpoints**: aim for 20–22 °C in heating mode; 24–26 °C in cooling. Don't fight extremes.
- **When leaving the home in cooler weather**, please close all doors and windows so the unit doesn't work against open ventilation.
