### Parmco Electric Oven

Three knobs: temperature, timer, function.

- **The timer must be turned on for the oven to heat properly** — most common source of "oven isn't heating" confusion.
- **Function settings**: top + bottom heat / top shelf with fan / light only / fan heat.
- Set function, set temperature, then turn the timer dial to the desired duration (or auto-bake position).
- Mains switch at the far right of the kitchen wall (shared with the hob) must be ON.
