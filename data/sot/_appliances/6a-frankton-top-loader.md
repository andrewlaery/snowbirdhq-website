### Top-Loader Washing Machine (No Dryer)

Top-loading washing machine, located in the **bathroom**.

- **Laundry powder is provided.**
- **No in-unit dryer** — use the **drying rack provided** in the bathroom; best results with the heat pump on.
- A **shared dryer** is available in a separate building across the apartment complex (details + access in the black booklet on the kitchen table).
- **Please do NOT hang laundry on the balcony** — body corporate rule.
