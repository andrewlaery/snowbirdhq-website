### Simpson Clothes Dryer EziLoader (SDV401, 4kg)

4 kg capacity electric clothes dryer — laundry room.

1. Ensure clothes are properly **spun** from the washer first.
2. Load clothes **loosely** into the drum.
3. Select drying program.
4. Set timer if required.
5. Press **Start**.

#### After Drying
- Remove clothes promptly.
- **Clean the lint filter** between loads.
- Leave the door slightly open for ventilation.
