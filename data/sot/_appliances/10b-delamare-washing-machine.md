### Washing Machine (No Dryer)

Front-loading washing machine in the laundry room beside the kitchen.

- **Laundry powder is provided.**
- **No dryer** — use the **outdoor clothes horse** to the **left of the back door**, plus an **indoor drying rack** for damp days.
- For best results in cold weather, use the indoor rack with the **lounge heat pump on**.
- Iron and ironing board are stored in the master bedroom.
