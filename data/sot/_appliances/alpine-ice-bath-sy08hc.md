### Alpine Ice Bath with Chiller (SY-08-HC)

Wooden barrel-style **cold plunge tub** with insulated cover and dedicated chiller — back of property.

#### Using the Ice Bath
1. Check the **water temperature** is at your desired level (typically 5–10°C for cold therapy).
2. Enter **slowly and gradually** — let your body adjust.
3. **Limit initial sessions to 2–3 minutes**.
4. **Exit immediately if you feel unwell** (dizziness, numbness, shortness of breath).

#### Maintenance / Etiquette
- Keep the **cover on** when not in use to retain temperature.
- Check water temperature **before** each use.
- Follow any posted safety guidelines on the unit.

#### Operating the Chiller
- The chiller maintains the set water temperature.
- Don't unplug the chiller — it needs to keep running.
- If you hear unusual noises, please contact us.

⚠️ **Cold-water immersion is not safe for everyone.** Avoid if you have heart conditions, are pregnant, or are unsure — please check with your doctor.
