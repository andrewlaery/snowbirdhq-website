### Gas Hob

Built-in gas cooktop in the kitchen.

- **Ignition**: turn the knob and press to spark; hold for ~5 seconds after the flame catches.
- Use the **fan extractor** above the hob when cooking.
- Turn the knob fully off when finished — listen for the click.
- More detailed instructions in the **black booklet** on the kitchen table.
