## Property Orientation

### Internet / Wi-Fi

The Wi-Fi network and password are shown above in the Quick Reference. The Wi-Fi modem is located in the bedrooms upstairs.

### Layout

- **Split level** — living, kitchen and balcony on the entry floor; bedrooms and bathroom on another level connected by internal stairs.
- **No elevator.**

### Bed Configuration

- **2 bedrooms**, max 4 guests:
  - 1 × queen bed
  - 1 × king bed, **splittable into 2 singles** on request
- Please let the host know your preferred configuration before arrival.
- **Travel cot and highchair** available on request — both stored in the owner's cupboard. Please request in advance. Guests to bring their own cot sheets.

### Heating

- **Heat pump** in the living area (see Appliances → Heating). The remote is on the wooden beam in the living area — easy to miss.
- **Oil heaters** in each bedroom for additional warmth on cold nights.
- **Summer:** Bedrooms can get warm and the heat pump's reach to the upper level is limited. Please open windows for ventilation.

### Hot Water

Instant hot water via a hot water cylinder — more than enough for 4 guests under normal usage.

### Bathroom

Towels, shampoo, conditioner, body wash, and a hair dryer provided.

### Laundry

- **AEG washing machine** — see Appliances → Laundry for operating instructions.
- **No dryer** — drying rack provided.

### Tap Water

Tap water is safe to drink throughout the apartment.

### Living Area

- **Smart TV** with Freeview channels (see Appliances → Tech).
- **Board games** including Monopoly.
- **Balcony:** No shade — can be warm in direct sun. Please be mindful of noise after 8pm (see House Rules).

### Utility Infrastructure

- **Fuse box:** In the laundry room, behind the door.
- If the power trips, flip the switch up and power should return. If it trips repeatedly, something is blowing it — please contact the host.
- Check the [Aurora Energy Queenstown outage page](https://www.aurora.energy/outages) for area-wide power information during any outage.

### Getting Around

- **Walking:** Lake Wakatipu, the town centre, and Queenstown Gardens are all a few minutes' walk.
- **Intercity bus stop:** Very close to the apartment.
- **Snow Centre ski bus pickup** (winter): 5-minute walk on Shotover Street.
- **Queenstown Airport:** ~10–15 minutes by car. Taxis available on arrival — no need to pre-book. Queenstown Taxis or Uber.
- **Parking:** Free in Man Street Carpark with the QR-code lanyard provided — see House Rules for details.

### Luggage Storage

- The apartment cannot offer luggage storage due to same-day turnovers.
- **iSite Queenstown** (Clocktower Building, 22 Shotover Street — a short walk) offers paid luggage storage for guests with late flights: https://www.queenstownisite.co.nz/listing/queenstown-luggage-storage-isite/

### Check-out

- Return the key to the lockbox beside the front door.
- Start the dishwasher.
- Strip the bed linens and leave them in the bedrooms.
- Take rubbish to the bins below the steps in the alleyway.
- Standard checkout is 10:00am; late checkout to 11:00am may be possible if no same-day booking — please ask the host.
