### Bosch Dishwasher (SMU68M05AU/28)

[User manual](https://www.bosch-home.co.nz/supportdetail/product/SMU68M05AU/28)

#### Instructions

1. Carefully stack dishes into the dishwasher.
2. Place a dishwasher capsule (or powder) in the compartment inside the door, then close the door.
3. Press **On/Off**.
4. Select the desired programme — we recommend rinsing dishes before loading and using **Quick 45°**, a ~30-minute cycle.
5. Press **Start**.
6. If you open the door mid-cycle, close the door and press Start again to resume.
