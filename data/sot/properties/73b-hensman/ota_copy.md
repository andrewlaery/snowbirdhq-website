## Description

Lakeview Lodge is an amazing contemporary home overlooking Lake Wakatipu and The Remarkables. Perched on the Queenstown hillside in a very quiet cul-de-sac, the house is located just three minutes drive from the very centre of Queenstown. Perfect spot for an escape.

The house has an amazing layout with 5 spacious double sized bedrooms. 2 of which are master bedrooms with their own ensuites. The bedrooms have flexibility in the set up with either all 5 being King beds or if desired 3 of the 5 rooms can have the kings split into single beds. Open plan living downstairs with indoor outdoor flow onto deck with BBQ, Spa and fireplace

The whole house with first class facilities including spa, back to back open fireplace, open plan dinning and entertaining area

The house is rented as a whole to the guests. I am kiwi but living overseas. My brother in law Paddy looks after the property and will be able to answer any questions or be on site if needed. It is all very easy
