### Gas Fireplace

The lounge has a **gas fireplace** which heats the living area immediately.

- **1-hour timer** on the controller — push to start, push again to extend.
- **If the fireplace won't start**, check two things in order:
    1. **Power switch** on the **left side wall, down low** — sometimes accidentally turned off. Make sure it's ON.
    2. **Batteries in the controller** — they occasionally die. Spare batteries can be requested from the host.
- **Switch off at the controller** before checking out and before going to bed.
