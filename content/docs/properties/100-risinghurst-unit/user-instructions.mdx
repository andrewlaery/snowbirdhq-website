## Property Orientation

### Internet / Wi-Fi

Fibre connection (500/100 Mbps via Lightspeed). The Wi-Fi network and password are shown above in the Quick Reference. A **10m ethernet cable** is available on request if you'd prefer a wired connection for remote work.

### Layout

- Single-level one-bedroom unit with a sunny aspect.
- A "snug" room off the kitchen can be used as a workspace — there is a desk (1500mm × 750mm) available.

### Bed Configuration

- 1 bedroom with a king bed (medium firmness).
- Second set of towels in the wardrobe.
- **Travel cot and highchair** available on request.

### Heating & Cooling

The unit has a reverse-cycle heat pump / aircon (see Appliances → Heating). The host pre-heats the unit for winter and late arrivals.

**Sunny-day tip:** Leave the blinds open — the sun warms the flat through the sliding doors.

### Bathroom

Shower with good pressure. Extractor fan. Body wash provided. Used towels go on the shower floor or in the black container.

### Tap Water

Tap water is safe to drink throughout the unit.

### Bikes

- **Two bikes with helmets** available for guest use, hanging by the entrance.
- **Bike lock code:** 1990.
- No need to lock at the property; lock when riding into town.
- Bike pump may be needed (tyres can lose air over time) — let us know if so.
- **Routes:** Bike to Arrowtown (a beautiful ride along the river) or into Queenstown via the bike trail.

### Utility Infrastructure

Check the [Aurora Energy Queenstown outage page](https://www.aurora.energy/outages) for area-wide power information during any outage.

### Getting Around

- **Bus stop:** ~5-minute walk down the street. A Bee card may be in the kitchen drawer.
- **5 Mile Shopping Centre:** ~7-minute drive (phone shops, retail).
- **Uber** available in Queenstown.
- **Airport taxi:** approximately $50–70 NZD one way.
- **Queenstown Airport:** 10–15 minutes' drive.

### Nearby

- **Small supermarket:** ~7-minute walk.
- **Frank's Coffee / 11th Avenue by Franks:** Short walk up the hill.
- **Arrowtown:** 10 minutes by car — recommended for couples (cafes, shops, history).
- **Ayrburn:** Just outside Arrowtown, recommended for dining.
- **5 Mile Shopping Centre:** 7-minute drive.
- **Queenstown town centre:** ~10 minutes by car.

### Check-out

- Return the unit to its original condition.
- Start the dishwasher.
- Dispose of rubbish in the blue-lid recycling bin or general waste bin.
- Leave used towels on the shower floor.
- Lock all doors and windows.
- Return the key to the lockbox (unit lockbox, not the main house lockbox).
