## Property Orientation

### Internet / Wi-Fi

- **Network:** Lakeview Upper.
- **Password:** provided on the welcome card at check-in.

### Bed Configuration

- The lodge accommodates up to **8 guests**.
- **Travel cot and highchair** available on request — please let the host know in advance.

### Heating

Heating is available throughout the lodge. If you'd like guidance on any specific control, please contact us via the booking platform.

### Kitchen

- Fully equipped kitchen.
- **Tap water is safe to drink** throughout the lodge.

### Bathroom

Towels, shampoo, body wash and a hair dryer provided.

### Laundry

Washing facilities available — please let us know if you'd like any assistance.

### Outdoor

Off-street parking and shared external areas with **73B (lower level)**.

### Utility Infrastructure

Check the [Aurora Energy Queenstown outage page](https://www.aurora.energy/outages) for area-wide power information during any outage.

### Getting Around

- **Walking:** ~20 minutes downhill to Queenstown town centre; longer uphill.
- **Taxi back from town:** ~$10.
- **Uber** available in Queenstown.
- **Queenstown Airport:** ~15 minutes' drive.
- **Closest ski field:** Coronet Peak; The Remarkables (~15 min further).

### Nearby Shops

- **FreshChoice** — 3 minutes' drive.
- **Pak'n'Save** (Frankton) — best prices, a short drive.
- **New World** (Frankton) — Remarkables Park.
- **Countdown** (Frankton) — Five Mile shopping centre.

### Check-out

- Return the key to the lockbox.
- Dispose of rubbish in the bins at the property.
- Switch off lights and heating.
- Leave bed linens in place and towels in their original rooms.
- Lock all windows and doors.

### A Note on Lakeview Lodge Upper

Some specific operational details (appliance models, fuse box location, mains water shutoff) are still being finalised. If you need anything specific during your stay — appliance instructions, additional supplies, or any other help — please message us via the booking platform and we'll respond quickly.
