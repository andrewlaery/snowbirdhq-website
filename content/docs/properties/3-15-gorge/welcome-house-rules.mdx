# Welcome

### Dear Guest

Thank you for choosing to stay at Unit 3, Whistler Apartments. We trust your stay here will be more than enjoyable. For your convenience we have prepared a Guidebook including House Rules to assist you during your stay. Should you experience any problems or difficulties with regard to the home during your stay please do not hesitate to contact us via the booking platform. We are here to help!

### The Home & Neighbourhood

Unit 3 is a cosy one-bedroom apartment in the Whistler Apartments complex at 15–17 Gorge Road, ideal for couples. You have a balcony with mountain views, a short walk to the town centre, and Fresh Choice supermarket and cafes nearby.

**Important:** Although Unit 3 is in the Whistler Apartments building, it is **privately managed**. The Whistler Apartments reception and its services do NOT apply to your stay. Please contact us directly via the booking platform for any issues — do not approach the building's reception.

# House Rules

### A Quick Note

- We're delighted to share our apartment with you in the Whistler complex
- To maintain our great relationship with our neighbours and comply with body corporate rules, we kindly ask that you follow these house guidelines
- Please remove your shoes inside to help keep our apartment clean and comfortable — no shoes or ski gear inside the apartment

### House Items

* Consumable items in the apartment are provided free of charge on a fair use basis (tea, sugar, basic cooking supplies, soaps & body wash).
* Unused items remain property of the home.

### Security & Responsibility

* Please help us make your stay an enjoyable and memorable one. Please look after your valuables and secure your accommodation. Despite protective measures we are unable to assume any responsibility and will not be liable for any loss or damage to your possessions in or around the property.
* The hosts/owners are not responsible for any accidents, injuries, or illness that occur while on the premises or its facilities, as well as loss of personal belongings or valuables of the guest.

### Check-In & Check-Out

* Check-In after: 3pm
* Check-Out before: 10am

**When checking out:**
* ensure the apartment appears as it was when you first arrived
* dispose of all rubbish and recycling in the bins (see below)
* load the dishwasher with all dirty dishes
* turn off all lights and any electrical items you have used. Do not switch off utility items such as Wi-Fi or modems
* turn off the heated towel rail and underfloor heating
* leave bed linens in place and towels in their original rooms
* ensure the apartment is secured (close and lock all windows and doors)
* Push the middle button on the door knob to lock the door behind you

### Access

* **How to find Unit 3:** Walk past the Whistler Apartments reception doors, through the corridor on the left-hand side. Unit 3 door is on the left.
* **Front Door:** Digital lock — enter the code provided in your check-in instructions (sent via the booking platform 24 hours before arrival).
* **Locking up:** Push the middle button on the door knob to lock the door internally on exit.

### Guests

* The maximum total number of guests is 2 (up to 3 with the sofa bed, additional $50 charge — must be requested in advance).
* The guest responsible for registering the booking must be 25 years old or older.
* All guests staying must be registered with the host beforehand.

### Visitors & events/parties

* Strictly registered guests only. No unregistered visitors.
* Parties are NOT allowed.
* No confetti, glitter, or excessive rubbish allowed otherwise an additional cleaning fee will be charged.

### Quiet Hours & Outdoor Spaces

- Balcony available 7am–10pm. Quiet hours 10pm–7am.
- No music or amplified sound on the balcony between 8pm - 8am
- After 8pm, continue activities indoors with windows and doors closed

Note: Under the District Plan, noise limits are:
- Daytime (8am - 8pm): 50dBA LAeq (15 min)
- Nighttime (8pm - 8am): 40dBA LAeq

### Parking

- **Free on-site parking** in the apartment complex car park.
- Spaces are not individually allocated — park in any available spot.
- Car park is accessible from both Gorge Road and Weaver Street.
- **Town parking:** Lakefront is free (no restrictions, but fills quickly). Camp Street and Man Street carparks are the cheapest paid options.

### Body Corporate Rules

- **No laundry hung on the balcony.**
- **Nothing stored on the balcony.**
- **No shoes or ski gear inside the apartment.**

### Rubbish & Recycling

* **RED bin:** General waste
* **BLUE bin:** Glass only
* **YELLOW bin:** Cardboard, tin, and hard plastic (no soft plastic)
* External bins are in the car park, just below the steps.
* Rubbish bags are under the kitchen sink.
* A small recycling bin is provided inside the apartment.

### Ski & Bike Storage

- **Ski storage:** Ski/dryer room below the apartments. Use the ski lock provided (shared space).
- **Bike rack:** Outside the drying room in the car park. Use a bike lock.
- Please do not store skis or bikes inside the apartment or on the balcony.

### Littering

* NO littering. If any part of the apartment or balcony is found to be littered requiring additional cleaning, an additional cleaning charge may apply.

### Commercial photography

* Commercial photography is not allowed unless guests have written permission from the host beforehand.

### Pets

* Pets are NOT permitted.

### Smoking, vaping, & e-cigarettes

* Smoking or vaping is NOT permitted inside or on the balcony.
* There may be a $250+ additional fee added for smoking inside and/or improper disposal of cigarette butts.

### Security cameras & other equipment

* Tampering with any security, safety, or utility devices (e.g., smoke detectors, internet, connectivity, power) will result in the immediate termination of your booking without a refund.

### Damages/breakages & furniture

* Please respect our apartment as if it is your own.
* Only use appliances, equipment, and furniture for their intended uses.
* Removing any items from the property is not permitted.

### Acceptance of House Rules

* By accepting this reservation, it is agreed that the House Rules are understood and accepted, and all guests are assuming the risk of any harm arising from their use of the premises or others whom they invite to use the premises.
