### Rinnai Gas Fire (Second Lounge)

A Rinnai gas fire in the second lounge.

- **Press the ON button** to start.
- **Arrow buttons** adjust the temperature setpoint.
- **Press 'Set'** to display the current temperature.
- **Switch off when leaving the room** for an extended period — and especially at checkout.
- **Don't place anything on or against** the unit; keep clearance.
