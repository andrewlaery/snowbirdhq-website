### Fisher & Paykel Gas Cooktop (PCH615FAU)

4-burner gas hob with **wok burner**, stainless steel — kitchen bench.

- **Ignition**: turn the burner control knob to ignition position; press and hold the **ignition button** until the flame catches; hold knob for ~5 seconds before releasing.
- Adjust flame to desired level.
- Use the **fan extractor** when cooking.
- Use **appropriately-sized cookware** for each burner (oversize cookware overhangs the wok burner ring).
- **Turn off the gas supply** at the wall when not in use for extended periods.
