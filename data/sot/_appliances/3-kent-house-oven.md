### Electrolux Double Oven (EVEP626DSD)
Built-in **double oven** (top oven + bottom oven), self-cleaning.

- **Isolation switch** is on the wall to the **left of the oven**.

#### Operating
1. Press the **power button** (top-left corner).
2. Press the **select button** to choose **top oven** or **bottom oven**.
3. Use the **function buttons (up/down)** to select cooking method.
4. Use **temperature +/−** to adjust temperature.
5. Use **cooking time +/−** to set timer.
6. To switch off: press the **power button (top-left)**.

#### Self-cleaning
- This is a **self-cleaning oven** — **no chemicals**.
- Follow the on-screen instructions to start a self-clean cycle.
- The oven heats very hot, turning food residue and grease into dust which can be wiped away with a damp cloth afterwards.
