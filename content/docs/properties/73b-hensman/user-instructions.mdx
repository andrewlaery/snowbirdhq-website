## Property Orientation

### Internet / Wi-Fi

- **Password:** provided on the welcome card at check-in.
- **Please leave the Wi-Fi modems running at all times** — do not unplug them.

### Owner On Site

Pete lives directly behind the lodge and is very accessible for any issues — please feel welcome to reach out **through the booking platform** first and we can connect you.

### Layout

- **Lower level:** living area, kitchen, dining, one toilet.
- **Upper level:** all 5 bedrooms, plus en-suites and the shared toilet and bathroom servicing the 3 back bedrooms.

### Bed Configuration

- **5 bedrooms** total, all on the upper level:
    - **3 king bedrooms** (Bedrooms 1, 2, 3) — permanent kings, mountain and lake views.
    - **2 twin bedrooms** (Bedrooms 4 & 5) — each has 2 single beds.
- **Note**: ensuite configuration is being reconfirmed with the owner — please confirm specific bathroom layout at booking if it matters for your party.
- **Porta cot, high chair, stroller/pram, and baby backpack carrier** all available on request.

### Bathrooms

- **4 toilets total:** 1 on the living level, 1 in each of the 2 en-suite master bathrooms, 1 standalone servicing the 3 back bedrooms.
- **2 baths** and **3 showers** (extendable hoses, not fixed rain shower type).
- **1 hair dryer** — check the drawers or laundry shelf.

### Hot Water

- The house has a **300 L electric hot water cylinder** which heats overnight.
- **With 10 guests, hot water can run out** if baths are taken or multiple showers run simultaneously. **Recommend showers over baths, and stagger usage.** If the water runs cool, it will reheat overnight.
- The **outdoor spa has its own separate heating system** and is always hot, even when the house hot water is empty.

### Other Amenities

- **Bicycles** available in the garage for guest use.
- **Spare foam mattress** available for occasional extra guest.
- **No coffee machine** — a coffee plunger is provided.
- **No food stocks provided** — please bring your own.
- **Iron and ironing board** available.

### Utility Infrastructure

Check the [Aurora Energy Queenstown outage page](https://www.aurora.energy/outages) for area-wide power information during any outage.

### Getting Around

- **Walking:** ~20 minutes downhill to Queenstown town centre; longer uphill coming back.
- **Taxi back from town:** ~$10.
- **Nearest supermarket:** FreshChoice, 3 minutes' drive.
- **Uber** available in Queenstown.
- **Closest ski field:** Coronet Peak; The Remarkables (~15 min further).

### Check-out

- Return keys to the lockbox.
- **Leave the garage remote on the dining table.**
- Start the dishwasher.
- Dispose of rubbish and recycling in the RED/YELLOW/BLUE bins (see House Rules).
- Switch off lights and the gas fireplace.
- Leave bed linens in place and towels in their original rooms.
