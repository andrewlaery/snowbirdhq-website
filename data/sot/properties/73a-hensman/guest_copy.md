# Welcome

### Dear Guest

Thank you for choosing to stay at Lakeview Lodge Upper. We trust your stay here will be more than enjoyable. For your convenience we have prepared a guidebook including house rules to assist you during your stay. Should you experience any problems or difficulties with regard to the home during your stay, please do not hesitate to contact us via the booking platform. We are here to help!

### The Home & Neighbourhood

Lakeview Lodge Upper (73A Hensman Road) is the upper level of a spacious lodge-style home with stunning lake and mountain views, in a quiet residential neighbourhood a short drive from central Queenstown. The lodge is on a sloped hillside site — enjoy the views from the balcony and the peaceful surroundings.

73B Hensman (the lower level) is a separate listing under the same owners — you may share the driveway and external areas. Please be considerate of other guests using those spaces.

Queenstown town centre is approximately 3 minutes by car or 15–20 minutes walking downhill. The Frankton supermarkets (FreshChoice, Pak'n'Save, New World) are within easy reach.
