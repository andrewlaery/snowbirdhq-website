### Heat Pump (Living Area)

Wall-mounted heat pump in the living area — heats the whole apartment.

- **Recommended setting**: 23 °C in heating mode.
- **Modes**: Heat / Cool / Dry / Auto / Fan — use the remote.
- Heats the open-plan living/kitchen area effectively. Bedroom warmth depends on door positions and supplementary heating.
- **Drying clothes**: turn on while clothes are on the drying rack to speed drying.
