# Welcome

Dear Guest,

Thank you for choosing to stay at 10/15 Gorge Road. We trust your stay here will be more than enjoyable. For your convenience we have prepared this Guidebook to assist you during your stay. Should you experience any problems or difficulties with regard to the apartment during your stay please do not hesitate to contact us via the booking platform. We are here to help!

### The Home & Neighbourhood

10/15 Gorge Road is a cosy one-bedroom apartment with a balcony, a short walk from central Queenstown. The home sits in a quiet street close to town with restaurants, shops, and Lake Wakatipu nearby.

### Bedrooms & Sleeping

- **Bedroom 1:** Queen bed
- **Maximum occupancy:** 2 guests
- A baby cot is available on request.
