### Electric Cooktop

A standard electric cooktop.

- **Mains switch** at the far right of the kitchen wall (shared with the oven). Must be ON for the hob to heat.
- Dials select burner + heat level.
- Allow 30–60 seconds for elements to come up to temperature; cookware should sit flat on the element.
