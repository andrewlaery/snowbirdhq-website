### Gas Cooktop (4 Burners)

The kitchen cooktop is a **4-burner gas hob** powered by external gas bottles.

- **Ignition**: turn knob and press to spark; hold for 5 seconds after flame catches.
- **Gas bottles** sit outside; on longer stays they can run low — please let us know if you're running out so we can swap a bottle.
- Use the **fan extractor** above the hob when cooking.
- Pots, pans, cutlery and dishes provided for **10 guests**.
