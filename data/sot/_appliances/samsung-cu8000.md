### Samsung TV (CU8000)

- Press the **Power** button on the Samsung remote to turn on.
- Press **Home** to access the menu — terrestrial TV (Freeview), YouTube, Netflix, Sky Sport, etc.
- Use channel buttons (∧/∨) to change channels.
- Use volume buttons (+/-) to adjust sound.
