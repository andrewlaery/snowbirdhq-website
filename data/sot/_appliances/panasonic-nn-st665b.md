### Panasonic Microwave (NN-ST665B)

[User manual](https://www.manualslib.com/manual/1116718/Panasonic-Nn-St665b.html)

Panasonic microwave with Inverter Technology — cook, reheat, defrost.

#### Starting

1. Open the door and place food in a microwave-safe dish on the glass tray.
2. Close the door.
3. Set power level via Micro Power button (P10 = highest).
4. Set cooking time using the time buttons.
5. Press Start.

#### Common functions

- **Defrost:** Turbo Defrost button + select weight.
- **Reheat:** Sensor Reheat — automatic.
- **Cook:** Sensor Cook for various foods.

#### Stop / pause

- Stop/Reset once — pause.
- Stop/Reset twice — cancel.
- Open door — pauses automatically.

#### Safety

- Microwave-safe dishes only.
- Do not operate empty.
- No metal containers or aluminium foil.
- Wipe down regularly with damp cloth and mild detergent.
