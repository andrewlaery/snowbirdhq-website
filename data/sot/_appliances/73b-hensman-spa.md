### Outdoor Spa / Hot Tub

Outdoor spa with mountain views.

- **Chlorinated fresh water** with its own separate heating system — **always hot, even when the house hot water is empty**.
- Lift the cover off and **roll it back onto the rear cradle** — never sit on the cover.
- **Sound carries to neighbours**, especially at night — please use considerately and keep voices low after 10pm.
- Replace the cover firmly when finished to retain heat.
- Towels: hang on the deck rail to dry, then return to bathroom.
