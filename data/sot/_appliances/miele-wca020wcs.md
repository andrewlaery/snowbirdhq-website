### Miele Washing Machine (WCA 020 WCS)

[User manual (PDF)](https://media.miele.com/downloads/ca/b8/06_C7F13838C02F1EEF988BF928C3BFCAB8.pdf)

Front-loading Miele washing machine. Located in the laundry as the bottom white appliance.

#### Starting

1. Open the door and load laundry loosely in the drum.
2. Add detergent to the dispenser drawer (pre-wash / main wash / fabric softener compartments).
3. Close the door securely.
4. Turn the program selector (Cottons, Minimum Iron, Woolens, etc.).
5. Adjust temperature and spin speed as needed.
6. Press the Start/Add Laundry sensor (▶️).

#### During operation

- You can add laundry mid-cycle if the lock symbol is not lit — touch the Start/Add Laundry sensor.
- The display shows remaining program time.

#### End of cycle

1. Wait for the program to finish (door stays locked during the Anti-crease phase).
2. Turn the program selector to OFF.
3. Open the door and remove laundry promptly.
4. Check the door seal for small items.
5. Leave the detergent drawer slightly open to air-dry.

#### Tips

- Empty pockets before washing.
- Sort by colour and care label.
- Run a hot wash (90°C) periodically when the hygiene indicator appears.
- Maximum load: 7.0 kg dry laundry.
