# User Instructions

### Internet / WiFi

The internet WiFi can be accessed by:
* **Network:** TP-Link_7BEF
* **Password:** provided on the welcome card at check-in

The Wi-Fi modem is to the right of the TV, under the grey corner chair. Wi-Fi details are also in the black booklet on the kitchen table.

### Access & Security

* **Entry:** Park in dedicated space 6A (below the steps). Go up two flights of stairs, staying to the right. 6A is the first door at the top of the steps.
* **Front Door:** Lockbox beside the front door. Code provided in your booking information.

### Layout

* One-bedroom apartment on an upper level of the Alpine Village complex.
* Private balcony overlooking Lake Wakatipu.

### Bed Configuration

* **Default:** King bed.
* **Splittable:** Into two singles on request — please let the host know before arrival if you'd like the split configuration.
* **Travel cot and highchair** available on request — please ask the host in advance.

### Heating & Cooling

* **Heat pump** for both heating and cooling — guest-controlled via the remote.
* **Panel heater** in the bedroom cupboard — additional heating for the bedroom.
* **Heated towel rail** in the bathroom — please turn off at checkout.

### Hot Water

* Hot water is supplied via a shared body corporate system. For body corporate hot water enquiries, the building manager can be contacted via the black booklet's details.

### Kitchen

* **Hob:** Gas. Instructions in the house manual.
* **Oven:** Standard electric. The **mains switch** for the oven is on the right-hand wall of the kitchen — if the oven has no power at all, check that switch.
* **No dishwasher** — dishes are hand-washed. Please do not attempt to run any dishes through automatic cycles.
* Basic cooking supplies provided (oil, coffee, etc.).
* **Tap water is safe to drink.**

### Bathroom

* Towels, shampoo, body wash, and a hair dryer provided.
* Heated towel rail.
* Washing machine (top loader) and drying rack are located in the bathroom.

### Laundry

* **Washing machine:** Top loader, in the bathroom. Laundry powder provided.
* **No in-unit dryer.** Use the drying rack provided (in the bathroom) — best used with the heat pump on.
* **Shared dryer** available in a separate building across the apartment complex — details in the black booklet on the kitchen table.
* **Please do NOT hang laundry on the balcony** (body corporate rule).
* **Iron and ironing board** in the bathroom.

### Living Area

* **TV:** Freeview via a streaming stick on HDMI 1.
    * **Two remotes:** the larger remote controls power and source (press Source, then select HDMI 1). The smaller remote navigates Freeview and Netflix.
    * You can log into your own Netflix account.
* **Vacuum** by the glass sliding balcony doors.

### Balcony

* Panoramic views over Lake Wakatipu — the standout feature of the apartment.
* **Storage box on the balcony** (code: 643) contains spare toilet paper and supplies.
* Please be mindful of noise after 8pm (see House Rules).
* **No laundry on the balcony** — body corporate rule.

### Utility Infrastructure

* **Fuse box:** Behind the bedroom door.
* If the power trips, flip the switch up. If it trips repeatedly, please contact the host.
* Check the Aurora Energy Queenstown outage page for area-wide power information during any outage.

### Getting Around / Transportation

* **Bus:** Connectabus stop is directly across the road. To the town centre or Frankton is around 10 minutes.
* **Uber:** ~$15–20 to town centre.
* **Walking:** ~15 minutes to Cargo Brewery along the lakefront. **The lakeside walking path is currently closed** (expected to reopen ~12 months from early 2026). The roadside footpath into town is ~45–60 minutes.
* **Queenstown Airport:** ~10 minutes' drive.

### Luggage Storage

* We cannot offer luggage storage due to same-day turnovers.
* **iSite Queenstown** (Clocktower Building, 22 Shotover Street) offers paid luggage storage for guests with late flights: https://www.queenstownisite.co.nz/listing/queenstown-luggage-storage-isite/

### Check-out

* Return the key to the lockbox beside the front door.
* Hand-wash any dishes.
* Place rubbish in the bins in the apartment complex.
* Standard checkout is 10:00am (strict); late checkout to 11:00am may be possible if no same-day booking — please ask the host.
