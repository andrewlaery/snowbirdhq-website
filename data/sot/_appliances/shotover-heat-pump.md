### Heat Pump (Living Area)

A wall-mounted heat pump in the living area — heats and cools.

- **Remote**: on the wooden beam in the living area (easy to miss — look up).
- **Modes**: Heat / Cool / Dry / Auto / Fan. Use the remote to switch.
- **Setpoints**: aim for 20–22 °C in heating mode; 24–26 °C in cooling.
- **Reach**: heats the living/kitchen area effectively; the upper level (bedrooms) gets some warmth but the **oil heaters** in each bedroom are recommended for cold nights.
- **Drying clothes**: turn the heat pump on while clothes are on the drying rack — speeds drying significantly.
