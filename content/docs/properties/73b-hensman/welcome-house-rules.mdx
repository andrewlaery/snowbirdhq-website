# Welcome

### Dear Guest

Thank you for choosing to stay at Lakeview Lodge Lower. We trust your stay here will be more than enjoyable. For your convenience we have prepared a Guidebook including House Rules to assist you during your stay. Should you experience any problems or difficulties with regard to the home during your stay please do not hesitate to contact us via the booking platform. We are here to help!

### The Home & Neighbourhood

Lakeview Lodge Lower (73B Hensman Road) is the lower level of a large lodge-style home in a quiet residential neighbourhood with stunning lake and mountain views. The lodge features five bedrooms, an outdoor spa, an outdoor fire pit, and a double garage. 73A Hensman is the upper level of the same lodge — a separate listing under the same owners, with shared external areas.

Pete, the owner, lives directly behind the lodge and can assist in person if needed. A cat named Basil is a friendly neighbourhood regular who may wander in through an open door — just let him out and close the door behind him.

# House Rules

### A Quick Note

- We're delighted to share our lodge with you in this quiet residential neighbourhood
- To maintain our great relationship with our neighbours and comply with local regulations, we kindly ask that you follow these house guidelines
- Please remove your shoes inside to help keep our home clean and comfortable

### House Items

* Consumable items (tea, coffee, dishwasher capsules, soaps) are provided free of charge on a fair use basis.
* Unused items remain property of the home.
* **Welcome bottle of Pinot Noir** provided for your stay.

### Security & Responsibility

* Please help us make your stay an enjoyable and memorable one. Please look after your valuables and secure your accommodation. Despite protective measures we are unable to assume any responsibility and will not be liable for any loss or damage to your possessions in or around the property.
* The hosts/owners are not responsible for any accidents, injuries, or illness that occur while on the premises or its facilities, as well as loss of personal belongings or valuables of the guest.

### Check-In & Check-Out

* Check-In after: 3pm
* Check-Out before: 10am

**When checking out:**
* ensure the lodge (each room) appears as it was when you first arrived
* dispose of all rubbish and recycling in the appropriate bins (see below)
* start the dishwasher with all used dishes
* turn off all lights and any electrical items you have used. Do not switch off utility items such as Wi-Fi or modems
* turn off heated towel rails and the gas fireplace
* leave bed linens in place and towels in their original rooms
* ensure the lodge is secured (close and lock all windows and doors)
* return the front door key to the lockbox
* **leave the garage remote on the dining table** — please do not take it with you

### Access

* **Front Door:** Lockbox beside the front door. Code provided in your booking information.

### Guests

* **The maximum total number of guests is 10 — strictly enforced for insurance and council consent reasons. No exceptions.**
* The guest responsible for registering the booking must be 25 years old or older.
* All guests staying must be registered with the host beforehand and only registered guests may spend the night.
* Guests are responsible for the actions of any visitors during their stay.

### Bed Configuration (please confirm at booking)

* **2 master bedrooms** — both permanently set as king beds, each with en-suite bathroom.
* **3 additional bedrooms** — each can be configured as **1 king bed** (zip-together) OR **2 single beds**.

**Linen is ordered in advance based on your configuration — please confirm your preferences at booking or as soon as possible after.** The most popular setup is two families where both adult couples have a master bedroom.

### Visitors & events/parties

* Strictly registered guests only. No unregistered visitors are permitted.
* Parties are NOT allowed.
* No confetti, glitter, or excessive rubbish allowed otherwise an additional cleaning fee will be charged.

### Quiet Hours & Outdoor Spaces

This is a quiet residential neighbourhood. To be good neighbours:
- Please keep outdoor noise to a minimum after 10pm
- **The spa / outdoor fire pit area can carry sound to neighbours** — please enjoy them considerately in the evening
- No music or amplified sound outside between 8pm - 8am
- After 8pm, please continue activities indoors with windows and doors closed

Note: Under the District Plan, noise limits are:
- Daytime (8am - 8pm): 50dBA LAeq (15 min)
- Nighttime (8pm - 8am): 40dBA LAeq

### Parking

- **Double garage** with a remote-controlled door. The garage remote should be left on the dining table at departure.
- **Off-street parking** is available on the grass area in front of the boat on the private land opposite the house.
- **Street parking** is also available.
- Larger vehicles (SUVs, vans) are best parked on the grass verge rather than in the garage.

### Rubbish & Recycling (private collection system)

* **RED bin (big red lid):** General waste / rubbish.
* **YELLOW bin (yellow lid):** Cardboard and plastic bottles.
* **BLUE bin (blue lid):** Glass only.
* Bins are located in or near the garage area. Rubbish bags are in the garage (extras can be requested).
* **Collection:** Private system, not the standard council pickup. Usually Wednesday but varies.

### Littering

* NO littering. If any part of the lodge, outdoor areas, or grounds is found to be littered requiring additional cleaning, an additional cleaning charge may apply.

### Commercial photography

* Commercial photography is not allowed unless guests have written permission from the host beforehand.

### Pets

* Pets are NOT permitted.
* **Basil the cat** is a friendly neighbourhood visitor. If he lets himself in through an open door, please put him back outside and close the door.

### Smoking, vaping, & e-cigarettes

* Smoking or vaping is NOT permitted inside the lodge.
* There may be a $250+ additional fee added for smoking inside and/or improper disposal of cigarette butts.

### Security cameras & other equipment

* Tampering with any security, safety, or utility devices (e.g., smoke detectors, internet, connectivity, power) will result in the immediate termination of your booking without a refund.

### Child safety

* Please supervise children carefully — especially around the outdoor spa, fire pit, and stairs to the upper bedroom level.
* Guests are fully responsible for the safety and security of their children at all times.

### Damages/breakages & furniture

* Please respect our lodge as if it is your own.
* Only use appliances, equipment, and furniture for their intended uses.
* Removing any items from the property is not permitted and you will be charged accordingly.
* Should any damage occur, please notify the owner immediately.

### Booking verification

* Always verify your booking is on Airbnb, Booking.com, VRBO, or another reputable platform. If you arrived here via a listing that seems off, please contact us — there have been scam listings copying this property's address.

### Acceptance of House Rules

* By accepting this reservation, it is agreed that the House Rules are understood and accepted, and all guests are assuming the risk of any harm arising from their use of the premises or others whom they invite to use the premises.
