### TV (Two-Remote Setup)

A Smart TV with a separate streaming device — uses **two remotes**.

- **Larger remote** = TV: power on/off, source selection. Set to **HDMI 1** to use the streaming device.
- **Smaller remote** = streaming device: navigation. Press **Home** for app selection.
- **Apps**: Freeview (NZ free-to-air channels), Netflix (sign in with your own account; please sign out before leaving).
- **Spare lightbulbs** are in the TV cabinet (housekeeping note, separate from TV operation).
