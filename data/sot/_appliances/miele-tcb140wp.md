### Miele Heat-Pump Dryer (TCB140WP)

[User manual (PDF)](https://media.miele.com/downloads/13/47/02_F6154D09FD151EDDA9D7285700611347.pdf)

Energy-efficient heat-pump tumble dryer. Located in the laundry as the top white appliance.

#### Starting

1. Make sure laundry has been spun in the washer.
2. Open the door, load loosely (don't overload).
3. Close the door gently.
4. Turn the programme selector to choose a programme.
5. Choose a drying level if applicable (Extra Dry / Normal / Hand Iron / Machine Iron).
6. Press the Start/Add laundry sensor.

#### During operation

- The display shows estimated remaining time.
- Add forgotten items: press Start/Add laundry, open door when "Add" appears.
- Don't open the door before the programme ends.

#### After drying

1. Open the door when the programme has finished.
2. Remove all laundry.
3. Turn the programme selector to OFF.
4. Clean the fluff filters (in the door opening).
5. Empty the condensed water container.

#### Care & safety

- Clean the fluff filters after every cycle.
- Empty the condensed water container after every use.
- Don't overload the drum.
- Empty pockets (lighters, coins, etc.).
- Adequate ventilation in the laundry; avoid freezing temperatures.
