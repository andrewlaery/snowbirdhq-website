### Fisher & Paykel Dish-Drawer Dishwasher

A Fisher & Paykel single dish-drawer (drawer-style dishwasher that pulls out from the cabinetry).

- **Loading**: pull the drawer out by the handle. Cutlery basket sits in the middle; plates and cups in the racks.
- **Tablets**: drop one in the dispenser at the front of the drawer interior.
- **Programs**: select on the front control panel — Eco / Normal / Fast / Heavy.
- **Start**: close the drawer firmly and press the start button.
- **F1 error**: if displayed, the drawer detected a leak — power off at the wall, contact the host. Don't keep starting the cycle.
- **Run before checkout** — please start before you leave.
