### Rinnai Gas Fireplace (Lounge)
Gas fireplace in the upstairs lounge.

- **Switch panel** is in the **top-left corner of the heater**.
- **Top button** = on/off.
- **Up/down arrows** below the on/off button control **temperature** and **fan**.

#### Important
- The gas fireplace **cannot be switched off via a normal wall switch** — it's designed/mounted with the **isolator behind the fire unit**. Use the on/off button on the heater's switch panel.
- Please turn the gas fireplace **off when leaving the lounge** for an extended period.
