# Welcome

### Dear Guest

Thank you for choosing to stay at De La Mare Views. We trust your stay here will be more than enjoyable. For your convenience we have prepared a Guidebook including House Rules to assist you during your stay. Should you experience any problems or difficulties with regard to the home during your stay please do not hesitate to contact us via the booking platform. We are here to help!

### The Home & Neighbourhood

De La Mare Views is a spacious standalone two-level home in Frankton's quiet, elevated De La Mare neighbourhood. The grand outdoor deck captures panoramic mountain and lake views across Lake Wakatipu. The property sits between Frankton Road and the Five Mile shopping precinct, roughly seven minutes' drive from Queenstown town centre and a short walk to Frankton's cafes, restaurants and services. This is a peaceful residential retreat close to the action but away from it.

# House Rules

### A Quick Note

- We're delighted to share our home with you in this peaceful residential neighbourhood
- To maintain our great relationship with our neighbours and comply with local regulations, we kindly ask that you follow these house guidelines
- Please remove your shoes inside to help keep our home clean and comfortable — no shoes or ski gear indoors, store in the garage

### House Items

* Consumable items in the house are provided free of charge on a fair use basis (this includes basic cooking supplies, tea, coffee, laundry powder, dishwasher capsules/powder, soaps & body wash, etc).
* Unused items remain property of the home.

### Security & Responsibility

* Please help us make your stay an enjoyable and memorable one. Please look after your valuables and secure your accommodation. Despite protective measures we are unable to assume any responsibility and will not be liable for any loss or damage to your possessions in or around the property.
* The hosts/owners are not responsible for any accidents, injuries, or illness that occur while on the premises or its facilities, as well as loss of personal belongings or valuables of the guest. This includes adults and children.
* **Note:** During your stay, the house alarm is not in operation.

### Check-In & Check-Out

* Check-In after: 3pm
* Check-Out before: 10am

**Note:** there are multiple parties who rely on these times for cleaning and maintenance. If you have requirements outside of these times, contact the host and we will do our best to accommodate you.

**When checking out:**
* ensure the house (each room) appears as it was when you first arrived
* place all rubbish & recycling (including bottles, cardboard and plastics) in the bins provided
* load all dirty plates and glasses into the dishwasher. Do not put any dirty plates, glasses, or cutlery away as if clean
* turn off all lights and any electrical items you have used. Do not switch off utility items such as Wi-Fi or modems
* turn off heated towel rails and panel heaters
* ensure the house is secured (close and lock all windows and doors)
* return the front door key and garage remote to the lockbox

### Access

* **Front Door:** Lockbox on the wooden fence to the left-hand side as you walk up the steps. Lockbox code provided in your booking information. The lockbox contains the front door key and a garage remote fob.
* **House Manual:** A black booklet on the kitchen table has all Wi-Fi info, appliance instructions and additional property details.

### Guests

* The maximum total number of guests is 6.
* The guest responsible for registering the booking must be 25 years old or older.
* All guests staying must be registered with the host beforehand and only registered guests may spend the night.
* Guests are responsible for the actions of any visitors during their stay.
* Any unregistered guest can be asked to leave at any time without cause.

### Luggage & Equipment

* The garage can be used for luggage storage before check-in or after check-out — please arrange with host in advance.
* Suitcases and larger items can go in the garage.
* No sports equipment (golf, snowboards / skis / boots, bicycles) allowed inside the house — store in the garage. The garage has space for bikes and ski gear.
* An outdoor clothes horse is located to the left of the back door, plus an indoor drying rack is provided. The house does not have a dryer.

### Visitors & events/parties

* Strictly registered guests only. No unregistered visitors are permitted.
* Parties are NOT allowed.
* No confetti, glitter, or excessive rubbish allowed otherwise an additional cleaning fee will be charged.
* Failure to comply may result in the termination of the contract and additional charges.

### Quiet Hours & Outdoor Spaces

To ensure everyone in our peaceful neighbourhood gets a good night's rest:
- Our outdoor deck is available for your enjoyment between 7am - 10pm
- We kindly ask that you keep noise levels considerate of our neighbours, especially between 10pm - 7am
- For outdoor areas, please refrain from playing music or amplified sound between 8pm - 8am
- After 8pm, we invite you to continue your activities indoors with windows and doors closed

If you experience any noise concerns from nearby properties during quiet hours, you can contact QLDC Noise Control.

These guidelines help maintain the tranquil atmosphere of our residential area and comply with local council requirements. Thank you for helping us be good neighbours.

Note: Under the District Plan, noise limits are:
- Daytime (8am - 8pm): 50dBA LAeq (15 min)
- Nighttime (8pm - 8am): 40dBA LAeq

### Parking

We have an on-site single garage with two separate remote-controlled doors. A few important notes:

- **Garage size:** Fits small and medium cars only. 7-seaters, SUVs, and vehicles with low body kits will NOT fit and may scrape. Most guests park in front of the garage doors for larger vehicles.
- **Garage remotes:** Kept inside the house alongside the black house manual on the kitchen table. Return to the lockbox at checkout.
- **Open from inside:** Wall switches at the bottom of the steps leading from the living area to the garage.
- **Street parking:** Available along the road parallel to the driveway.
- **Winter note:** The driveway is steep and can become icy and slippery in winter. Salt for de-icing is in a large white bag inside the garage — please use it as needed.
- **Caution:** The garage door swings outward — ensure you and any vehicle are **at least 2 metres clear** of the door before opening.
- Bus or coach drop-offs/pickups are not permitted at the property.

### Rubbish & Recycling

* **RED bin:** General waste
* **YELLOW bin:** Mixed recycling (bottles, cardboard, plastics)
* Bins are located near/in front of the garage by the driveway.
* **Bin collection day:** Tuesday morning. Please put bins out on the street curb the night before.
* Rubbish bags are under the kitchen sink and in the boiler cupboard (first left as you come in the front door).
* Bins need to be collected off the street by Tuesday evening.

### Littering

* NO littering. This includes leaving food outside for long periods.
* If any part of the house, yard, or appliances is found to be littered requiring additional cleaning, an additional cleaning charge may apply.

### Commercial photography

* Commercial photography is not allowed unless guests have written permission from the host beforehand. Please contact the host for pricing.

### Pets

* Pets are NOT permitted. If you bring a pet, the host is not liable for any incident involving your pet and any other person or animal, and you will be asked to leave without a refund.

### Smoking, vaping, & e-cigarettes

* Smoking or vaping is NOT permitted inside or directly outside the house.
* There may be a $250+ additional fee added for smoking inside the home and/or not properly disposing of cigarette butts.

### Security cameras & other equipment

* Tampering with any security, safety, or utility devices (e.g., smoke detectors, noise monitors, internet, connectivity, power) will result in the immediate termination of your booking without a refund.

### Child safety

* Children must be attended to — there are multiple hazards throughout the house, including internal stairs between levels.
* The house has two levels connected by carpeted stairs with a handrail. Stairs may be a consideration for elderly guests or families with very young children.
* Guests are fully responsible for the safety and security of their children at all times.

### Damages/breakages & furniture

* Please respect our home as if it is your own.
* Only use appliances, equipment, and furniture for their intended uses.
* Please keep the property and all furnishings in good order.
* Removing any items from the property, including linens and living and kitchen furnishings, is not permitted and you will be charged accordingly if anything is removed.
* Should any damage occur, please notify the owner immediately.
* Any damage to any items at the property, including sofas, chairs, tables, TVs, beds, sheets, towels, and pillows, are the responsibility of the renter.
* Every item will be looked at before arrival and after departure.

### Acceptance of House Rules

* By accepting this reservation, it is agreed that the House Rules are understood and accepted, and all guests are assuming the risk of any harm arising from their use of the premises or others whom they invite to use the premises.
