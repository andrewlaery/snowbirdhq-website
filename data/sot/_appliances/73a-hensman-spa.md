### Spa Pool (Outdoor)

Outdoor spa pool — covered when not in use.

- **Spa key**: a **plastic key** is kept **with the garage remote** — use it to unlock the cover.
- **Shower before use** — please rinse off sunscreen, body lotion, and deodorant before entering.
- **Chlorine only** — **NO bubbles, foaming agents, bath salts, or other substances** in the spa.
- **NO food, beverages, or glass** in or near the spa area.
- Replace the cover firmly when finished to retain heat.
- Sound carries to neighbours, especially after dark — please be considerate.
