### Food Disposal (Under-Sink Waste Grinder)

In-sink food waste disposer — switch is **inside the cabinet under the sink**.

#### Using the Disposal
1. **Turn on cold water** — run a steady stream **before and during** use.
2. Activate the **switch inside the cabinet** under the sink.
3. Feed **soft food waste only** — fruit/vegetable scraps, small amounts of rice or pasta.
4. Keep water running for **10–15 seconds** after the grinding noise stops to flush.
5. Turn off the switch, then the water.

⚠️ **Never** put bones, fruit pits, fibrous vegetables (celery, corn husks), oil/grease, eggshells, coffee grounds, or non-food items into the disposal.
