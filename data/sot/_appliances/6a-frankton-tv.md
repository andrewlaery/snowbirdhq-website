### TV (Two-Remote: Freeview Stick + Netflix)

Living-room TV with a **Freeview streaming stick on HDMI 1** — uses **two remotes**.

- **Larger remote** = TV: power on/off, source. Press **Source** then select **HDMI 1** to use the streaming stick.
- **Smaller remote** = streaming stick: navigation, app launching.
- **Apps**: Freeview (NZ free-to-air channels), Netflix.
- **Netflix**: you can sign in with your own account — please **sign out before checking out**.
