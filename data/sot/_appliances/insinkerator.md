### Insinkerator (Food Waste Disposal)

Press the silver button on the right-hand side of the tap to use the
food waste disposal system. Ensure water is running for smooth
operation.

This is an **air button** — if water from the sink gets into the
button it can limit operation. The button self-corrects after a few
hours.
