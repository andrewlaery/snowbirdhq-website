### Washing Machine + Dryer (Garage)

A washer and dryer in the garage (accessible from inside the house via the internal door).

- **Laundry powder** is provided.
- **Washer**: select cycle on the front dial, add powder to the dispenser, press start.
- **Dryer**: standard vented or condenser dryer — clean the lint filter after each load.
- The garage location keeps laundry noise away from the living areas.
