### Miele Dishwasher (G 7130 SC AutoDos)

[User manual (PDF)](https://www.miele.com.au/e/freestanding-dishwasher-g-7130-sc-autodos-clst-stainless-steel-cleansteel-12116900-p)

A built-under dishwasher with **AutoDos**: drop in a Miele PowerDisk and the machine auto-doses detergent for ~20 cycles — no need to refill mid-stay.

- **Loading**: cutlery tray on top, glasses + bowls in the upper basket, plates + pots in the lower basket.
- **AutoDos**: detergent dispenses automatically from the PowerDisk reservoir. Don't add tablets unless the AutoDos lamp shows empty.
- **Programs**: **QuickPowerWash** is a 58-min full clean for normally soiled loads — start here. **Auto** sensor-controlled for mixed loads. **Eco** lowest energy/water (~3 h).
- **Door pop-out**: at the end of the cycle the door pops slightly open to vent moisture — leave a little space above the appliance.
- **Don't pre-rinse heavily** — the sensor reads soil load from the wash water.
