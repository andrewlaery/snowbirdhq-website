### Samsung Smart TV (UA43DU8000SXNZ) — Bedroom

[Samsung support](https://www.samsung.com/nz/support/model/UA43DU8000SXNZ/#downloads)

Samsung Smart TV with live channels, streaming apps, and the Samsung Smart Remote (with voice control).

#### Getting started

1. Power button on the Smart Remote turns it on.
2. Use the directional buttons to navigate menus.
3. Press the centre Select button to choose options.

#### Basic controls

- **Volume:** +/− rocker on the remote.
- **Channels:** Channel up/down rocker.
- **Home:** HOME (⌂) button — apps and features.
- **Settings:** Settings button (gear icon) for TV settings.
- **Voice:** hold the microphone button, speak, then release.

#### Apps & content

- Netflix, YouTube, and other apps from the Home screen.
- Use Search to find specific content.
- Supports screen mirroring from compatible smartphones.

#### Remote

- Solar cell panel — leave it exposed to light to extend battery life.
- Also charges via USB-C at the bottom.

#### Power off

- Press the power button to turn off.
- Press and hold for complete shutdown.
