### Electrolux Washing Machine (EDC2075GDW)
Front-loading washing machine.

#### Operating
1. **Load items** into the washing machine. Ensure the door is **closed and latched securely**.
2. Place **washing liquid** into the dispenser — **top-left corner**.
3. Close the dispenser.
4. Press the **power button**.
5. Use the **large stainless-steel dial** (right of the power button) to select wash mode.
6. Press the **start / play button** (right of the dial).
7. **Don't try to open the door** until the cycle is complete.

Note: this washer accepts liquid detergent (the upstairs unit uses powder).
