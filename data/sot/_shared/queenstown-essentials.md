## Essentials & Other

### Insects

- Central Queenstown does not have sandflies, however we do have small, non-harmful flying bugs which are attracted to light. If you'd like windows open in the evenings for ventilation, turn out your lights or close blinds — this minimises insects in your room.
- If you're travelling to the West Coast, the Glenorchy/Dart River area, or Milford / Doubtful Sound, take insect repellent for sandflies.

### Power

- All power sockets in New Zealand are 230/240 volts (50 Hz). If you're plugging in devices, ensure they are rated accordingly.

### ATMs & Currency Exchange
