### Smart TV (Freeview)

A Smart TV with built-in Freeview channels.

- **Power on**: use the TV remote (on the coffee table or beside the TV).
- **Channels**: press **Source** / **Input** to switch between Freeview, HDMI inputs, or apps.
- **Apps**: Netflix / YouTube / Disney+ available on the TV's home screen — sign in with your own account; please sign out before leaving so the next guest doesn't see your profile.
- **Freeview**: free over-the-air NZ channels; press **TV** or **Live** to switch from app mode.
