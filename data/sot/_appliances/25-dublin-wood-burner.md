### Studio Wood Burner

- Lift the handle up to open and close the fire door.
- Light the fire with the fire-lighters provided in the wood box.
- Leave the door slightly open while the fire establishes and the
  glass warms (avoids condensation build-up).
- Close the door once the fire is established and lock it back into
  place.
- Do not leave the door open — risk of over-firing damages the
  appliance.
- Use the supplied baskets to collect firewood / kindling stored
  in the garage.
- Open the door only by touching the wooden knob on the front.
- Do not empty ash into the rubbish bins or any area exposed to
  high winds.
- If excess ash builds up, contact the hosts.
