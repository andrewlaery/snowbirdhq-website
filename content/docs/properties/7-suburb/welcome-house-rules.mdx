# House Rules

### A Quick Note

- We're delighted to share our home with you in this peaceful residential neighborhood
- To maintain our great relationship with our neighbors and comply with local regulations, we kindly ask that you follow these house guidelines
- Please remove your shoes inside to help keep our home clean and comfortable

### House Items

- Consumable items in the house are provided free of charge on a fair use basis (this includes basic cooking supplies, tea, coffee, laundry powder, dishwasher capsules/powder, soaps & body wash, etc).
- Unused items remain property of the home.

### Security & Responsibility

- Please look after your valuables and secure your accommodation. Despite protective measures we are unable to assume any responsibility and will not be liable for any loss or damage to your possessions in or around the property.
- The hosts/owners are not responsible for any accidents, injuries, or illness that occur while on the premises or its facilities, as well as loss of personal belongings or valuables of the guest. This includes adults and children.
- **Note:** During your stay, the house alarm is not in operation.

### Check-In & Check-Out

Check-in/out times are shown above in the Quick Reference. Multiple parties rely on these times for cleaning and maintenance — if you have requirements outside of these, contact the host and we'll do our best to accommodate.

**When checking out:**

- Ensure each room appears as it did when you first arrived
- Place all rubbish & recycling in the bins/bags provided
- Load all dirty plates and glasses into the dishwasher (don't put dirty items away as if clean)
- Turn the lights off and any electrical items used. Do not switch off WiFi/modems.
- Turn off heated towel rails and the gas fire
- Ensure the house is secured (all windows and doors)

### Access

- **Front Door:** Unique access code provided to you as part of your booking information.
- **Garage Door:** Open/close using the bottom button on the switch panel next to the front door.

### Guests

- Maximum 8 guests, with no more than 6 adults (see Quick Reference for capacity).
- The lead guest must be 25 years old or older.
- All overnight guests must be registered with the host beforehand.
- Lead guest is responsible for the actions of any visitors during their stay.
- Any unregistered guest can be asked to leave at any time.

### Luggage & Equipment

- Small luggage can be stowed at the foot of the beds; suitcases and larger items can go in the garage.
- No sports equipment allowed in the house (golf, snowboards/skis/boots, bicycles, etc.) — please store in the garage.
- The garage includes a clothes rack for drying ski gear and power points for charging e-bikes.
- An outdoor sunny clothes rack is located at the rear of the house for additional drying needs.

### Visitors & Events/Parties

- Strictly registered guests only. No unregistered visitors are permitted.
- Parties are NOT allowed.
- No confetti, glitter, or excessive rubbish allowed otherwise an additional cleaning fee will be charged.
- Failure to comply may result in termination of the contract and additional charges.

### Quiet Hours & Outdoor Spaces

To ensure everyone in our peaceful neighborhood gets a good night's rest:

- Our lovely outdoor spaces are available for your enjoyment between 7am – 10pm.
- We kindly ask that you keep noise levels considerate of our neighbors, especially between 10pm – 7am.
- For outdoor areas, please refrain from playing music or amplified sound between 8pm – 8am.
- After 8pm, we invite you to continue your activities indoors with windows and doors closed.

If you experience noise concerns from nearby properties during quiet hours, contact QLDC Noise Control.

Under the District Plan, noise limits are:

- Daytime (8am – 8pm): 50dBA LAeq (15 min)
- Nighttime (8pm – 8am): 40dBA LAeq

### Parking

We have convenient on-site parking available for up to three vehicles in our driveway:

- Please park all vehicles within our property's driveway (avoid street parking)
- Cars can be parked one behind the other (tandem style)
- The driveway can accommodate up to 3 vehicles
- Bus/coach drop-offs or pickups are not permitted at the property

While we don't have a designated mobility parking space, our driveway is accessible for loading and unloading. However, please be aware the house is accessed via stairs, which may not be suitable for guests with mobility requirements.

### Rubbish & Recycling

- Rubbish (red bins) are collected every Wednesday.
- Glass recycling (blue bins) and mixed recycling (yellow bins) are collected every Wednesday. Blue and yellow bins alternate each week.
- Please double check the QLDC website for rubbish collection or check which bins the neighbours have out that week.
- Bins need to be placed at the street curb beside the driveway by 7am or the night before.
- Bins need to be collected off the street by Wednesday evening.

### Littering

- NO littering. This includes leaving food outside.
- If any part of the house/yard/appliances is found to be littered requiring additional cleaning, there may be an additional cleaning charge.

### Commercial Photography

- Commercial photography is not allowed unless guests have written permission from the host beforehand. Please contact the host for pricing.

### Pets

- Pets are NOT permitted. If you bring a pet, the host is not liable for any incident with your pet and you will be asked to leave without a refund.

### Smoking, Vaping & E-Cigarettes

- Smoking or vaping is NOT permitted inside or directly outside the house.
- There may be a $250+ additional fee for smoking inside the home and/or not properly disposing of cigarette butts.

### Security Cameras & Other Equipment

- An external security camera is installed at the front door.
- A noise monitor is located in the main living area.
- Tampering with any security, safety, or utility devices (smoke detectors, carbon monoxide detectors, noise monitors, internet, connectivity, power, etc.) will result in immediate termination of your booking without a refund.

### Child Safety

- Children must be attended to — there are multiple hazards throughout the house.
- Guests are fully responsible for the safety and security of their children at all times.

### Damages/Breakages & Furniture

- Please respect our home as if it is your own.
- Only use appliances, equipment, and furniture for their intended uses.
- Removing any items from the property (including linens and furnishings) is not permitted and you will be charged accordingly.
- Should any damage occur, please notify the owner immediately.
- Damage to any items at the property is the responsibility of the renter.
- Every item is inspected before arrival and after departure.

### Acceptance of House Rules

By accepting this reservation, it is agreed that the House Rules are understood and accepted, and all guests are assuming the risk of any harm arising from their use of the premises or others whom they invite to use the premises.
