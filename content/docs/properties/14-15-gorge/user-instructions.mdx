# User Instructions

### Internet / WiFi

The internet WiFi can be accessed by:
* **Password:** provided on the welcome card at check-in

If you have connectivity issues, switch the modem off and on again (unplug for 20 seconds, plug back in).

### Access & Security

* **Entry:** The apartment is part of the Whistler Apartments complex at 15–17 Gorge Road. Walk past reception and follow the corridor to Unit 14 — a full photo walkthrough is sent with your check-in instructions 24 hours before arrival.
* **Front Door:** Self check-in via lockbox. Code provided in your booking information.
* **Note:** Unit 14 is privately managed — please do not use Whistler Apartments reception for guest services; contact us directly.

### Bed Configuration

* Queen bed.
* **Travel cot** available on request. Please notify host before arrival.
* **Highchair** available on request.

### Heating

* **Heat pump** in the living area — heats the whole apartment.
* **Underfloor heating** in the bathroom — on/off switch and temperature dial on the bathroom wall.

### Hot Water

* Hot water is managed by the building manager and should be instant.
* If there are issues with hot water supply, call the building manager on **03 409 0394** or use the phone by reception.

### Kitchen

* **Electric hob** and **electric oven.** The oven timer must be turned on for the oven to heat properly — common source of confusion.
* **Dishwasher** — eco mode recommended; tablets under the sink.
* **Kettle and coffee plunger** (no coffee machine).
* Tea, sugar, and basic cooking supplies provided.
* Rubbish bags under the kitchen sink.
* **Tap water is safe to drink.**

### Bathroom

* Shower.
* **Heated towel rail** — please turn off at checkout.
* **Underfloor heating.**
* **Washing machine** in the bathroom. Washing powder provided.

### Laundry

* Washing machine provided. Washing powder supplied.
* **Shared dryer** in the complex's communal drying/storage room — costs $4 per load (2 × $2 coins). Access code on the back of the apartment keychain.
* **No laundry hung on the balcony** (body corporate rule).

### Ski, Bike & Luggage Storage

* **Ski storage:** Ski/dryer room below the apartments. Use the ski lock provided.
* **Bike rack:** Outside the drying room in the car park. Use a bike lock.
* **Luggage storage** (pre-check-in / post-check-out): storage room in the garage area below reception. Walk down the steep driveway from reception, turn right into the garage. **Door code: C8743.** Alternative: Bounce.com for luggage storage in town.

### Balcony

* Available for use 7am–10pm.
* **No laundry on the balcony** (body corporate rule).
* **Nothing stored on the balcony** (body corporate rule).

### Utility Infrastructure

* Check the Aurora Energy Queenstown outage page for area-wide power information during any outage.

### Getting Around / Transportation

* **Bus:** By the Four Square in town, $5 cash or Bee Card (cheaper). Use the Transit app or orc.govt.nz for timetables.
* **Airport bus:** Number 1 bus from opposite Super Liquor on Stanley Street — $4 cash only, goes directly to the airport.
* **Uber** available. **QT Taxis:** 03 450 3000.
* **Queenstown Airport:** ~10–15 minutes by car.

### Nearby Shops

* **Fresh Choice** — ~5 min walk (across the road). Open until 10pm.
* **Raeward Fresh** — ~5 min walk (deli and butchers).
* **Pak'n'Save** and **Countdown** — Frankton, ~20 min drive.
* **Four Square** — in town centre.

### Check-out

* Return the key to the lockbox.
* Load the dishwasher.
* Dispose of rubbish in the bins.
* Switch off lights and heated towel rail.
* Leave bed linens in place and towels in their original rooms.
