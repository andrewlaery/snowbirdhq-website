## Description

A delightful and memorable getaway awaits you at Snowbird! This award winning, architecturally-designed home offers plenty of thoughtful and livable spaces for guests to be cosy and relaxed. Prepare to be blown away by breathtaking views of Lake Wakatipu and the snow-capped peaks!

*Full kitchen w/ Miele/AEG/Bosch appliances
*Wood-burning fireplace
*Dedicated workspace
*Laundry onsite
*Private garden w/ spa pool
*Outdoor dining and BBQ
*Very walkable - 5 mins to town centre
*Free parking

This stylish, modern 3 bedroom/3.5 bathroom house will have you feeling relaxed and at home right away. Featuring an open floor plan, it offers lots of space while still maintaining a sense of comfort and functionality. From the floor to ceiling windows, to a fully-equipped stainless kitchen, to the snug movie room, every element of the residence has been carefully selected to ensure guests enjoy a delightful and tranquil stay.

In 2012, Snowbird won the Southern Architecture Award "The clever massing of the building results in an optimal orientation towards the street, the arc of the sun and the sumptuous views on offer. A well-articulated stair element deftly stitches together the various levels of the house, and a modest material palette, relieved with splashes of vibrant colour, communicates the primary purpose of the house as a holiday residence."

Guests will find the following in the home:
*Main bedroom - Located on the upper level it offers lots of privacy. It features 1 super king bed, a walk-in closet, and an ensuite bathroom.

*Office space - Also on the upper level; get some work done in private while taking in picturesque views of the landscape.

*2nd bedroom - Located on the main level, it features 1 super king bed and a single bunk bed, along with its own ensuite bathroom.

*3rd bedroom - Also located on the main level, this bedroom features 1 super king bed and 1 king single bed, 1 single trundle bed, plus its own ensuite bathroom.

*Living areas - The home offers multiple living spaces, including a movie/media room to curl up and watch movies on the TV, an airy living space off the dining room that opens to the backyard garden, and a lounge/library featuring a fireplace and lots of cosy seating.

*Kitchen - The beautiful, modern kitchen features stainless steel countertops, Miele & Bosch appliances, a pantry with cooking essentials, and much more. To use the oven, turn on the isolation switch inside the pantry labelled "Oven Leave On". Everything to prepare an amazing meal is conveniently at your fingertips, and the best part is you get to do it while admiring a mesmerising view from the kitchen!

*Dining area - The kitchen offers informal seating and the large dining table seats 8 people.

*Laundry / Drying Room - There is a laundry/drying room with AEG washer, a heatpump dryer, an air-conditioning unit and space to hang your ski gear.

*Half bathroom on the main level.

*Outdoor space - Enjoy the fresh air and natural ambiance outside in the garden/on the verandah. There’s a BBQ and outdoor seating, perfect for al fresco dining. Relax while soaking in the modern spa pool while gazing up at the stars!

Check-in is easy with our smart locking system. Guests will have access to the entire property, including partial access to the garage.

Check-Out: Before 10am

Check-Out Requirements:
- Leave the property in the same condition as when you arrived
- Place all rubbish and recycling in appropriate bins/bags provided
- Load dirty dishes into dishwasher (do not put dirty items away as if clean)
- Turn off lights and electrical items used (do not switch off utilities like WiFi/modems)
