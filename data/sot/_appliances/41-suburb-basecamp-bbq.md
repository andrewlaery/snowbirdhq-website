### Gas BBQ (Deck)

A gas BBQ on the private deck.

- **Before lighting**: check the gas bottle is connected and the valve is open.
- **Lighting**: open the lid first (never light with the lid closed — gas builds up). Set burner to high, press the igniter button.
- **Cooking**: pre-heat with the lid closed for 5–10 minutes; clean the grill with the wire brush before placing food.
- **After use** — keep it clean (wire-brush the grill, scrape the bottom plate), monitor while in use, and ensure the BBQ is **fully extinguished** (turn the burner knobs to OFF, then close the bottle valve).
- **Fire risk**: don't leave the BBQ unattended while lit.
