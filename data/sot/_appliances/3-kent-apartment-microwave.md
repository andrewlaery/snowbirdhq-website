### Panasonic Microwave

Bench-top microwave in the downstairs kitchen.

- **Isolation switch** is **behind the microwave**.
- **Manual** is in the **top drawer of the TV cabinet**.
- Open the door, place food in a microwave-safe dish, close the door.
- Set time + power level, press **Start**.
