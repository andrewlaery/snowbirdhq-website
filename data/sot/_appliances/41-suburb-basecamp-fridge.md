### Beko Side-by-Side Fridge / Freezer

An American-style side-by-side fridge / freezer with external water and ice dispenser.

- **Layout**: freezer on the left, fridge on the right (each side has its own door).
- **Display panel** on the freezer door:
  - Left side controls **freezer** temperature.
  - Right side controls **fridge** temperature.
  - **ION ioniser** indicator — air-purification function for the fridge compartment.
  - **Eco mode** indicator — energy-saving setpoints.
- **Water + ice dispenser** on the freezer door — push glass against the lever.
- **Default setpoints** are right for normal use.
