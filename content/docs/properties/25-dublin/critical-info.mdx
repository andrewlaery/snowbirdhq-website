### Health & Safety

* This home is equipped with certified smoke detectors, CO2 detectors, a first aid kit and fire extinguishers for use in an emergency.
* **Instructions:**
    * First Aid Kit: Located in the kitchen pantry LHS upper shelf.
    * Fire Extinguisher: Located underneath kitchen sink.
    * Gas Shutoff Valve: To shut off gas main supply area is located RHS of the entrance stairwell, garage side.
    * Emergency Exit: In the event of an emergency, please exit the home through the front door. The emergency meeting point is outside the garage in the driveway.

### Emergency Procedures - Fire

* Snowbird is fitted with smoke detectors throughout the house.
* **Exits:**
    * Fire Exits: Please familiarise yourself with your nearest Fire Exit.
    * Top Floor Exit: at the exterior sliding door located at the bottom of the stairs
    * Entry Floor Exit: at the front entry or from any of the sliding doors (lounge, bedrooms)
    * Lower Floor Exit: from the garage door or the exterior sliding door at the top of the stairs
* **Action in case of fire:**
    * Please remain calm
    * Touch your door. If hot, do NOT open
    * If you are able to leave your room, proceed to the nearest fire exit
    * Stay beneath the smoke by crawling on the floor if necessary
    * Upon exiting the building, proceed to the assembly area at the bottom of the drive
    * Call 111 to alert the fire brigade, either from your phone or a neighbouring house
    * Do not re-enter the building until authorised to do so by your hosts
* **How to survive if you have to stay in your room:**
    * Stay calm
    * Fill the bathroom sink with water
    * Wet sheets and towels, seal these around the door
    * Call 111 to advise you are unable to evacuate your room

### Hazards

* **Please be aware of the following hazards at Snowbird, which include but are not limited to:**
    * Driveway may have blinds areas when manoeuvring vehicles. Please use extreme caution and have another person watch for children if needed.
    * Large unfenced drop off areas are evident in various areas of the garden. Please do not leave children unsupervised and make yourself familiar with the surrounding area upon arrival.
    * Stairwell - Please refrain young children from playing on the stairwell as there is a risk of fall causing injury.
    * The driveway and outdoor stairs can become very icy after snow with conditions rapidly changing as freeze and thaw action occurs. Please use driveway with caution and fit snow chains if in doubt of road conditions.
    * Please do not leave young children unsupervised around the bathtubs/spa bath when full of water. If this is full of water please ensure children are supervised and the tub is drained immediately after use.
    * BBQ is located on the deck. Should a grease fire occur this a potential hazard. Ensure BBQ is always kept clean and is monitored at all times while being used. Please ensure the BBQ is fully extinguished immediately after use.
    * There is a wood log fire which is a potential a burn risk for unsupervised children. Children should be monitored if necessary. Please follow the instructions in the compendium.
    * Windows/Sliders - Please do not leave young children unattended in these rooms where they can be easily accessed. Risk of causing injury if not opened and closed correctly.

# Essentials & Other

### Insects

* Central Queenstown does not have sandflies (similar to mosquitos), however we do have small, non-harmful flying bugs which are attracted to light. If you would like to have your windows open in the evenings for ventilation, please ensure to turn out your lights or alternatively keep blinds closed. This will help to minimise insects in your room.
* If you are travelling to the West Coast, the Glenorchy/Dart River area or Milford or Doubtful Sound area then we recommend taking insect repellent with you to avoid being bitten by the sandflies.

### Power

* Generally all power sockets in New Zealand are 230/240 volts (50Hz). If you are plugging in devices, please ensure they are rated accordingly.
