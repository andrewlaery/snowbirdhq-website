### The Home & Neighbourhood

The downstairs apartment at 3 Kent Street is a thoughtfully converted lower level (originally a double garage / engineer's workshop) — now a contemporary 2-bedroom apartment with floor-to-ceiling windows along one side and a small extra-bed nook (windowless, suitable for kids) for the "0.5" in the "2.5 bedrooms" descriptor. Quiet, sunny, and direct access to the Queenstown Hill walking track via the back garden.

Underfloor heating throughout, set to a constant 20°C, plus a Daikin heat pump in the living/dining room for additional warmth or summer cooling.

**Note**: the upstairs apartment may be occupied during your stay (it's a separate listing). Sound carries between the levels — the floor between is concrete but heavy footsteps from above are audible. The owners chose the whole-home option specifically to solve this for groups who want privacy.
