## Property Orientation

### Internet / Wi-Fi

- **Network:** Monkeymousewifi
- **Password:** bendalong
- A **Wi-Fi extender** is upstairs — turn Wi-Fi off then on if you're not getting fast internet; your device will pick the strongest signal.
- Note: the Southern Lakes occasionally has temperamental Wi-Fi due to atmospheric conditions and high demand. Speeds can slow during peak periods.

### Heating

- **Underfloor heating** is set at **20°C** in each room — please don't adjust the controls (system takes ~2 days to change temperature).
- See **Appliances** below for heat pump and gas fireplace operating instructions.

### Garden + Walking Track

- The garden has fruit trees (quince, apricot, apple) — please feel welcome.
- The **Queenstown Hill walking track** is accessible via the back garden — recommended for a walk straight from the house.
- Please bring **outdoor cushions inside at night** so they're not damp the next day.

### Other Amenities

- **Self-cleaning oven** — no chemicals; follow the on-screen instructions.
- **Dishwasher detergent**: use only the **dishwasher powder provided** — liquid causes a fault and repair fees apply.
- **Dining table**: please use placemats and coasters provided — no hot items directly on the table.
