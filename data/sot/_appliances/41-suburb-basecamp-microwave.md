### Beko Built-in Microwave + Grill Combi

A built-in combination microwave + grill oven.

- **Set the clock first** after any power cut.
- **Six function pads**: power levels, grill, combi (microwave + grill), defrost, auto-cook.
- **Three control buttons**: clock, pause, start.
- **Bottom dial**: sets cook time / weight depending on selected function.
- **Combi mode** combines microwave power with the grill element — good for melting cheese on top while heating through.
