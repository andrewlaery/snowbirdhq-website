### Sonos Audio System — SkyCrest Living + Upstairs Deck

Whole-home Sonos speakers covering the **living room** and the **upstairs deck**. Always on — no power button.

#### Getting Started
1. Connect your phone to our Wi-Fi (network: SkyCrest).
2. Download the **Sonos app**.
3. When asked, choose **"Use a system that's already set up"**.
4. Pick the room you want to play in.

#### Physical Controls (Lounge)
In the **wooden side panel beside the gas fireplace** you'll find two small black boxes:

- **Bottom box** — controls the **Living Room (downstairs)** Sonos. Play/pause + volume.
- **Top box** — controls the **Upstairs** Sonos.

Handy when you just want to pause or nudge the volume.

#### Apple Devices (AirPlay)
1. Open **Control Center** (swipe down from top-right).
2. Tap the **AirPlay** icon.
3. Pick a speaker:
    - **SkyCrest-Living** (Living Room)
    - **SkyCrest-UpstairsDeck** (Outdoor speakers)
4. Play from any app (Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube).

#### Spotify (Any Phone)
1. Open Spotify (on our Wi-Fi).
2. Start a song.
3. Tap **Devices Available**.
4. Pick the Sonos speaker.

#### TV Sound
- TV audio routes through the Living Room speakers automatically.
- Use the **TV remote** to adjust volume — TV sound takes priority over music.

#### Troubleshooting
- Check you're on our Wi-Fi.
- Close and reopen the Sonos app.
- If needed, contact us — happy to help.

#### When You're Done
Just stop playing music — no shutdown required.
