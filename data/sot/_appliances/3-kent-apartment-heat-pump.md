### Daikin Heat Pump (Living/Dining)
Wall-mounted Daikin heat pump providing both **heating and cooling**.

- **Remote control** sits in a cradle on the wall **next to the main light switch**.

#### Operating
1. **Point the remote** at the AC wall unit.
2. Press the **centre green ON button**.
3. Slide the **cover down** to display the programme buttons.
4. Press **mode** to select your desired program:
   - **☀ Sun icon** = heating
   - **❄ Snowflake icon** = cooling
5. Use **temp up/down arrows** to set temperature.
6. Use the **fan button** to set fan speed.
7. **Replace the remote in the cradle** when finished.
