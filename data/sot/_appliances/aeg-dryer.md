### AEG Dryer

**AEG Dryer (top appliance).**
- Turn on with the **On/Off** button.
- Select the drying program using the wheel.
- Select the dryness level.
- Press **Start/Pause**.
- Every time you use the dryer, check / clean the lint filter inside
  the bottom of the dryer door.
