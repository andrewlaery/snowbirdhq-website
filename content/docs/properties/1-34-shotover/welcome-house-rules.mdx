# Welcome

### Dear Guest

Thank you for choosing to stay at 1/34 Shotover Street. We trust your stay here will be more than enjoyable. For your convenience we have prepared a Guidebook including House Rules to assist you during your stay. Should you experience any problems or difficulties with regard to the home during your stay please do not hesitate to contact us via the booking platform. We are here to help!

### The Home & Neighbourhood

1/34 Shotover Street is a stylish two-bedroom apartment in the heart of central Queenstown with a large balcony. Restaurants, shops, Lake Wakatipu and the Queenstown Gardens are all within easy walking distance. The intercity bus stop is less than 100m away, and the Snow Centre ski bus pickup on Shotover Street is a 5-minute walk. Please note the apartment is central and lively — the surrounding street can be busy during the evenings, especially in summer.

# House Rules

### A Quick Note

- We're delighted to share our apartment with you in the middle of Queenstown's central village
- Please follow these house guidelines so we can maintain great relationships with our neighbours and comply with local regulations
- Please remove your shoes inside to help keep our apartment clean and comfortable

### House Items

* Consumable items in the apartment are provided free of charge on a fair use basis (this includes basic cooking supplies, tea, coffee, laundry powder, dishwasher capsules/powder, soaps & body wash, etc).
* Unused items remain property of the home.

### Security & Responsibility

* Please help us make your stay an enjoyable and memorable one. Please look after your valuables and secure your accommodation. Despite protective measures we are unable to assume any responsibility and will not be liable for any loss or damage to your possessions in or around the property.
* The hosts/owners are not responsible for any accidents, injuries, or illness that occur while on the premises or its facilities, as well as loss of personal belongings or valuables of the guest. This includes adults and children.
* **Note:** During your stay, the apartment alarm is not in operation.

### Check-In & Check-Out

* Check-In after: 3pm
* Check-Out before: 10am

**Note:** Late checkout to 11am may be possible if no same-day booking — please ask the host. Multiple parties rely on these times for cleaning and maintenance.

**When checking out:**
* ensure the apartment appears as it was when you first arrived
* strip the bed linens and leave them in the bedrooms
* start the dishwasher
* take rubbish and recycling to the bins below the steps in the alleyway
* turn off all lights and any electrical items you have used. Do not switch off utility items such as Wi-Fi or modems
* turn off heated towel rails, heat pump, and oil heaters
* ensure the apartment is secured (close and lock all windows and doors)
* return the front door key to the lockbox

### Access

* **Location:** The apartment is accessed via the alleyway between Canyon Swing and the Kebab Shop on Shotover Street. Google Maps will NOT take you directly to the apartment — please follow the photo walkthrough in your check-in instructions (sent via the booking platform 24 hours before arrival).
* **Front Door:** Lockbox beside the front door. Code provided in your booking information.
* **No elevator:** Stairs are required to access the apartment. This may be a consideration for elderly guests or guests with mobility requirements.

### Guests

* The maximum total number of guests is 4.
* The guest responsible for registering the booking must be 25 years old or older.
* All guests staying must be registered with the host beforehand and only registered guests may spend the night.
* Guests are responsible for the actions of any visitors during their stay.
* Any unregistered guest can be asked to leave at any time without cause.

### Visitors & events/parties

* Strictly registered guests only. No unregistered visitors are permitted.
* Parties are NOT allowed.
* No confetti, glitter, or excessive rubbish allowed otherwise an additional cleaning fee will be charged.
* Failure to comply may result in the termination of the contract and additional charges.

### Quiet Hours

Central Queenstown is a lively area and we ask that guests are considerate of neighbours in the surrounding apartments:
- Please keep noise levels considerate between 10pm - 7am
- No music or amplified sound on the balcony between 8pm - 8am
- After 8pm, please continue activities indoors with the balcony door closed

Note: Under the District Plan, noise limits are:
- Daytime (8am - 8pm): 50dBA LAeq (15 min)
- Nighttime (8pm - 8am): 40dBA LAeq

**Note on ambient noise:** The apartment is next to a pub/restaurant whose outdoor dining area is active roughly 7pm–midnight, especially in warmer months (October–February). Most guests find this acceptable as part of the central-Queenstown experience; light sleepers may wish to bring earplugs or use a white-noise app.

### Parking

There is no parking at the apartment itself. Our preferred arrangement for guests:

- **Man Street Carpark (free):** Entry via Man Street, behind the building. A QR code lanyard is provided on the dining table for the exit barrier.
- **At the exit barrier:** Scan the QR code in the **LEFT lane**.
- **If the QR code doesn't scan:** Try cleaning it first, then the middle lane, then press the help button on the barrier.
- **Height limit:** Approximately 2m at Man Street — not suitable for campervans.

**Important:** There is a 10-minute loading zone outside Canyon Swing on Shotover Street but it is camera-enforced and heavily policed. **We do NOT recommend using it for luggage drop-off** — tickets are issued easily. If you must drop luggage, err strongly on the side of caution.

**No overnight street parking** on Shotover Street — 24/7 camera enforcement.

### Rubbish & Recycling

* Rubbish bins are located below the steps in the alleyway leading to the apartment.
* Please deposit all rubbish and recycling in the bins on checkout.
* Recycling options in central Queenstown are more limited than in residential areas.

### Littering

* NO littering. This includes leaving food outside for long periods.
* If any part of the apartment or balcony is found to be littered requiring additional cleaning, an additional cleaning charge may apply.

### Commercial photography

* Commercial photography is not allowed unless guests have written permission from the host beforehand. Please contact the host for pricing.

### Pets

* Pets are NOT permitted. If you bring a pet, the host is not liable for any incident involving your pet and any other person or animal, and you will be asked to leave without a refund.

### Smoking, vaping, & e-cigarettes

* Smoking or vaping is NOT permitted inside the apartment or on the balcony.
* There may be a $250+ additional fee added for smoking inside and/or not properly disposing of cigarette butts.

### Security cameras & other equipment

* Tampering with any security, safety, or utility devices (e.g., smoke detectors, internet, connectivity, power) will result in the immediate termination of your booking without a refund.

### Child safety

* The apartment is on an upper floor with internal stairs to the bedroom level — please supervise children carefully, especially overnight trips to the bathroom.
* Guests are fully responsible for the safety and security of their children at all times.

### Damages/breakages & furniture

* Please respect our apartment as if it is your own.
* Only use appliances, equipment, and furniture for their intended uses.
* Removing any items from the property, including linens, towels, cookware, games and kitchen items, is not permitted and you will be charged accordingly.
* Should any damage occur, please notify the owner immediately.
* Every item will be looked at before arrival and after departure.

### Acceptance of House Rules

* By accepting this reservation, it is agreed that the House Rules are understood and accepted, and all guests are assuming the risk of any harm arising from their use of the premises or others whom they invite to use the premises.
