### Dishwasher

A standard built-in dishwasher.

- **Detergent**: tablets are provided — drop one into the dispenser inside the door.
- **Loading**: cutlery in the basket on the lower rack; plates and pots on the lower rack; glasses and cups on the upper rack.
- **Programs**: select the wash cycle on the front panel — Normal / Eco / Quick. Eco is the default; use Quick for lightly-soiled loads.
- **Run before checkout** — please start the dishwasher before you leave (loaded with the last items used).
