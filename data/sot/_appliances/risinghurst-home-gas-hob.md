### Gas Hob

- **Hold the knob down to ignite** the burner — the spark fires while you hold the knob in. Once the flame catches, keep the knob pressed for a few extra seconds so the thermocouple stays open, then release.
- If the flame goes out when you release, hold the knob a little longer next time — the thermocouple needs heat.
- **Extractor fan**: switch above the hob — please use when cooking to clear steam and grease.
