# Welcome

Dear Guest,

Thank you for choosing to stay at 1/34 Shotover Street. We trust your stay here will be more than enjoyable. For your convenience we have prepared this Guidebook to assist you during your stay. Should you experience any problems or difficulties with regard to the apartment during your stay please do not hesitate to contact us via the booking platform. We are here to help!

### The Home & Neighbourhood

1/34 Shotover Street is a stylish two-bedroom apartment in the heart of central Queenstown with a large balcony. Restaurants, shops, Lake Wakatipu and the Queenstown Gardens are all within easy walking distance. The intercity bus stop is less than 100 m away, and the Snow Centre ski bus pickup on Shotover Street is a 5-minute walk.

Please note the apartment is central and lively — the surrounding street can be busy during the evenings, especially in summer.

### Bedrooms & Sleeping

- **Bedroom 1:** Queen bed (upstairs)
- **Bedroom 2:** Queen bed (upstairs)
- **Maximum occupancy:** 4 guests
- A cot for infants can be supplied on request.

# House Rules

### A Quick Note

- We're delighted to share our apartment with you in the heart of Queenstown's central village.
- Please follow these house guidelines so we can maintain great relationships with our neighbours and comply with local regulations.
- Please remove your shoes inside to help keep our apartment clean and comfortable.

### House Items

- Consumable items are provided free of charge on a fair-use basis (cooking basics, tea, coffee, laundry powder, dishwasher capsules, soaps & body wash).
- Unused items remain property of the home.

### Security & Responsibility

- Please look after your valuables and secure your accommodation. We are unable to assume responsibility for any loss or damage to your possessions.
- Hosts/owners are not responsible for any accidents, injuries, or illness while on the premises.

### Check-In & Check-Out

- Check-in after 3pm. Check-out before 10am.
- **When checking out:**
  - Ensure each room appears as it did when you arrived.
  - Place all rubbish and recycling in the bins provided.
  - Load all dirty dishes into the dishwasher.
  - Turn off all lights and electrical items used (do not switch off Wi-Fi or modems).
  - Lock all windows and doors.
  - Return the key to the lockbox.

### Access

- Self check-in via lockbox beside the front door. Code 3411 is also sent via the booking platform 24 hours before arrival.
- Access via the alleyway between Canyon Swing and the Kebab Shop on Shotover Street — Google Maps will not route directly to the apartment.

### Guests

- Maximum total number of guests: 4.
- Lead guest must be 25 years old or older.
- All overnight guests must be registered with the host beforehand.

### Visitors & Events / Parties

- Strictly registered guests only. No unregistered visitors permitted.
- Parties are NOT allowed.

### Quiet Hours

- Please keep noise levels considerate between 10pm and 7am.
- This is a central CBD apartment building — please be mindful of neighbours.

### Parking

- Off-site parking only (free). Park in a Shotover Street loading bay to unload, then move to the underground carpark across the road. Carpark height limit 2.2 m — no camper vans.

### Smoking, Vaping, & E-Cigarettes

- Smoking or vaping is NOT permitted inside the apartment or on the balcony.
- A $250+ fee may apply for smoking inside or improper disposal of cigarette butts.

### Pets

- Pets are NOT permitted.

### Damages

- Please respect our apartment as if it were your own. Notify the host immediately of any damage.
