### Health & Safety

* The apartment is equipped with certified smoke detectors and a fire extinguisher for use in an emergency.
* **Emergency Exit:** In the event of an emergency, please exit the apartment through the front door and descend the two flights of stairs to the parking area. The emergency meeting point is in the parking area, a safe distance from the building.

### Emergency Procedures - Fire

* The apartment is fitted with smoke detectors — please do not disconnect them.
* **Exits:**
    * Front door → external stairs → parking area.
    * The balcony is not a fire exit.
* **Action in case of fire:**
    * Please remain calm.
    * Touch your bedroom door. If hot, do NOT open.
    * If you can leave, proceed to the front door and down the stairs to the parking area.
    * Stay beneath the smoke by crawling on the floor if necessary.
    * Assemble in the parking area, a safe distance from the building.
    * Call 111 to alert the fire brigade.
    * Do not re-enter the building until authorised to do so.
* **How to survive if you have to stay in your room:**
    * Stay calm.
    * Fill the bathroom sink with water.
    * Wet sheets and towels, seal these around the door.
    * Call 111 to advise you are unable to evacuate your room.

### Hazards

* **Please be aware of the following hazards at 6A/643 Frankton Road:**
    * **External stairs:** Two flights of stairs from the parking area to the apartment. Please take care with luggage, and use caution in wet or icy conditions.
    * **Balcony:** An upper-level balcony with a drop to the complex below — please take normal care.
    * **Construction site:** The adjacent apartment block is under construction (expected to complete late 2026). The path to the rubbish bins may be uneven or disrupted by construction work — please take care when moving through the complex.

### Noise expectations

* **Construction noise:** Weekdays approx. 7:45am–6pm, with limited weekend work. Includes heavy machinery and piling rigs. Not something we can influence, but we want you prepared — earplugs are available from Life Pharmacy Wilkinsons in town if helpful.
* **Road traffic:** The apartment faces Frankton Road and road traffic is audible in the bedroom. Double glazing helps but does not fully block it. Light sleepers may want earplugs or a white noise app.
* **Flight path:** The property is under the Queenstown Airport flight path — occasional aircraft noise during the day.

# Essentials & Other

### Insects

* Central Queenstown does not have sandflies, however we do have small non-harmful flying bugs which are attracted to light. If you would like to have your windows open in the evenings for ventilation, please ensure to turn out your lights or keep blinds closed.
* If you are travelling to the West Coast, the Glenorchy/Dart River area or Milford/Doubtful Sound, we recommend taking insect repellent.

### Power

* Generally all power sockets in New Zealand are 230/240 volts (50 Hz). If you are plugging in devices, please ensure they are rated accordingly.
* The fuse box is behind the bedroom door. If the power trips, flip the switch up. If it trips repeatedly, please contact the host.
