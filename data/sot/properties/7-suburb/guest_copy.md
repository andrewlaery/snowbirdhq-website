### Health & Safety

This home is equipped with certified smoke detectors, CO2 detectors, a first aid kit and fire extinguishers for use in an emergency.

- **First Aid Kit:** Located in the cupboard above the hobs and rangehood.
- **Fire Extinguisher:** Located underneath the kitchen sink.
- **Emergency Exit:** In the event of an emergency, please exit the home through the front door. The emergency meeting point is outside the garage in the driveway.

### Emergency Procedures – Fire

Wright's Garden is fitted with smoke detectors throughout the house.

**Exits:**

- Entry Floor Exits:
    - Front Door
    - Rear Door
    - Dining Sliding Door

**Action in case of fire:**

1. Please remain calm
2. Touch your door. If hot, do NOT open
3. If you are able to leave your room, proceed to the nearest fire exit
4. Stay beneath the smoke by crawling on the floor if necessary
5. Upon exiting the building, proceed to the assembly area at the bottom of the drive
6. Call 111 to alert the fire brigade, either from your phone or a neighbouring house
7. Do not re-enter the building until authorised to do so by your hosts

**How to survive if you have to stay in your room:**

1. Stay calm
2. Fill the bathroom sink with water
3. Wet sheets and towels, seal these around the door
4. Call 111 to advise you are unable to evacuate your room

### Hazards

Please be aware of the following hazards at Wright's Garden, which include but are not limited to:

- **Driveway:** May have blind areas when manoeuvring vehicles. Please use extreme caution and have another person watch for children if needed.
- **Drop-off areas:** Large unfenced drop off areas are evident in various areas of the garden. Please do not leave children unsupervised and make yourself familiar with the surrounding area upon arrival.
- **Icy conditions:** The driveway and outdoor stairs can become very icy after snow with conditions rapidly changing as freeze and thaw action occurs. Please use driveway with caution and fit snow chains if in doubt of road conditions.

#### Bosch Induction Hobs (PUE611BB5)
- **User Manual:** [Link](https://media3.bsh-group.com/Documents/9001867174_A.pdf)
- **Description:** Bosch induction cooktop that heats pots and pans directly using magnetic technology. Faster, more energy-efficient, and safer than conventional cooktops.

**Starting the Hob**
1. Touch the main power switch (👉) to turn on the hob
2. Select a cooking zone by touching its corresponding circle symbol
3. Within 10 seconds, set your desired power level (1-9) using the + or - symbols
   - Level 1: Lowest heat (melting, gentle warming)
   - Level 9: Highest heat (fast boiling, searing)

**While Cooking**
- The hob automatically detects if suitable cookware is placed on a cooking zone
- You can adjust the power level at any time by selecting the cooking zone and pressing + or -
- For extra power, use the PowerBoost function by pressing the BOOST button

**Stopping the Hob**
1. To turn off a specific cooking zone: Select it and use - to reduce to 0
2. To turn off the entire hob: Press the main power switch (👉)
3. Beware of residual heat! The indicator (H/h) will show which zones are still hot

**Important Notes**
- Only use cookware with a magnetic base (test with a magnet if unsure)
- The hob will automatically shut off if all cooking zones remain unused for 20 seconds
- Don't place metal objects like utensils on the cooking surface
- Clean with a glass ceramic cleaner after the surface has cooled

#### Miele Washing Machine (WCA020 WCS)
- **User Manual:** [Link](https://media.miele.com/downloads/ca/b8/06_C7F13838C02F1EEF988BF928C3BFCAB8.pdf)
- **Description:** Front-loading Miele washing machine with various program options for different fabric types.

**Starting the Machine:**
1. Open the door and load your laundry loosely in the drum
2. Add appropriate detergent to the dispenser drawer (marked with compartments for pre-wash, main wash, and fabric softener)
3. Close the door securely
4. Turn the program selector to choose your desired wash program (e.g., Cottons, Minimum Iron, Woolens)
5. Adjust temperature and spin speed if needed using the sensors on the control panel
6. Press the Start/Add Laundry sensor (▶️)

**During Operation:**
- You can add laundry during a cycle by touching the Start/Add Laundry sensor if the 🔒 symbol is not lit
- The display will show the remaining program time

**Ending a Cycle:**
1. Wait for the program to finish (the door remains locked during the Anti-crease phase)
2. Turn the program selector to the OFF position (◯)
3. Open the door and remove laundry promptly to prevent creasing
4. Check for small items in the door seal
5. Leave the detergent drawer slightly open to air dry

**Important Tips:**
- Empty all pockets before washing
- Sort laundry by color and care label instructions
- Maximum load is 7.0 kg of dry laundry
