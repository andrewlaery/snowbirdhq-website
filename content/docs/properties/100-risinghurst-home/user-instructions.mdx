# User Instructions

### Internet / WiFi

The internet WiFi can be accessed by:
* **Network:** Bethan & Paddy
* **Password:** provided on the welcome card at check-in

The Wi-Fi modem is on the desk in the second lounge room.

### Access & Security

* **Lockbox:** On the right-hand side wall of the garage, above the bins. Code provided in your booking information.
* **Front door:** The key from the lockbox opens the front door (to the left of the garage).
* **Garage fob:** A separate fob is kept with the house key — please return both at checkout.

### Layout

* Main living level with lounge (heat pump), second lounge (gas fire), full kitchen, dining.
* 3 bedrooms + bathrooms.
* Garage accessible from inside the house.

### Bed Configuration

* **3 bedrooms** — 1 king bed, 2 queen beds (max 6 guests).
* **Travel cot and highchair** available on request.

### Heating

* **Mitsubishi Electric heat pump / A/C** in the lounge by the patio door — remote controlled. Mode buttons: sun icon = heat, snowflake = cool. Fan speed button adjusts airflow.
* **Rinnai gas fire** in the second lounge — press the ON button to start, use arrows to adjust temperature. Press 'Set' to display the current temperature.
* **Electric heaters** in each bedroom wardrobe — portable, plug in where needed.
* **Heated towel rails** in the bathrooms — please turn off at checkout.

### Hot Water

* **400 L hot water tank** — provides approximately 7 showers per full tank.
* **Reheat time:** approximately 1 hour — stagger showers if possible.

### Kitchen

* **Gas hob:** Hold the knobs down to ignite the burner.
* **Extractor fan:** Switch above the hob — use when cooking.
* **Oven** (electric).
* **Dishwasher.**
* Pots, pans and cooking equipment provided.
* Cleaning supplies are under the garage sink (orange tub — includes drain cleaner).
* **Tap water is safe to drink.**

### TV & Entertainment

* **Samsung Smart TV** in the lounge — screen shows artwork in sleep mode.
* Press 'Home' on the white remote for the main menu.
* Streaming services available (Netflix, etc.) via the Samsung TV app.
* NZ Freeview available.

### Laundry

* **Washing machine and dryer** in the garage (accessible from inside the house).
* Laundry powder provided.

### Garage

* Accessible from inside the house via the internal door.
* **Garage door:** Wall switch inside the garage. If the door reopens after closing, press the button again — it can occasionally play up.
* Contains laundry, cleaning supplies, and space for **ski equipment storage**.
* **Large bins** provided near the entrance for drying ski boots indoors.
