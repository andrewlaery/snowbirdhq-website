### Gas Fire (Upstairs Lounge)

Not recommended as a primary heat source — use as needed while
you're in the upstairs lounge. The gas fire is remote-controlled.
Remote is on the wall in the same room.

1. Press the **top button** on the black switch directly under the
   remote (this powers the gas fire).
2. Press **Power** on the remote. Confirm the temperature appears
   on the digital display (if not, press Power again).
3. The fireplace takes a few seconds to ignite.
4. The gas fire is on a **60-minute timer** — repeat the steps to
   reignite if needed.
5. Always turn the gas fire off after use.
