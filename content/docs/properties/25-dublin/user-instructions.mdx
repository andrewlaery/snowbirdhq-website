### Heating Guide

#### Overview
- Snowbird sits in New Zealand's Southern Alps, where temperatures can swing dramatically (from 30°C+ to near freezing)
- The house features a heated concrete floor slab that maintains a comfortable preset temperature throughout
- Wall radiators are integrated into this whole-house heating system

#### For Best Comfort
- Keep doors and windows closed during cold periods to maintain the heat stored in the concrete slab
- Adjust your clothing for individual temperature preferences
- *Please contact your host if you need any temperature adjustments - we're happy to help make your stay comfortable!

#### When Leaving the House
- Close all doors and windows during cooler weather to maintain ideal temperature for your return

#### Top Floor Master Bedroom Tips
- This room can be more challenging to regulate as heat rises from below
- Recommended approach:
    - Close all bedroom doors (entrance and bathroom)
    - Close the atrium sliding window/door
    - Open 1-3 windows as needed to adjust temperature
    - Use the provided floor fan for additional airflow

#### Wall Radiators
- These are fully integrated with the whole-house heating system
- They are automatically managed by the general house system and require no adjustment

#### Gas Fire (Upstairs Lounge)
- Not recommended as a primary heat source
- Use only as needed while you're in the upstairs lounge
#### Studio Wood Burner (located in the lounge / dining room)
* Lift the handle up to open and close the fire door.
* Light the fire with fire lighters provided in the wood box.
* Leave the door slightly open as the fire establishes and the glass warms to avoid build-up of condensation.
* Close the door as soon as the fire is established, ensure it is locked back into place.
* Do not leave the door open as this could over-fire and damage the appliance.
* Use the baskets provided to collect the firewood and kindling which is stored in the garage.
* If there is excess ash in the fireplace, please contact the hosts.
* Important:
	* Open only by touching the wooden knob on the front
	* Please do not empty ash contents in to the rubbish bins or leave in an area exposed to high winds.

#### Gas Fire
* The Gas fire in the upstairs lounge is remote controlled. The remote is found on the wall in the same room.
* **Instructions:**
	* Directly under where the remote is on the wall, press to top button on the black switch (this provides power to the gas fire).
	* Press the Power button on the remote. Ensure after you press the power button, you can see the temperate on the digital display of the remote (if not, press the power button again).
	* The fireplace will takes a few seconds to respond and ignite.
	* Note the gas fire is on a 60 minute timer. After 60 minute, you will need to follow the process above to power the gas fire again.
	* Please ensure you turn off the gas fire after use.

### 🛁 Hot Tub / Spa

#### 1. Opening the Spa
- Use the **cover lifter** to open the lid.
- Lift **slowly and gently** to avoid damage.
- Fold the cover fully onto the lifter.
- If the spa cover is locked, use the plastic key found with the garage door remote

#### 2. Before You Get In
To help keep the water clean and safe:
- **Have a quick shower** before entering.
- Please rinse off:
  - Sunscreen
  - Moisturiser / lotions
  - Makeup
  - Dirt, sweat, sand
- Avoid entering if you’ve used **fake tan**, heavy oils, or hair products.
- **No glass** in or near the spa.

#### 3. Using the Control Panel
- The temperature is preset for comfort and safety.
- Press **JETS** to turn the jets on/off.
- Press **LIGHTS** to turn underwater lights on/off.
- If the Control Panel Appears Locked
- If the LCD shows a padlock icon or buttons are unresponsive: ➡️ **Press and hold the *OPTIONS* button and the *LIGHTS* button together for 3–5 seconds**

#### 4. While Using the Spa
- Limit soak time to **15–20 minutes**.
- Keep long hair and loose items away from drains and suction points.
- Supervise children at all times.
- Avoid using the spa while intoxicated.

#### 5. After You Finish
##### 🔒 Close & Secure the Cover
- Turn **JETS** and **LIGHTS** off.
- Pull the cover back over gently using the lifter.
- Ensure all clips are fastened to retain heat and protect the cover from wind.

##### 🧪 Add Chlorine (Important)
To keep the water clean for the next guests:
- Add **1 teaspoon of chlorine granules per person** who used the spa.
- Sprinkle evenly over the water with jets **running for 1–2 minutes**.

#### 6. Things to Avoid
Please do **not**:
- Add bubble bath, oils, salts, or soaps to the water.
- Sit or stand on the spa cover.
- Change the heater, temperature lock, or filtration settings.
- Eat or drink in the spa (plastic drinkware only if needed).

#### 7. If You Notice a Problem
If you see:
- Cloudy or foamy water
- A strong or unusual smell
- Error messages on the display

Please message us — we’re happy to help.

#### Bosch Microwave/Oven (HBC86K753B)

* **User Manual:** https://www.bosch-home.co.nz/supportdetail/product/HBC86K753B/09
* **Instructions:**
    * Starting the Microwave:
        * Turn the oven on by pressing the Power button.
        * Select the microwave power setting (e.g., 90, 180, 360, 600, or 900 W) based on your cooking needs. For defrosting delicate foods, use 90 W; for defrosting and continued cooking, use 180 W; for cooking meat and heating delicate foods, 360 W; for heating and cooking food, 600 W; and for heating liquids, use 900 watts.
        * Set the cooking time using the rotary selector.
        * Start the microwave by pressing the Start/Pause button. The cooking time will count down in the display.
    * During Cooking:
        * You can pause the microwave by pressing the Start/Pause button. To continue, press the Start/Pause button again.
        * If you need to open the oven door during cooking, the operation will be suspended. Close the door and press the Start/Pause button to resume.
    * Safety and Care:
        * Do not heat liquids or food in tightly sealed containers as they may explode.
        * Use microwave-safe ovenware made of glass, glass ceramic, porcelain, ceramic, or heat-resistant plastic. Avoid using metal ovenware.
        * Keep at least 2 cm distance between metal objects (like a spoon in a glass) and the oven walls or door to prevent sparks.
        * After cooking, allow the food to stand for 2-5 minutes for even temperature distribution.
    * After Use:
        * At the end of cooking, a signal sounds, and the cooking time shows 00:00 min:sec.
        * Press the 0 button to cancel the signal if needed.
        * Switch off the microwave by pressing the Power button.

#### ➡️ Sonos System
* **User Manual:** [Link](https://www.sonos.com/en-us/support/by-product)
* **Description:** Your Airbnb is equipped with a Sonos sound system consisting of:
	- A Sonos Beam soundbar connected to the Samsung Frame TV
	- Two additional Sonos One speakers
	- All devices are connected to a dedicated Sonos account via Ethernet and WiFi
##### ***Basic Operations ==>***

**What You Have**
- 2 Sonos One speakers (in the Media Room and in the Living Room)

**Getting Started**
1. The Sonos system is always powered on and ready to use
2. Download the Sonos App (iOS and Android)
3. Connect to Our Wi-Fi
4. Open the Sonos App
	- When prompted, select "Use a system that's already set up"
	- The app will automatically detect the house's Sonos system
5. You now have control of all Sonos speakers in the house

**Basic Controls - Using the Sonos App**
- Browse: Explore music services, radio stations, and podcasts
- Rooms: View and control individual speakers or group them together
- Search: Find specific songs, artists, albums, or playlists
- Settings: Access advanced features and customization options

**Basic Controls - Volume Control**
- Adjust volume using the slider in the app
- Control individual speaker volumes or adjust grouped speakers together
- Use physical volume buttons on each Sonos One speaker
- When using the TV, control Beam volume with your TV remote

**Playing Music - Streaming Services**
1. Tap the "Browse" tab in the Sonos app
2. Select your preferred music service (Spotify, Apple Music, Amazon Music, etc.)
3. Log in to your personal account if prompted
4. Browse and select content to play
5. Music will play through your selected speaker(s)

**Playing Music - Radio**
1. Go to "Browse" > "Radio by TuneIn" in the Sonos app
2. Browse stations by category or search for specific stations
3. Select a station to begin playback

**Playing Music - Personal Music Library**
1. Go to "Browse" > "On this Mobile Device" to play music stored on your phone
2. Follow prompts to allow access to your device's music library

**Casting - AirPlay (for Apple devices)**
1. Connect your iPhone, iPad, or Mac to the house WiFi
2. Swipe down from the top-right corner (or up from bottom on older iPhones) to access Control Center
3. Tap the AirPlay icon (triangle with circles)
4. Select the Sonos speaker(s) you want to cast to
5. Start playing audio from any app on your device

**Casting - Casting from Android Devices**
1. Connect your Android device to the house WiFi
2. Open your media app (YouTube, Google Play Music, etc.)
3. Look for the Cast icon (looks like a TV with a WiFi symbol)
4. Tap the Cast icon and select the Sonos speakers
5. Your content will now play through the selected Sonos speaker(s)
6. Control playback from your device or the Sonos app

**Casting - Spotify Connect**
1. Open the Spotify app on your device
2. Connect to the house WiFi
3. Play a song
4. Tap "Devices Available" at the bottom of the screen
5. Select the Sonos speaker(s) from the list
6. Your audio will now play through the selected speaker(s)

**Casting - Other Music Services**
Many streaming services offer direct casting options:
- Look for a "Devices," "Cast," or "Connect" icon in your music app
- Select the Sonos speaker from available devices
- Steps may vary slightly depending on the app

**Bluetooth Connection**
1. Press and hold the Play/Pause button on the back of the Sonos One
2. When you hear a chime and see a flashing blue light, the speaker is in pairing mode
3. Go to Bluetooth settings on your device and select the Sonos speaker
4. Play music from any app on your device

**TV Audio**
1. The Sonos Beam automatically detects when the Samsung Frame TV is turned on
2. TV audio will play through the Beam soundbar
3. Use your TV remote to adjust volume
4. For enhanced sound settings, use the Sonos app > Settings > System > Beam

**Switching Between TV and Music**
1. TV audio takes priority when the TV is on
2. To switch to music while the TV is on, use the Sonos app to select and play music
3. To return to TV audio, stop music playback in the Sonos app

**Troubleshooting - If Speakers Aren't Connecting**
1. Ensure your device is connected to the house WiFi
2. Close and reopen the Sonos app
3. Check that all Sonos speakers have power (solid white light)
4. If problems persist, restart your device

**Troubleshooting - If Audio Quality Is Poor**
1. Check your WiFi connection strength
2. Move closer to the WiFi router if possible
3. Close other apps that might be using bandwidth
4. Reduce the volume slightly if you hear distortion

**Troubleshooting - If a speaker isn't responding:**
1. Check that it has power (light should be on)
2. Restart the speaker by unplugging it, waiting 10 seconds, and plugging it back in
3. Try controlling it from the Sonos app

**Troubleshooting - If you can't connect to the system:**
1. Make sure you're connected to our Wi-Fi
2. Restart the app
3. If problems persist, contact us.

**When Leaving**
- No formal shutdown is required
- Simply stop playing your music when you're done
- The system will automatically enter standby mode
- Sonos speakers use minimal power when not in use

Enjoy your stay and the premium sound experience!
