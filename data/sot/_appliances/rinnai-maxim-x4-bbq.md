### Rinnai Gas BBQ (MAXIM X4 PLUS)

4-burner outdoor gas grill with stainless steel construction — outdoor area.

#### Starting the BBQ
1. Check the gas connection at the bottle is **secure**.
2. Open the **gas valve** at the bottle.
3. Turn a burner control to **HIGH**.
4. Press the **ignition button**.
5. Adjust heat as needed once lit.

#### After Use
1. Turn off **all** burner controls.
2. Close the gas valve at the bottle.
3. Clean the grates when cool with the supplied brush.
