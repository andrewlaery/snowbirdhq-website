### DeLonghi Nespresso Essenza Mini (EN85.BMAE)

[User manual (PDF)](https://www.nespresso.com/shared_res/manuals/essenza-mini/2017/UM_NESPRESSO_ESSENZA_MINI_DELONGHI_PROD_2017_05_01.pdf)

Nespresso capsule coffee machine — espresso and lungo. Located on the kitchen bench.

#### Starting the machine

1. Fill the water tank at the back with fresh, cold water.
2. Plug in and press the power button.
3. Wait for the indicator lights to stop blinking (~25–30 seconds).
4. Place a cup under the coffee outlet.

#### Making coffee

1. Lift the lever to open the capsule compartment.
2. Insert a Nespresso capsule (rounded end facing down).
3. Close the lever completely.
4. Press the espresso button (small cup) or lungo button (large cup).
5. The machine stops automatically when the selected amount is brewed.
6. Lift and close the lever to eject the used capsule.

#### After use

- Empty the drip tray as needed.
- Used-capsule container holds 9–11 capsules before emptying.
- Power off via the button; unplug for extended non-use.

#### Tips

- Descale every 3 months / 300 coffees.
- Use Nespresso or Nespresso-compatible capsules only.
- If unused for several days, run a water cycle without a capsule before brewing.
