### Miele Heat-Pump Dryer (TWD 660 WP EcoSpeed, 8 kg)

An 8 kg heat-pump dryer — gentle, low-temperature drying with no external venting required.

- **Heat-pump tech**: dries at lower temperatures than vented dryers — gentler on fabrics, much lower energy use.
- **Condensate**: empties either to a tank in the dryer (empty between loads) or via a drain hose to the laundry sink (preferred — no manual emptying).
- **EcoSpeed**: cuts dry time by up to 20 minutes vs the standard programme — use for normal cotton/synthetic mixes.
- **Wash2Dry**: when paired with the Miele washer, auto-selects the matching dryer programme.
- **Lint filter**: clean after every load — pull out the door filter, brush out lint.
- **Capacity**: 8 kg max — match what the washer just produced.
