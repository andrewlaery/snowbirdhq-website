# Welcome

### Dear Guest1

Thank you for choosing to stay at Snowbird. We trust your stay here will be more than enjoyable. For your convenience we have prepared a Guidebook to assist you during your stay. Should you experience any problems or difficulties with regard to the home during your stay please do not hesitate to contact us via the booking platform. We are here to help!

### The Home & Neighbourhood

Snowbird is an architectural award winning home nestled on Queenstown's hillside in a very safe and quiet neighbourhood, surrounded by parklands. Enjoy this peaceful and private location, while being just a short walk to the town centre. Here you will find lots of adventure, shopping, dining, nightlife, transportation, and more!

# House Rules

### Important

* This home is a short-term rental property and operates in a quiet residential area with consent from the local council.
* Compliance to the House Rules ensures the home remains compliant to the conditions of the consent from the local council.

### House Items

* Consumable items in the house are provided free of charge on a fair use basis (this includes basic cooking supplies, tea, coffee, laundry powder, dishwasher capsules/powder, soaps & body wash, etc).
* Unused items remain property of the home.

### Security & Responsibility

* Please help us make your stay an enjoyable and memorable one. Please look after your valuables and secure your accommodation. Despite protective measures we are unable to assume any responsibility and will not be liable for any loss or damage to your possessions in or around the property.
* The hosts/owners are not responsible for any accidents, injuries, or illness that occur while on the premises or its facilities, as well as loss of personal belongings or valuables of the guest. This includes adults and children.
* **Note:** The house alarm is not in operation.

### Check-In & Check-Out

| Check-In after | Check-Out before |
| :------------: | :--------------: |
|      3pm       |       10am       |
* **Note:** there are multiple parties who rely on these times for cleaning and maintenance. If you have requirements outside of these times, contact the host and we will do our best to accommodate you.

**When checking out:**
* ensure the house (each room) appears as is when you first arrived
* place all rubbish & recycling (including bottles, cardboard and plastics) in the bins &/or bags provided.
* load all dirty plates and glasses into dishwasher. Do not put any dirty plates, glasses, cutlery, etc away (as if clean).
* turn the lights off and any electrical items you have used. Do not switch off any utilities items such as WiFI, modems, etc)
* turn off heated towel rails and the gas fire
* ensure the house is secured (i.e. all windows and doors)
* leave the access keys/remotes on the kitchen bench

### Access

* Front Door: Unique access code provided to you as part of your booking information
* Garage Door: 1 garage remote is provided
* Spa Pool cover: 1 key to unlock the clips on the spa is provide with the garage remote
* **Note:** there will be a charge for a lost key or garage remote.

### Guests

* The maximum total number of guests is limited to 9.
* The guest responsible for registering the booking must be 25 years old or older.
* All guests staying must be registered with the host beforehand and only registered guests may spend the night.
* Guests are responsible for the actions of any visitors during their stay.
* Any unregistered guest can be asked to leave at any time without cause. 

### Visitors & events/parties

* Strictly registered guests only. No unregistered visitors are permitted.
* Parties are NOT allowed.
* No confetti, glitter, or excessive rubbish allowed otherwise an additional cleaning fee will be charged.
* Failure to comply may result in the termination of the contract, additional charges.

### Quiet hours & outdoor spaces

* Due to local council requirements, use of the outdoor spaces is not allowed between 10pm - 7am.
* Guests must keep noise to a minimum so as not to disturb the neighbours particularly from 10pm- 7am.
* If you are experiencing problems with noise from other properties between these hours, please phone Noise Control: +64 3 4410499.

### Spa Pool Usage

* Use the provided plastic key, located with the garage door remote, to unlock the spa pool cover.
* Please shower before using the spa pool to ensure cleanliness
* Only chlorine may be added to the spa pool. Do not add any other substances, including bubbles or foaming agents.
* Food and beverages are not permitted in the spa pool area.
* Glass items are strictly prohibited.
* **Important Notice:** The use of the spa pool is at your own risk. The host is not liable for any injuries, accidents, or damages that may occur during its use. We kindly request that all guests exercise caution, adhere to safety guidelines, and refrain from using the spa pool under the influence of alcohol or substances. It is your responsibility to ensure that the spa pool is used safely and responsibly. By using the spa pool, you acknowledge and accept these terms.

### Parking

* Please park only in the car parking spaces provided.

### Rubbish & recycling

* Rubbish (red bins) are collected every Wednesday.
* Glass recycling (blue bins) and mixed recycling (yellow bins) are collected every Wednesday. Blue and yellow bins alternate each week.
* Please double check here: (https://www.qldc.govt.nz/services/rubbish-recycling/rubbish-recycling-collection/) or which ones that week the neighbours have out.
* Bins need to be placed out on the street curb beside the driveway by 7am or later the night before. 
* Bins need to be collected off the street by Wednesday evening.

### Littering

* NO littering. This includes leaving food outside and sitting around for long periods of time.
* If any part of the house/yard/appliances is found to be littered that would require additional cleaning, there may be an additional cleaning charge.

### Commercial photography

* Commercial photography is not allowed unless guests have written permission from the host beforehand. Please contact the host for pricing.

### Pets

* Pets are NOT permitted. If you bring a pet, and as well if any incident happens with your pet and any other person or animal, the host is not liable and you will be asked to leave without a refund. 

### Smoking, vaping, & e-cigarettes

* Smoking or vaping is NOT permitted inside or directly outside the house.
* There maybe a $250+ additional fee added for smoking inside the home and/or not properly disposing of cigarette butts.

### Security cameras & other equipment

* Several external security cameras are installed at the front door, the garage, and the rear yard.
* A noise monitor is located in the main living area.
* Tampering with any security, safety, or utility devices (e.g., smoke detectors, carbon monoxide detectors, noise monitors, internet, connectivity, power, etc.) will result in the immediate termination of your booking without a refund.

### Child safety

* Children must be attended to as there are multiple hazards throughout the house for children.
* Guests are fully responsible for the safety and security of their children at all times.

### Damages/breakages & furniture

* Please respect our home as if it is your own.
* Only use appliances, equipment, furniture, etc for their intended uses.
* Please keep the property and all furnishings in good order.
* Removing any items from the property, including linens, and living and kitchen furnishings, is not permitted and you will be charged accordingly if anything is removed.
* Should any damage occur, please notify the owner immediately.
* Any damage to any items at the property, including sofas, chairs, tables, TVs, beds, sheets, towels, pillows, etc., are the responsibility of the renter.
* Every item will be looked at before arrival and after departure.

### Acceptance of House Rules

* By accepting this reservation, it is agreed that the House Rules are understood and accepted, all guests are assuming the risk of any harm arising from their use of the premises or others whom they invite to use the premises.
