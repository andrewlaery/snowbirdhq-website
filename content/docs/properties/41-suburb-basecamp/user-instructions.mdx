# User Instructions

### Internet / WiFi

The internet WiFi can be accessed by:
* **Network:** BaseCamp
* **Password:** provided on the welcome card at check-in

### Access & Security

* **Smart lock** on the front door. Your personal access code is sent approximately 2 days before arrival via the booking platform.
* Private entrance.

### Bed Configuration

* **3 bedrooms** (6 guests max).
* **Baby cot and high chair** available on request — please let the host know in advance. Baby bath and children's dinnerware also available on request.
* Extra pillows and blankets provided.

### Heating & Cooling

* **Air conditioning** and **heating** both available.
* **Fireplace** for additional cosy heating.

### Kitchen

* Fully equipped with dishwasher, oven, electric kettle, microwave, blender, coffee/tea maker, freezer, baking sheet, and cooking utensils.
* Cooking basics (oil, tea, coffee) provided.
* Dining table for shared meals.
* **Tap water is safe to drink.**

### Bathrooms

* 2 bathrooms.
* Hair dryer, body soap, conditioner, shampoo, and essentials provided.

### Laundry

* **Washing machine** and **dryer** in-unit.
* **Drying rack** provided.
* **Iron and ironing board** available.

### Outdoor

* Private garden / backyard.
* **Outdoor grill (BBQ)** with utensils provided.
* Outdoor furniture for dining al fresco.

### Utility Infrastructure

* Check the Aurora Energy Queenstown outage page for area-wide power information during any outage.

### Getting Around / Transportation

* **Walking:** Central Queenstown is a short walk.
* **Queenstown Airport:** ~10–15 minutes by car.
* **Uber** available. **QT Taxis:** 03 450 3000.

### Check-out

* Ensure the house is returned to its original condition.
* Load the dishwasher.
* Dispose of rubbish and recycling in the bins.
* Switch off lights and any fireplace / heating.
* Leave bed linens in place and towels in their original rooms.
* Secure the property (close and lock all windows and doors).
