### Dishwasher

Built-in dishwasher in the kitchen.

- **Eco mode recommended** for normal mixed loads.
- **Tablets** are stored **under the kitchen sink**.
- Scrape solids into the bin first — no need to pre-rinse for the Eco cycle.
- Start the dishwasher as part of check-out.
