### Portable Heater (Bedroom)

A portable electric heater stored in the cupboard, available for the bedroom on cold nights.

- **Plug into the wall socket** in the bedroom, then turn on at the unit.
- **Don't drape clothes / towels** over the heater — keep clearance for safety.
- **Switch off when leaving** the room for an extended period.
