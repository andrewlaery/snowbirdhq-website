### AEG Steam Oven (KSK782220M)

[AEG support page](https://www.aegnewzealand.co.nz/support/cooking/ovens/compact-ovens/ksk782220m/)

Premium steam oven for traditional baking, roasting, grilling, plus steam cooking that keeps food moist.

#### Starting

1. Press ON/OFF (power button).
2. Use up/down arrow keys to select a cooking function; press OK.
3. Set temperature; press OK to begin.

#### Main cooking functions

- *True Fan* — multi-shelf baking
- *Conventional* — top/bottom heat, single-level
- *Grill* — flat foods, toasting
- *Steam* — vegetables, fish, reheating

#### Steam functions

1. Open the water drawer in the control panel.
2. Fill with cold tap water to maximum (~950 ml).
3. Push the drawer back into position.
4. Select a steam function and set temperature.
5. Empty the water drawer when finished.

#### When finished

- Press ON/OFF to turn off.
- Wipe the cavity with a soft cloth.
- If steam was used, leave the door open to dry.

#### Notes

- Always wait for the oven to cool before cleaning.
- After steam cooking, run the Tank Emptying function.
- No abrasive cleaners on oven surfaces.
- Auto-shutoff if left unattended for an extended period.
