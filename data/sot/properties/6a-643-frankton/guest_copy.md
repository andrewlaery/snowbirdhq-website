# Welcome

Dear Guest,

Thank you for choosing to stay at 6A/643 Frankton Road. We trust your stay here will be more than enjoyable. For your convenience we have prepared this Guidebook to assist you during your stay. Should you experience any problems or difficulties during your stay please do not hesitate to contact us via the booking platform. We are here to help!

### The Apartment & Neighbourhood

6A is a one-bedroom apartment in the Alpine Village complex on Frankton Road, with a private balcony overlooking Lake Wakatipu. The balcony view is the standout feature — long lake and mountain panoramas. The apartment is a short bus ride (or 45–60 minute walk along the road) to central Queenstown, with cafes, Cargo Brewery and a lakefront walking path nearby. Queenstown Airport is about 10 minutes' drive.

**A note on construction noise:** An adjacent apartment block is currently under construction (expected to complete late 2026). You'll experience significant daytime noise on weekdays (approx. 7:45am–6pm) including heavy machinery and piling rigs; limited weekend work. The construction site is also visible from the balcony and partially blocks one side of the lake view. We include this disclosure here because the construction is real and ongoing, and we'd rather you arrive prepared than disappointed.

### Bedrooms & Sleeping

- **Bedroom:** King bed (splittable into two singles on request — please ask before arrival).
- **Maximum occupancy:** 2 guests.

# House Rules

### House Items

- Consumable items in the apartment are provided free of charge on a fair-use basis (tea, coffee, cooking oil, soaps & body wash, etc).
- **Welcome extras:** complimentary coffee vouchers for the Boat Shed Cafe and Canyon beer vouchers — please enjoy.

### Security & Responsibility

- Please look after your valuables and secure your accommodation. Hosts are not liable for loss or damage.
- The apartment alarm is not in operation during your stay.

### Check-In & Check-Out

- Check-in after 3pm. Check-out before 10am (strict — multiple parties rely on this time for cleaning and maintenance).
- Late checkout to 11am may be possible if no same-day booking — please ask the host.
- Self check-in via lockbox beside the front door. Code provided in your booking information.

### Access

- 6A is the first apartment at the top of the steps. Park in space 6A (below the steps), go up two flights of stairs, staying to the right. 6A is the first door at the top.
- Lockbox beside the front door. Code provided in your booking information.
- Parcel delivery: use Mountain Club, 85 Beach Street, Queenstown, 9300 (addressed to SnowbirdHQ).

### Quiet Hours

- This is an apartment complex with neighbours on all sides. Please keep noise levels considerate between 10pm and 7am.
- No music or amplified sound on the balcony between 8pm and 8am.
- After 8pm, please continue activities indoors with the balcony door closed.

### Parking

- Dedicated parking space 6A is reserved for this apartment — free, below the steps.
- If someone has parked in 6A when you arrive, please let us know and use any empty spot temporarily.

### Smoking, Vaping, & E-Cigarettes

- Smoking or vaping is NOT permitted inside or on the balcony (body corporate rule).

### Pets

- Pets are NOT permitted.

### Damages

- Please respect our apartment as if it were your own. The sofa is suede — please take care to avoid spills and stains. Notify the host immediately of any damage.
