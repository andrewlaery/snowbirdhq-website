### Samsung Smart TV (UA58NU7103) — Top Floor

58" 4K Smart TV with Tizen OS — top-floor TV nook.

- **Power on**: press **Power** on the Samsung Smart Remote.
- **Navigate**: directional pad → **Select** (centre) to confirm.
- **Volume**: rocker on the remote.
- **Channels**: rocker for channel up/down (Freeview built-in).
- **Apps**: press **Home** for streaming apps (Netflix, YouTube, etc.). Sign in with your own account; **sign out before checking out**.
- **Settings**: press the gear button if you need to adjust picture or audio.
