### Blanco Oven
Built-in electric oven.

- **Isolation switch** is on the wall **above the GPO, left of the cooktop**.

#### Operating
1. Ensure the **isolation switch is ON** at the wall.
2. Turn the **left-hand temperature dial** to set temperature (°C).
3. Turn the **right-hand function dial** to select cooking method (grill, conventional, fan-force, etc.). **See the manual** in the top drawer of the TV cabinet for symbol meanings.
4. To set a timer: press and hold the **centre button**, then use **+/−** to adjust time.
