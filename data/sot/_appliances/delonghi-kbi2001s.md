### DeLonghi Electric Kettle (KBI2001.S)

Electric kettle, 220–240V, 1850–2200W — kitchen bench.

1. Fill kettle with desired amount of water (min 0.5L, max line on the side).
2. Place on the power base.
3. Press the power button.
4. Auto-shutoff when boiled.

Descale with white vinegar + water if scale builds up after extended stays.
