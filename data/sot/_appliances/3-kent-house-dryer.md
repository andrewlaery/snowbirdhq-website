### Fisher & Paykel Auto Sensing 7kg Dryer
7 kg auto-sensing dryer (non-vented).

#### Operating
1. **Open the laundry window** — this is **not a vented dryer**, so the steam needs somewhere to go.
2. Load items (don't overload).
3. Close the door securely.
4. Press the **power button** (top-left of control panel).
5. Select the type of items (Everyday, Delicate, Heavy, etc.).
6. Press **Start**.

A hard-copy manual is available — ask the host if you need a PDF.
