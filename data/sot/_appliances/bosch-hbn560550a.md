### Bosch Single Wall Oven (HBN560550A/02) — Bottom Unit

The kitchen has **two stacked Bosch ovens**. This is the **BOTTOM** unit — a standard built-in electric oven (~60L, 3.4kW element).

- **Multi-function**: fan/convection, conventional bake, grill.
- **Best for**: roasts, large trays, anything that needs a full-size oven cavity.
- The top unit is the [Bosch combination microwave/oven](#) (HBC86Q650A/02) — see its entry for microwave + speed-oven use.

#### Operating

1. **Ensure the wall power switch labelled "oven" is turned ON first** — both ovens share this isolator. If the oven won't turn on, check this switch.
2. Press the power button on the unit.
3. Select cooking function with the **function selector** (top dial) — fan/convection, conventional, grill, or a combination.
4. Set temperature with the **temperature dial**.
5. Set timer if desired and press **Start**.

#### Tips
- **Preheat** for 10–15 min before putting food in.
- **Fan/convection** cooks ~20°C lower + ~25% faster than the conventional setting.
- The light comes on when the oven is on; double-check it's truly off when finished.
