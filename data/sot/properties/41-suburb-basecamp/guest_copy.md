# Welcome

Dear Guest,

Thank you for choosing to stay at BaseCamp. We trust your stay here will be more than enjoyable. For your convenience we have prepared this Guidebook to assist you during your stay. Should you experience any problems or difficulties with regard to the home during your stay please do not hesitate to contact us via the booking platform. We are here to help!

### The Home & Neighbourhood

BaseCamp is a three-bedroom elevated home at 41 Suburb Street, redesigned by Wilde & Stone. The standout glass-roofed sunroom fills with natural light and frames sweeping views of The Remarkables and surrounding mountains. The home sits in a quiet residential pocket close to the town centre, with a private entrance, garden, and outdoor dining area — a 5–10 minute walk to central Queenstown, with Pak'n'Save and the Frankton amenities a short drive away.

### Property Features

- Panoramic mountain and lake views from multiple rooms
- Glass-roofed sunroom conservatory
- Open-plan living area
- Gourmet kitchen with built-in oven and waterfall-edge island
- Private deck with lake and mountain outlook
- Manicured garden with outdoor dining and BBQ
- Free parking for 3 vehicles

### Bedrooms & Sleeping

- **Bedroom 1 (Master):** King bed, ensuite bathroom, garden views
- **Bedroom 2:** Queen bed, lake and mountain views
- **Bedroom 3:** Queen bed, lake and mountain views
- **Maximum occupancy:** 6 guests
- A baby cot, high chair, baby bath, and children's dinnerware are available on request — please let us know in advance.

### Appliances

If you'd like to look up a manual or have a question about how something works, here are the key appliance models in the home:

- **Dishwasher:** Miele G7130 SC AutoDos — drop in a Miele PowerDisk and it auto-doses detergent for ~20 cycles. The door pops open at the end of the cycle to vent moisture.
- **Oven:** Beko OIM 22301 X (built-in 65 L electric). Two pop-out knobs flank the digital clock — left selects heating mode, right sets temperature. Cooling fan keeps running 20–30 min after the oven turns off; that's normal.
- **Cooktop:** Bosch induction stove.
- **Microwave:** Beko built-in microwave + grill combi. Set the clock first after any power cut.
- **Fridge / Freezer:** Beko side-by-side American style with external water + ice dispenser.
- **Washing Machine:** Miele WWD 660 WCS TDos with TwinDos auto-dosing — no need to add detergent each time.
- **Clothes Dryer:** Miele TWD 660 WP EcoSpeed heat-pump dryer — gentle on fabrics, no external venting required.
- **Air Conditioner:** Daikin FVXG50K2V1B Nexura floor-standing unit (heating and cooling) with a radiant front panel for fast, draught-free warmth.
- **Plus:** electric kettle, blender, drying rack, iron and ironing board.

Manuals are available online from the Miele, Beko, Bosch, and Daikin support sites — search by model number above.

# House Rules

### A Quick Note

- We're delighted to share our home with you in this peaceful residential neighbourhood.
- To maintain our great relationship with our neighbours and comply with local regulations, we kindly ask that you follow these house guidelines.
- Please remove your shoes inside to help keep our home clean and comfortable.

### House Items

- Consumable items are provided free of charge on a fair-use basis (cooking basics, tea, coffee, laundry powder, dishwasher capsules, soaps & body wash).
- All unused items remain property of the home.

### Security & Responsibility

- Please look after your valuables and secure your accommodation. Despite protective measures we are unable to assume responsibility for any loss or damage to your possessions in or around the property.
- The hosts/owners are not responsible for any accidents, injuries, or illness that occur on the premises or its facilities, including children.

### Check-In & Check-Out

| Check-In after | Check-Out before |
| :------------: | :--------------: |
|      3pm       |       10am       |

Multiple parties rely on these times for cleaning and maintenance. If you need different times, contact the host and we'll do our best to accommodate.

**When checking out:**

- Ensure each room appears as it did when you arrived.
- Place all rubbish and recycling in the bins provided.
- Load all dirty dishes into the dishwasher.
- Turn off all lights and electrical items you've used (do not switch off WiFi or modems).
- Turn off the air conditioner and any heating.
- Leave bed linens in place and towels in their original rooms.
- Lock all windows and doors.
- Leave the key on the kitchen bench.

### Access

- The front door will be unlocked when you arrive. A key is left on the kitchen bench — please use it to lock the home whenever you go out and again at checkout.
- Private entrance to the property.

### Guests

- Maximum total number of guests: 6.
- The lead guest must be 25 years old or older.
- All overnight guests must be registered with the host beforehand.
- Guests are responsible for the actions of any visitors during their stay.

### Visitors & Events / Parties

- Strictly registered guests only. No unregistered visitors permitted.
- Parties are NOT allowed.
- No confetti, glitter, or excessive rubbish — additional cleaning fees may apply.

### Quiet Hours & Outdoor Spaces

To be good neighbours in this quiet residential area:

- Outdoor areas available for your enjoyment between 7am and 10pm.
- Please keep noise levels considerate between 10pm and 7am.
- No music or amplified sound outside between 8pm and 8am.
- After 8pm, please continue activities indoors with windows and doors closed.

District Plan noise limits:

- Daytime (8am – 8pm): 50 dBA LAeq (15 min)
- Nighttime (8pm – 8am): 40 dBA LAeq

### Parking

- Free off-street parking for up to 3 vehicles on the driveway.

### Rubbish & Recycling

- Deposit all rubbish and recycling in the bins provided.
- Check the QLDC website for collection days, or look at which bins the neighbours have out on collection day.
- Bins go on the curb beside the driveway by 7am on collection morning (or the night before), and back in the same evening.

### Littering

- NO littering — including leaving food outside for long periods.
- Excessive mess requiring additional cleaning may incur an additional fee.

### Commercial Photography

- Not permitted without written host permission.

### Pets

- Pets are NOT permitted. If you bring a pet, you may be asked to leave without a refund.

### Smoking, Vaping, & E-Cigarettes

- Smoking or vaping is NOT permitted inside the property.
- A $250+ fee may apply for smoking inside or improper disposal of cigarette butts.

### Security Cameras & Other Equipment

- External security cameras face the street.
- A noise monitor is located in the main living area.
- Tampering with any security, safety, or utility devices will result in immediate termination of your booking without a refund.

### Child Safety

- A baby cot, high chair, baby bath, and children's dinnerware are available on request — please let us know in advance.
- Guests are fully responsible for the safety and security of their children at all times.

### Damages / Breakages & Furniture

- Please respect our home as if it were your own.
- Use appliances, equipment, and furniture only for their intended purposes.
- Removing items from the property is not permitted; you will be charged accordingly.
- Should any damage occur, please notify the host immediately.

### Acceptance of House Rules

- By accepting this reservation, you agree these House Rules are understood and accepted, and all guests assume the risk of any harm arising from their use of the premises.
