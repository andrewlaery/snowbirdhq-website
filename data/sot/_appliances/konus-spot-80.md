### KONUS Telescope (KONUSSPOT-80) with SLIK Tripod

Spotting scope/telescope with SLIK tripod mount — for stargazing and mountain observation.

#### Setting Up
1. **Attach the telescope to the tripod mount** — lock the quick-release.
2. **Secure all connections** before adding weight.
3. **Adjust tripod height** and use the bubble level.
4. **Focus the eyepiece** for clear viewing — start at low magnification.

Best on clear nights with little moon — the Southern Alps offer some of NZ's darkest skies.

Please **return to its case** in the cupboard after use.
