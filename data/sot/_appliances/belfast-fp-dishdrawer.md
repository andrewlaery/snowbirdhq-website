### Fisher & Paykel DishDrawer (DS603/DD603)

**Dishwasher drawer** system — kitchen, under the bench.

- **Loading**: load dishes in either drawer (or both).
- Add F&P-recommended dishwasher detergent.
- Select wash program: **Normal** for everyday, **Quick** for lightly soiled, **Eco** for energy-efficient.
- Press **Start**.
- **F1 error** indicates a leak detection — drain the drip tray + open both drawers slightly to dry; restart.
