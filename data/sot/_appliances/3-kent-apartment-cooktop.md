### Blanco Induction Cooktop
Touch-control induction cooktop.

- **Isolation switch** is on the wall to the **left of the hob**, above the GPO — a small white square switch with a cooktop image identifies it.

#### Operating
1. Press the **On/Off symbol (bottom-left)** for at least **1 second**. A buzzer confirms power.
2. All zones display "0" with flashing decimal points (1 sec on, 1 sec off).
3. **If no operation within 10 sec**, the display turns off and the cooktop returns to standby.
4. **Select a zone** by tapping its position.
5. Set heat level **1–9** using the +/− buttons.
6. To turn off: hold the **On/Off symbol for 2+ seconds**.

#### Notes
- Buttons must be pressed within **3 sec** of selecting a zone, or the selection is cancelled.
- Each button press is confirmed with a tone.
- Residual-heat indicators show on each zone display when surfaces are still hot.
- Use **flat-bottomed magnetic cookware** (induction-compatible).
