### Studio Wood Burner

#### Lighting the fire

1. Lift the handle up to open the fire door.
2. Light with the **fire-lighters provided in the wood box**.
3. Leave the door slightly open while the fire establishes and the glass warms (avoids condensation build-up).
4. Close the door once the fire is established and lock it back into place.
5. Open the door only by touching the **wooden knob** on the front.

#### Fuel + ash

- Use the supplied baskets to collect firewood / kindling stored in the garage.
- Keep the door **closed** once the fire is established (open only briefly for re-loading) — over-firing damages the appliance.
- **Allow ash to cool** thoroughly before clearing.
- If excess ash builds up, contact the hosts.

> Safety policy is in **House Rules → Studio Wood Burner** (attended use, ash disposal, child supervision, fuel restrictions).
