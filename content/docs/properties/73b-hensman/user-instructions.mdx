# User Instructions

### Internet / WiFi

The internet WiFi can be accessed by:
* **Password:** provided on the welcome card at check-in

### Access & Security

* **Entry:** Lockbox beside the front door. Code provided in your booking information.
* **Owner on site:** Pete lives directly behind the lodge and is very accessible for any issues — please feel welcome to reach out through the booking platform first and we can connect you.

### Layout

* **Lower level:** Living area, kitchen, dining, one toilet.
* **Upper level:** All 5 bedrooms, plus en-suites and the shared toilet and bathroom servicing the 3 back bedrooms.

### Bed Configuration

* **5 bedrooms** total, all on the upper level:
    * **2 master bedrooms** (permanent king beds, each with en-suite). Both have mountain and lake views.
    * **3 additional bedrooms** across the back: each configurable as **1 king** (zip-together) OR **2 singles**.
* **Please confirm your preferred configuration at booking** — linen is ordered in advance.
* **Porta cot, high chair, stroller/pram, and baby backpack carrier** all available on request.

### Bathrooms

* **4 toilets total**: 1 on the living level, 1 in each of the 2 en-suite master bathrooms, 1 standalone servicing the 3 back bedrooms.
* **2 baths** and **3 showers** (extendable hoses, not fixed rain shower type).
* **1 hair dryer** — check the drawers or laundry shelf.

### Heating

* **Underfloor heating:** Programmed to come on in the evenings and mornings.
* **Gas fireplace:** Heats immediately, with a 1-hour timer. **If the fireplace won't start**, two things to check:
    1. **Power switch** on the left side wall, down low — sometimes accidentally turned off. Make sure it's ON.
    2. **Batteries in the controller** — they occasionally die. Spare batteries can be requested from the host.
* **No bedroom-specific heaters** — underfloor heating and the gas fireplace are the primary heating sources for the lodge.

### Hot Water

* The house has a **300 L electric hot water cylinder** which heats overnight.
* **With 10 guests, hot water can run out** if baths are taken or multiple showers run simultaneously. **Recommend showers over baths, and stagger usage.** If the water runs cool, it will reheat overnight.
* **The outdoor spa has its own separate heating system** and is always hot, even when the house hot water is empty.

### Kitchen

* **Gas cooktop** (4 hobs) powered by gas bottles. On longer stays, gas bottles can run low — please let us know if you're running out.
* **Dishwasher.**
* **No coffee machine** — a coffee plunger is provided.
* **Pots, pans, cutlery and dishes for 10 guests.**
* **No food stocks provided** — please bring your own.
* **Iron and ironing board** available.
* **Tap water is safe to drink.**

### Outdoor Spa / Hot Tub

* Outdoor spa with mountain views.
* Chlorinated fresh water with its own separate heating system — always hot.
* Please use considerately — sound carries to neighbours, especially at night.

### Outdoor Fire Pit

* Firewood is stacked and available for guest use.
* **Axe** for chopping kindling is on top of the garage fridge.
* **Matches** in a drawer inside the house.
* Please do not leave the fire unattended and ensure it is extinguished before heading inside.

### BBQ

* BBQ available for outdoor cooking.
* Spare gas bottles are in the garage area.

### Laundry

* Washing machine and dryer in the property.
* **Washing machine taps:** Need to be turned on.
* **Known issue:** The laundry power point is faulty and trips under combined load. **Do NOT run the washer and dryer simultaneously** — this trips the circuit. Use the extension cord provided to the power point on the opposite side of the laundry if needed. If the circuit trips, there's a red reset button on the plug.
* Washing powder provided (may run low on longer stays — let us know and we'll top it up).
* Additional soaps and supplies are in the big box in the cupboard by the main entrance.

### Living Area

* **TV with Chromecast capability** — cast from your phone/laptop.

### Other Amenities

* **Bicycles** available in the garage for guest use.
* **Spare foam mattress** available for occasional extra guest.

### Utility Infrastructure

* Check the Aurora Energy Queenstown outage page for area-wide power information during any outage.

### Getting Around / Transportation

* **Walking:** ~20 minutes downhill to Queenstown town centre; longer uphill coming back.
* **Taxi back from town:** ~$10.
* **Nearest supermarket:** FreshChoice, 3 minutes' drive.
* **Uber** available in Queenstown.
* **Closest ski field:** Coronet Peak (closest), The Remarkables (~15 min further).

### Check-out

* Return keys to the lockbox.
* **Leave the garage remote on the dining table.**
* Start the dishwasher.
* Dispose of rubbish and recycling in the RED/YELLOW/BLUE bins (see House Rules).
* Switch off lights and the gas fireplace.
* Leave bed linens in place and towels in their original rooms.
