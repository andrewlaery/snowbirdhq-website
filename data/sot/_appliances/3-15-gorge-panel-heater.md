### Panel Heater (Hallway)

A wall-mounted electric panel heater in the hallway.

- **Switch on the wall** beside the unit, plus an on/off / thermostat dial on the heater itself.
- **Slow to warm** — gentle radiant heat, takes 15–20 minutes for noticeable effect.
- **Switch off when checking out**.
