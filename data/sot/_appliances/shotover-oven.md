### Electric Oven

A standard electric oven.

- **The timer must be turned on for the oven to heat properly** — this is the most common source of confusion. If the oven isn't heating, check the timer first.
- Set the function (bake / fan / grill) and temperature, then turn the timer dial to the desired duration (or the auto-bake position) — the oven only powers the elements while the timer is active.
