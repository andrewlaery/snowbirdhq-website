### Braun Steam Iron (TextStyle 6)

Steam iron with multiple temperature settings — laundry room.

1. Fill water tank with **clean water**.
2. Set temperature according to fabric type (low for synthetics, medium for wool, high for cotton/linen).
3. Wait for the **ready indicator**.
4. Iron garments using appropriate technique.

- Use **steam burst** for stubborn wrinkles.
- **Vertical steam** for hanging garments.
- **Empty the water tank** after use to prevent scale build-up.
