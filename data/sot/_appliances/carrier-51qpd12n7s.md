### Carrier Portable Air-Conditioner (51QPD12N7S)

Located in the laundry / drying room.

#### Instructions

1. Press the wall switch — gives 2 hours of power per use.
2. Press the **Power** button on the unit.
3. Set the mode to **Dry** (raindrop symbol). In this mode you cannot select fan speed or temperature.
4. Leave doors and windows closed.

#### Drying ski / bike / hiking gear

- Hang wet weather clothing on a clothes rack or suitable hangers. Do not drape clothes directly over the air conditioner.
- Arrange clothes so air can circulate. Avoid overcrowding.
- Check periodically; turn off the unit once dry.

#### Safety + tips

- Do not block airflow — keep the air intake and outlet clear of clothing or other objects.
- Check the water drainage system isn't full or blocked.
- Refer to the manufacturer's manual for detailed operating instructions and safety warnings.
