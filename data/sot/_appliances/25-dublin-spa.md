### Spa Pool / Hot Tub

#### Cover + key

- The spa cover is **locked**. The **plastic key** is kept with the **garage remote**.
- Unlock the cover with the key, then **roll it back onto the rear cradle** — never sit on the cover.
- After use, **replace and re-lock** the cover (so the spa stays warm, clean, and child-safe).

#### Control panel

- Temperature is preset for comfort and safety.
- Press **JETS** to turn jets on/off.
- Press **LIGHTS** to turn underwater lights on/off.
- **If the panel appears locked** (padlock icon, unresponsive buttons): hold **OPTIONS + LIGHTS** together for 3–5 seconds.

> Safety policy is in **House Rules → Spa Pool / Hot Tub** (use at own risk, child supervision, no glass, chlorine-only, shower-before).
