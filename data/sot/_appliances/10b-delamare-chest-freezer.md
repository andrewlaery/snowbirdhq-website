### Chest Freezer (Garage)

A **chest freezer** in the garage provides additional frozen storage beyond the kitchen fridge/freezer.

- Useful for ski lunches, large grocery hauls, or pre-prepared meals.
- Lid latches firmly — push it shut after each use.
- Please **don't unplug** to defrost during your stay.
