# Welcome

### Dear Guest

Thank you for choosing to stay at 6A/643 Frankton Road. We trust your stay here will be more than enjoyable. For your convenience we have prepared a Guidebook including House Rules to assist you during your stay. Should you experience any problems or difficulties with regard to the home during your stay please do not hesitate to contact us via the booking platform. We are here to help!

### The Home & Neighbourhood

6A is a one-bedroom apartment in the Alpine Village complex on Frankton Road, with a private balcony overlooking Lake Wakatipu. The balcony view is the standout feature — long lake and mountain panoramas. The apartment is a short bus ride (or 45–60 minute walk along the road) to central Queenstown, with cafes, Cargo Brewery and a lakefront walking path nearby. Queenstown Airport is about 10 minutes' drive.

**A note on construction noise:** An adjacent apartment block is currently under construction (expected to complete late 2026). You'll experience significant daytime noise on weekdays (approx. 7:45am–6pm) including heavy machinery and piling rigs; limited weekend work. The construction site is also visible from the balcony and partially blocks one side of the lake view. We include this disclosure here because the construction is real and ongoing, and we'd rather you arrive prepared than disappointed. Earplugs are available from Life Pharmacy Wilkinsons in town if helpful.

# House Rules

### A Quick Note

- We're delighted to share our apartment with you in the Alpine Village complex
- To maintain our great relationship with our neighbours and comply with body corporate rules, we kindly ask that you follow these house guidelines
- Please remove your shoes inside to help keep our apartment clean and comfortable

### House Items

* Consumable items in the apartment are provided free of charge on a fair use basis (tea, coffee, cooking oil, soaps & body wash, etc).
* Unused items remain property of the home.
* **Welcome extras on the kitchen table:** complimentary coffee vouchers for the Boat Shed Cafe and Canyon beer vouchers — please enjoy.

### Security & Responsibility

* Please help us make your stay an enjoyable and memorable one. Please look after your valuables and secure your accommodation. Despite protective measures we are unable to assume any responsibility and will not be liable for any loss or damage to your possessions in or around the property.
* The hosts/owners are not responsible for any accidents, injuries, or illness that occur while on the premises or its facilities, as well as loss of personal belongings or valuables of the guest.
* **Note:** During your stay, the apartment alarm is not in operation.

### Check-In & Check-Out

* Check-In after: 3pm
* Check-Out before: 10am (**strict** — multiple parties rely on this time for cleaning and maintenance)

**Note:** Late checkout to 11am may be possible if no same-day booking — please ask the host.

**When checking out:**
* ensure the apartment appears as it was when you first arrived
* hand-wash any dishes (there is no dishwasher)
* place rubbish in the bins in the apartment complex
* turn off all lights and any electrical items you have used. Do not switch off utility items such as Wi-Fi or modems
* turn off heated towel rail and heat pump
* ensure the apartment is secured (close and lock all windows and doors)
* return the front door key to the lockbox

### Access

* **Location:** 6A is the first apartment at the top of the steps. Park in space 6A (below the steps), go up two flights of stairs, staying to the right. 6A is the first door at the top.
* **Front Door:** Lockbox beside the front door. Code provided in your booking information.
* **Parcel delivery:** If you need parcels sent to you during your stay, please use this address instead: Mountain Club, 85 Beach Street, Queenstown, 9300 (addressed to SnowbirdHQ).

### Guests

* The maximum total number of guests is 2.
* The guest responsible for registering the booking must be 25 years old or older.
* All guests staying must be registered with the host beforehand and only registered guests may spend the night.

### Visitors & events/parties

* Strictly registered guests only. No unregistered visitors are permitted.
* Parties are NOT allowed.
* No confetti, glitter, or excessive rubbish allowed otherwise an additional cleaning fee will be charged.

### Quiet Hours

This is an apartment complex with neighbours on all sides. To be a good neighbour:
- Please keep noise levels considerate between 10pm - 7am
- No music or amplified sound on the balcony between 8pm - 8am
- After 8pm, please continue activities indoors with the balcony door closed

Note: Under the District Plan, noise limits are:
- Daytime (8am - 8pm): 50dBA LAeq (15 min)
- Nighttime (8pm - 8am): 40dBA LAeq

### Parking

- **Dedicated parking space 6A** is reserved for this apartment — free, below the steps.
- If someone has parked in 6A when you arrive, please let us know and use any empty spot temporarily.
- Town centre parking: free lakefront parking (limited availability), paid on Camp Street and Man Street, some free spots on Man Street (check blue signs for time restrictions).
- Overflow/day parking: Goldfield Heights has unrestricted street parking.

### Body Corporate Rules

- **No laundry hung on the balcony** (body corporate rule). Please use the drying rack indoors, or the shared dryer in a separate building in the complex.

### Rubbish & Recycling

* Rubbish bins are located in the apartment complex — see the black booklet on the kitchen table for the exact location.
* Green rubbish bags are provided.
* **The apartment complex no longer has recycling facilities** — general waste only. We'd love to recycle here too but it's outside our control.

### Littering

* NO littering. This includes leaving food outside for long periods.
* If any part of the apartment or balcony is found to be littered requiring additional cleaning, an additional cleaning charge may apply.

### Commercial photography

* Commercial photography is not allowed unless guests have written permission from the host beforehand. Please contact the host for pricing.

### Pets

* Pets are NOT permitted. If you bring a pet, the host is not liable for any incident involving your pet and any other person or animal, and you will be asked to leave without a refund.

### Smoking, vaping, & e-cigarettes

* Smoking or vaping is NOT permitted inside or on the balcony (body corporate rule).
* There may be a $250+ additional fee added for smoking inside and/or improper disposal of cigarette butts.

### Security cameras & other equipment

* Tampering with any security, safety, or utility devices (e.g., smoke detectors, internet, connectivity, power) will result in the immediate termination of your booking without a refund.

### Damages/breakages & furniture

* Please respect our apartment as if it is your own.
* The sofa is suede — please take care to avoid spills and stains.
* Only use appliances, equipment, and furniture for their intended uses.
* Removing any items from the property is not permitted and you will be charged accordingly.
* Should any damage occur, please notify the owner immediately.

### Acceptance of House Rules

* By accepting this reservation, it is agreed that the House Rules are understood and accepted, and all guests are assuming the risk of any harm arising from their use of the premises or others whom they invite to use the premises.
