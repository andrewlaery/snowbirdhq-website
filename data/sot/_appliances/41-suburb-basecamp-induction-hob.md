### Bosch Induction Hob

A Bosch induction cooktop with touch controls.

- **Cookware must be ferrous** — a magnet should stick to the base. Aluminium / copper / glass pans won't work; if the heat indicator doesn't come on, switch to a steel pan.
- **Touch controls**: tap the power button, then select a zone, then use the slider / + / − to set heat. Hold the power button for ~2 seconds to switch the hob off.
- **Child lock**: hold the lock symbol for ~3 seconds to engage / disengage.
- **Auto-off**: if a zone runs at high heat for an unusually long time it will safety-shut-off.
