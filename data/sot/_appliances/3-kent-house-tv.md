### LG OLED 55" Smart TV (CXPVA) + Sky Box
55" LG OLED Smart TV in the lounge, with a Sky Box on HDMI 4.

#### Watching streaming apps
1. Press the **top-left red circle Power button** on the LG remote.
2. From the Home screen, **launch your desired streaming service** (Netflix, Disney+, Prime, etc.).
3. **Sign in with your own account** — please **sign out before checking out**.

#### Watching Sky TV
1. Turn on the **TV first** (LG remote → Power).
2. From the home screen, scroll down and select **HDMI 4** to switch to the Sky Box.
3. Turn on the **Sky Box** by pressing the **power button (top-right)** on the Sky remote.
4. Use the Sky remote to change channels.
