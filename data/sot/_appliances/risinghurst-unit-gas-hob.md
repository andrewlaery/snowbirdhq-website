### 4-Burner Gas Hob

A 4-burner gas cooktop.

- **Critical — power switch above the washing machine**: the gas hob (and the oven below it) are wired to a switch located **above the washing machine**. This switch must be **ON** before either appliance will ignite.
- **If the stove or oven won't light**: check that switch first. This is the most common source of "gas isn't working" reports at this property.
- **Hold the knob down to ignite** the burner — keep pressed for a few seconds after flame catches so the thermocouple stays open.
