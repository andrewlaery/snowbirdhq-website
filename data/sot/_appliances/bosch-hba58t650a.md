### Bosch Oven (HBA58T650A)

[User manual](https://www.bosch-home.com.au/supportdetail/product/HBA58T650A/10)

Located beneath the microwave/oven combo.

#### Starting

1. Press **Power** to switch on.
2. Set the operating mode using the rotary selector — we recommend **3D Hot Air**.
3. Use the Up/Down buttons to select the temperature setting, then adjust with the rotary selector.
4. Press **Start/Pause**.

#### During cooking

- Opening the door suspends operation; it resumes when the door is closed.
- Press the n button briefly to pause; press again to continue.

#### After use

- Press **Power** to switch off.
