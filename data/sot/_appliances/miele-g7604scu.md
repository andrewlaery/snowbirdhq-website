### Miele Dishwasher (G 7604 SCU)

[User manual (PDF)](https://www.miele.com.au/e/built-under-dishwasher-g-7604-scu-clst-stainless-steel-cleansteel-12121370-p)

A semi-integrated built-under dishwasher with a custom door panel — the operating controls are visible along the top edge.

- **Open the door**: pull the recessed handle along the top of the appliance. The door drops to its open position.
- **Load**: cutlery tray on the very top (slide tabs to remove if you need extra height for tall items below); upper basket holds glasses + bowls; lower basket holds plates + pots. Spray arms are above and below — make sure nothing blocks them.
- **Detergent + rinse aid**: tablet or powder goes in the dispenser inside the door. Rinse-aid reservoir is alongside (top-up indicator lamp on the front control panel will alert when low).
- **Salt**: refill via the cap in the floor of the lower basket when the salt indicator lights up. Use Miele or generic dishwasher salt — not table salt.
- **Programs**: select via the buttons on the top control strip:
  - **Auto** — sensor-controlled; adjusts temperature + duration to soil load. Use this for most washes.
  - **Eco** — lowest energy + water; longer cycle (~3 h).
  - **Quick** — ~30-minute light wash for lightly-soiled items.
  - **Intensive** — for heavily-soiled pots + roasting tins.
- **Start**: press **Start/Stop**. The display shows estimated time remaining.

**Don't pre-rinse heavily.** The Auto sensor reads soil load from the wash water — pre-rinsing tells it the load is "clean" and shortens the wash. Scrape plates, but skip the rinse.
