### Weber Q3200 BBQ

Located in the garden area. Connected to **mains gas** (no bottle to swap).

#### Lighting

1. **Open the lid** before igniting — prevents gas build-up.
2. Turn one burner control knob to the **start / high** position.
3. Push the ignition button. If it doesn't catch on first try, turn the gas off, wait 5 minutes, and try again.
4. Close the lid once lit.
5. Preheat for 10–15 minutes for optimal cooking temperature.
6. Use the control knobs to adjust flame and temperature — the Q3200 has two burners for heat zoning.

#### After use

1. Turn **all** burner control knobs OFF.
2. Allow the grill plate to cool before brushing/cleaning — use the supplied wire brush.
3. Close the lid.

> Safety policy is in **House Rules → Outdoor BBQ** (location restrictions, attended use, child supervision).
