# Welcome

### Dear Guest

Thank you for choosing to stay at Sunny Shotover Suite. We trust your stay here will be more than enjoyable. For your convenience we have prepared a Guidebook including House Rules to assist you during your stay. Should you experience any problems or difficulties with regard to the home during your stay please do not hesitate to contact us via the booking platform. We are here to help!

### The Home & Neighbourhood

Sunny Shotover Suite is a modern one-bedroom unit in Lower Shotover, separate from the main house at 100 Risinghurst Terrace. A sunny aspect with mountain views, well-equipped kitchen, a smart TV with complimentary Netflix, and two bikes with helmets available for exploring. Central Queenstown is a short drive, and Arrowtown is about 10 minutes by car — perfect for couples looking to mix town and countryside.

Your hosts, Paddy & Bethan, live in the main house next door with their children and are very accessible if anything comes up.

**Important — two properties, one address:** The main house at 100 Risinghurst (listed separately) may have its own guests during your stay. Please use ONLY the unit's lockbox — there is a second lockbox for the main house and guests have occasionally gone to the wrong one.

# House Rules

### A Quick Note

- We're delighted to share our unit with you in this peaceful neighbourhood
- To be good neighbours, please keep noise to a minimum especially outdoors in the evenings
- Please remove your shoes at the door

### House Items

* Consumable items in the unit are provided free of charge on a fair use basis (tea, instant coffee, eco laundry detergent, body wash, etc).
* Unused items remain property of the home.

### Security & Responsibility

* Please help us make your stay an enjoyable and memorable one. Please look after your valuables and secure your accommodation.
* The hosts/owners are not responsible for any accidents, injuries, or illness that occur while on the premises.

### Check-In & Check-Out

* Check-In after: 3pm
* Check-Out before: 10am

**Note:** If you're arriving late in the evening, let us know and we can leave the outdoor lights and the heating on for you.

**When checking out:**
* return the unit to its original condition
* start the dishwasher
* dispose of rubbish in the appropriate bins
* leave used towels on the shower floor or in the black container
* lock all doors and windows
* return the key to the lockbox

### Access

* **Finding the property:** Follow Google or Apple Maps to the street. Look for postbox #100 and come up the driveway.
* **Lockbox:** Walk between the flat and the shed. The lockbox is by the outdoor sink.
* **IMPORTANT:** There is a SECOND lockbox for the main house. Make sure you go to the correct one for the UNIT — not the main house door.
* Check-in instructions are sent 3 days before arrival via the booking platform.

### Guests

* The maximum total number of guests is **2 — strictly.** The couch is not suitable for sleeping; no extra guests.
* The guest responsible for registering the booking must be 25 years old or older.

### Bed Configuration

* 1 bedroom with a king bed (medium firmness mattress).
* Towels placed on the bed; a second set in the wardrobe.
* **Travel cot and highchair** available on request.

### Visitors & events/parties

* Strictly registered guests only.
* Parties are NOT allowed.
* No confetti, glitter, or excessive rubbish — otherwise an additional cleaning fee will be charged.

### Quiet Hours

This is a residential family neighbourhood. Please:
- Keep noise to a minimum, especially outdoors in the evening
- Quiet hours 10pm - 7am

### Parking

* **One off-street parking space** in the driveway.
* Park to the right of the main house by the air conditioning unit.
* Do NOT block shed access.
* One vehicle only.

### Rubbish & Recycling

* **Blue-lid recycling bin** by the gate.
* General waste bin also available.
* Bin bags in the kitchen.

### Littering

* NO littering.

### Commercial photography

* Commercial photography is not allowed unless guests have written permission from the host beforehand.

### Pets

* Pets are NOT permitted.

### Smoking, vaping, & e-cigarettes

* Smoking or vaping is NOT permitted inside the unit.
* There may be a $250+ additional fee added for smoking inside and/or improper disposal of cigarette butts.

### Security cameras & other equipment

* Tampering with any security, safety, or utility devices will result in the immediate termination of your booking without a refund.

### Damages/breakages & furniture

* Please respect our unit as if it is your own.
* Only use appliances, equipment, and furniture for their intended uses.

### Acceptance of House Rules

* By accepting this reservation, it is agreed that the House Rules are understood and accepted, and all guests are assuming the risk of any harm arising from their use of the premises.
