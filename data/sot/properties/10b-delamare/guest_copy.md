# House Rules

### House Items

- Consumable items are provided free of charge on a fair-use basis (cooking basics, tea, coffee, soaps).
- Black house manual on the kitchen table — Wi-Fi info, appliance instructions, property details.

### Security & Responsibility

- Please look after your valuables and secure your accommodation. Hosts are not liable for loss or damage.

### Check-In & Check-Out

- Check-in after 3pm. Check-out before 10am.
- Self check-in via lockbox on the wooden fence on the left-hand side of the house.

### Access

- Walk up the steps to the LEFT of the house. Lockbox code 1010.
- Lockbox contains the front door key AND a garage remote fob.
- Boiler cupboard is first on the left as you come in the front door — has extra cleaning products, trash bags, and supplies.

### Quiet Hours

- Please keep noise levels considerate between 10pm and 7am.

### Parking

- Single garage with remote-controlled door. Garage fits small/medium cars only — 7-seaters and SUVs may NOT fit.
- Larger vehicles — most guests park in front of the garage doors.
- Street parking also available.
- Driveway is steep — can be icy in winter (de-icing salt is in a large white bag inside the garage).
- Garage door swings outward — clear the area before opening.

### Smoking, Vaping, & E-Cigarettes

- Smoking or vaping is NOT permitted inside the home.

### Pets

- Pets are NOT permitted.

### Damages

- Please respect our home as if it were your own. Notify the host immediately of any damage.
