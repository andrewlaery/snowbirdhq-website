# Welcome

### Dear Guest

Thank you for choosing to stay at 100 Risinghurst Terrace. We trust your stay here will be more than enjoyable. For your convenience we have prepared a Guidebook including House Rules to assist you during your stay. Should you experience any problems or difficulties with regard to the home during your stay please do not hesitate to contact us via the booking platform. We are here to help!

### The Home & Neighbourhood

100 Risinghurst Terrace (the main home) is a spacious three-bedroom family home in Lower Shotover — a peaceful mountain-view setting a short drive to central Queenstown and ~10 minutes to Arrowtown. The home has a lounge with heat pump, a second lounge with a gas fire, a full kitchen with dishwasher, a garage accessible from inside the house, and a garden with a BBQ.

**Important — shared address:** There is a separate attached unit at the same address (100 Risinghurst Terrace — Unit, Sunny Shotover Suite) listed separately with its own guests from time to time. The driveway is shared. Please be considerate of neighbours and let us know if you hear from people you don't recognise.

# House Rules

### A Quick Note

- We're delighted to share our home with you in this quiet Lower Shotover neighbourhood
- To maintain our great relationship with our neighbours and comply with local regulations, we kindly ask that you follow these house guidelines
- **No shoes or ski gear indoors** — please remove your shoes at the door and store skis/snowboards in the garage or back garden

### House Items

* Consumable items in the house are provided free of charge on a fair use basis (tea, coffee, laundry powder, dishwasher capsules, soaps & body wash, etc).
* Unused items remain property of the home.

### Security & Responsibility

* Please help us make your stay an enjoyable and memorable one. Please look after your valuables and secure your accommodation.
* The hosts/owners are not responsible for any accidents, injuries, or illness that occur while on the premises or its facilities, as well as loss of personal belongings or valuables of the guest.

### Check-In & Check-Out

* Check-In after: 3pm
* Check-Out before: 10am

**When checking out:**
* ensure the house (each room) appears as it was when you first arrived
* dispose of all rubbish and recycling in the bins (RED/BLUE/YELLOW — see below)
* load the dishwasher with all dirty dishes
* turn off all lights and any electrical items you have used. Do not switch off utility items such as Wi-Fi or modems
* turn off heated towel rails, heat pump, gas fire, and the BBQ
* leave bed linens in place and towels in their original rooms
* ensure the house is secured (close and lock all windows and doors)
* return the front door key **and** the garage fob to the lockbox

### Access

* **Finding the property:** Look for postbox #100. Follow the driveway up and around to the left to the grey house straight ahead.
* **Lockbox:** On the right-hand side wall of the garage, above the bins. Code provided in your booking information.
* **Key:** Opens the front door (to the left of the garage).
* **Garage fob:** A separate key/fob for the garage — please keep it with the house key and return both at checkout.

### Guests

* The maximum total number of guests is 6.
* The guest responsible for registering the booking must be 25 years old or older.
* All guests staying must be registered with the host beforehand.

### Bed Configuration

* **3 bedrooms** — 1 king bed (master), 1 queen bed, and 1 bunk room with 2 single beds (max 6 guests).
* **Travel cot** available on request.
* **High chair** available on request.

### Visitors & events/parties

* Strictly registered guests only. No unregistered visitors are permitted.
* Parties are NOT allowed.
* No confetti, glitter, or excessive rubbish allowed otherwise an additional cleaning fee will be charged.

### Quiet Hours & Outdoor Spaces

- Outdoor areas available 7am–10pm. Quiet hours 10pm–7am.
- No music or amplified sound outside between 8pm - 8am
- After 8pm, please continue activities indoors with windows and doors closed

Note: Under the District Plan, noise limits are:
- Daytime (8am - 8pm): 50dBA LAeq (15 min)
- Nighttime (8pm - 8am): 40dBA LAeq

### Parking

- **Driveway:** 1 vehicle — please park in front of the front door/garage door on the left-hand side by the rose bush.
- **Important:** Leave room on the far right side of the driveway for the attached-unit guests.
- **Street parking** is available on the main street for additional vehicles.

### Rubbish & Recycling

* **RED bin:** General waste
* **BLUE bin:** Glass only
* **YELLOW bin:** Cardboard, tin, and hard plastic (no soft plastic)
* Bins are located on the right-hand side of the garage.

### Littering

* NO littering. If any part of the property is found to be littered requiring additional cleaning, an additional cleaning charge may apply.

### Commercial photography

* Commercial photography is not allowed unless guests have written permission from the host beforehand.

### Pets

* Pets are NOT permitted.

### Smoking, vaping, & e-cigarettes

* Smoking or vaping is NOT permitted inside the home.
* There may be a $250+ additional fee added for smoking inside and/or improper disposal of cigarette butts.

### Security cameras & other equipment

* Tampering with any security, safety, or utility devices will result in the immediate termination of your booking without a refund.

### Damages/breakages & furniture

* Please respect our home as if it is your own.
* Only use appliances, equipment, and furniture for their intended uses.
* Removing any items from the property is not permitted.

### Acceptance of House Rules

* By accepting this reservation, it is agreed that the House Rules are understood and accepted, and all guests are assuming the risk of any harm arising from their use of the premises or others whom they invite to use the premises.
