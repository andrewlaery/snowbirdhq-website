## Property Orientation

### Internet / Wi-Fi

- **Network:** SkyCrest.
- **Password:** provided on the welcome card at check-in.

### Drinking Water

- The **kitchen tap water is not filtered**. For drinking water, please use the **fridge's filtered water dispenser**.

### Cot / Travel Crib

- A **travel cot** is available on request. Please let the host know at least **24 hours before arrival** so we can set it up for you.

### Heating Guide

#### Main Floor (Kitchen, Living, Master Bedroom)

- SkyCrest sits in **New Zealand's Southern Alps**, where temperatures swing dramatically (30°C+ to near freezing).
- The house features a **heated concrete floor slab** that maintains a comfortable preset temperature throughout.
- The temperature is set for comfortable general use. **Use clothing layers to fine-tune your personal temperature.**

**For Best Comfort**

- Keep doors and windows closed during cold periods to retain the heat stored in the concrete slab.
- Adjust your clothing for individual temperature preferences.
- *Please contact your host if you need any temperature adjustments.*

**Gas Fire (Living Room)**

- **Not recommended as a primary heat source.**
- Use only as needed while you're in the living room.
- See **Appliances → Heating → Escea Gas Fireplace** below for operating instructions.

#### Top Floor (TV Nook, Bedrooms 2 & 3)

- **3 air-conditioning heat pumps (Daikin CTXM25RVMA)** — one for each top-floor space.
- **Remote controls** are mounted **on the walls** in each room.

#### When Leaving the House

- Close all doors and windows during cooler weather to maintain ideal temperature for your return.

### Sauna, Spa, Ice Bath — Operating Etiquette

This property has three substantial wellness amenities. Each has its own operating instructions in the Appliances section below — please read them before first use:

- **Harvia Barrel Sauna** — three switches must be on (isolation switch, light switch, timer dial). Turn on **30–45 min before use**.
- **HotSpring Spa** — always-on; remove cover completely before entering; replace firmly after use.
- **Alpine Ice Bath** — chiller is always running; sessions of 2–3 min for first-timers; check with your doctor if you have any health concerns.

### Lockbox + FOB Replacement

- **Lost FOB**: $300 replacement charge applies. Please return all FOBs to the lockbox at check-out.

### Utility Infrastructure

Check the [Aurora Energy Queenstown outage page](https://www.aurora.energy/outages) for area-wide power information during any outage.

### Check-out

- Return keys + FOBs to the lockbox.
- Start the F&P DishDrawer.
- Turn off the **Escea gas fireplace** (via the remote).
- Turn off the **Harvia sauna** (timer dial back to 0).
- Turn off all **Daikin heat pumps**.
- Switch off the **OJ Microline thermostats** in the bathrooms.
- Empty rubbish into the appropriate bins.
- Lock all doors and windows.
