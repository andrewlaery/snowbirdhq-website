### Tefal Steam Iron (FV2868)

[User manual (PDF)](https://dam.groupeseb.com/m/03f8daa9aa342ebf/original/1820004836-IFU.pdf)

Tefal FV28xx-series steam iron. Iron and ironing board located in the laundry.

#### Getting started

1. Fill the water tank with tap water (don't exceed maximum).
2. Plug in.
3. Set the temperature dial for your fabric.
4. Wait for the iron to heat up — the indicator light goes off when ready.

#### Tips

- For steam ironing, set the dial in the steam zone.
- Press and hold the steam-boost button for difficult wrinkles.
- Auto-shutoff: 8 min when standing, 30 sec when left on its soleplate.
- Avoid passing directly over zippers, buttons, metal accessories.
- Hold the iron upright when using vertical steam.

#### After use

1. Set temperature dial to minimum.
2. Unplug.
3. Empty remaining water.
4. Allow to cool completely before storing.

#### Maintenance

- Wipe soleplate with a damp cloth when cool.
- Descale ~once a year depending on use.
