# User Instructions

### Internet / WiFi

The internet WiFi can be accessed by:
* **Network:** Lakeview Upper
* **Password:** provided on the welcome card at check-in

### Access & Security

* **Entry:** Lockbox beside the front door. Code provided in your booking information.
* A full photo walkthrough is sent with your check-in instructions 24 hours before arrival.

### Bed Configuration

* The lodge accommodates up to 8 guests.
* **Travel cot and highchair** available on request — please let the host know in advance.

### Heating

* Heating available throughout the lodge. If you'd like guidance on any specific controls, please contact us via the booking platform.

### Kitchen

* Fully equipped kitchen.
* **Tap water is safe to drink.**

### Bathroom

* Towels, shampoo, body wash and a hair dryer provided.

### Laundry

* Washing facilities available — please let us know if you'd like any assistance.

### Outdoor

* The lodge has off-street parking and shared external areas with 73B (lower level).

### Utility Infrastructure

* Check the Aurora Energy Queenstown outage page for area-wide power information during any outage.

### Getting Around / Transportation

* **Walking:** ~20 minutes downhill to Queenstown town centre; longer uphill.
* **Taxi back from town:** ~$10.
* **Uber** available in Queenstown.
* **Queenstown Airport:** ~15 minutes' drive.
* **Closest ski field:** Coronet Peak (closest); The Remarkables (~15 min further).

### Nearby Shops

* **FreshChoice** — 3 minutes' drive.
* **Pak'n'Save** (Frankton): best prices, a short drive.
* **New World** (Frankton): Remarkables Park.
* **Countdown** (Frankton): Five Mile shopping centre.

### Check-out

* Return the key to the lockbox.
* Dispose of rubbish in the bins at the property.
* Switch off lights and heating.
* Leave bed linens in place and towels in their original rooms.
* Lock all windows and doors.

### A note on Lakeview Lodge Upper

Some specific operational details (appliance locations, fuse box, mains water shutoff) are being finalised. If you need anything specific during your stay — appliance instructions, additional supplies, or any other help — please message us via the booking platform and we'll respond quickly.
