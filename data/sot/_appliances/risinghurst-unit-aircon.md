### Heating + Air Conditioning (Reverse-Cycle)

A reverse-cycle heat pump / aircon — heats and cools.

- **Remote**: kept beside the unit or on the dining table.
- **Modes**: Heat / Cool / Dry / Auto / Fan.
- **The host pre-heats the unit** for winter and late arrivals — you should walk in to a warm room.
- **Sunny-day tip**: leave the blinds open during the day — the sun warms the flat through the sliding doors and the unit doesn't need to work hard.
