# Welcome

### Dear Guest

Thank you for choosing to stay at BaseCamp. We trust your stay here will be more than enjoyable. For your convenience we have prepared a Guidebook including House Rules to assist you during your stay. Should you experience any problems or difficulties with regard to the home during your stay please do not hesitate to contact us via the booking platform. We are here to help!

### The Home & Neighbourhood

BaseCamp is a three-bedroom elevated home at 41 Suburb Street with views across Lake Wakatipu and The Remarkables. The home sits in a quiet residential pocket close to the town centre, with a private entrance, garden, and outdoor dining area. Walking access to central Queenstown, with Pak'n'Save and the Frankton amenities a short drive away.

# House Rules

### A Quick Note

- We're delighted to share our home with you in this peaceful residential neighbourhood
- To maintain our great relationship with our neighbours and comply with local regulations, we kindly ask that you follow these house guidelines
- Please remove your shoes inside to help keep our home clean and comfortable

### House Items

* Consumable items in the house are provided free of charge on a fair use basis (cooking basics, tea, coffee, laundry powder, dishwasher capsules/powder, soaps & body wash, etc).
* Unused items remain property of the home.

### Security & Responsibility

* Please help us make your stay an enjoyable and memorable one. Please look after your valuables and secure your accommodation. Despite protective measures we are unable to assume any responsibility and will not be liable for any loss or damage to your possessions in or around the property.
* The hosts/owners are not responsible for any accidents, injuries, or illness that occur while on the premises or its facilities, as well as loss of personal belongings or valuables of the guest. This includes adults and children.

### Check-In & Check-Out

* Check-In after: 3pm
* Check-Out before: 10am

**When checking out:**
* ensure the house (each room) appears as it was when you first arrived
* dispose of all rubbish and recycling in the bins provided
* load the dishwasher with all dirty dishes
* turn off all lights and any electrical items you have used. Do not switch off utility items such as Wi-Fi or modems
* turn off heating and any fireplace
* leave bed linens in place and towels in their original rooms
* ensure the house is secured (close and lock all windows and doors)

### Access

* **Smart lock** on the front door. Your personal access code is sent via the booking platform approximately 2 days before arrival.
* Private entrance to the property.

### Guests

* The maximum total number of guests is 6.
* The guest responsible for registering the booking must be 25 years old or older.
* All guests staying must be registered with the host beforehand and only registered guests may spend the night.
* Guests are responsible for the actions of any visitors during their stay.

### Visitors & events/parties

* Strictly registered guests only. No unregistered visitors are permitted.
* Parties are NOT allowed.
* No confetti, glitter, or excessive rubbish allowed otherwise an additional cleaning fee will be charged.

### Quiet Hours & Outdoor Spaces

To be good neighbours in this quiet residential area:
- Outdoor areas available for your enjoyment between 7am - 10pm
- Please keep noise levels considerate between 10pm - 7am
- No music or amplified sound outside between 8pm - 8am
- After 8pm, please continue activities indoors with windows and doors closed

Note: Under the District Plan, noise limits are:
- Daytime (8am - 8pm): 50dBA LAeq (15 min)
- Nighttime (8pm - 8am): 40dBA LAeq

### Parking

- **Free off-street parking** is available at the property.

### Rubbish & Recycling

* Please deposit all rubbish and recycling in the bins provided.
* Check the QLDC website for collection days, or look at which bins the neighbours have out on collection day.
* Bins need to be placed on the curb by 7am on collection morning (or the night before), and collected back in the same evening.

### Littering

* NO littering. This includes leaving food outside for long periods.
* If any part of the property is found to be littered requiring additional cleaning, an additional cleaning charge may apply.

### Commercial photography

* Commercial photography is not allowed unless guests have written permission from the host beforehand.

### Pets

* Pets are NOT permitted. If you bring a pet, the host is not liable for any incident, and you will be asked to leave without a refund.

### Smoking, vaping, & e-cigarettes

* Smoking or vaping is NOT permitted inside the property.
* There may be a $250+ additional fee added for smoking inside and/or improper disposal of cigarette butts.

### Security cameras & other equipment

* Tampering with any security, safety, or utility devices will result in the immediate termination of your booking without a refund.

### Child safety

* A baby cot, high chair, baby bath, and children's dinnerware are available on request — please let us know in advance.
* Guests are fully responsible for the safety and security of their children at all times.

### Damages/breakages & furniture

* Please respect our home as if it is your own.
* Only use appliances, equipment, and furniture for their intended uses.
* Removing any items from the property is not permitted and you will be charged accordingly.
* Should any damage occur, please notify the owner immediately.

### Acceptance of House Rules

* By accepting this reservation, it is agreed that the House Rules are understood and accepted, and all guests are assuming the risk of any harm arising from their use of the premises or others whom they invite to use the premises.
