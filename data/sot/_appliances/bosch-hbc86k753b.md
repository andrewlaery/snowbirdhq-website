### Bosch Microwave / Oven (HBC86K753B)

[User manual](https://www.bosch-home.co.nz/supportdetail/product/HBC86K753B/09)

#### Starting the microwave

1. Press **Power** to turn on.
2. Select the microwave power setting:
   - 90 W — defrosting delicate foods
   - 180 W — defrosting + continued cooking
   - 360 W — cooking meat / heating delicate foods
   - 600 W — heating + cooking food
   - 900 W — heating liquids
3. Set the cooking time using the rotary selector.
4. Press **Start/Pause** to begin.

#### During cooking

- Press **Start/Pause** to pause; press again to resume.
- Opening the door suspends operation. Close the door and press Start/Pause to resume.

#### Safety

- Do not heat liquids or food in tightly sealed containers — they may explode.
- Use microwave-safe cookware (glass, glass ceramic, porcelain, ceramic, heat-resistant plastic). Avoid metal cookware.
- Keep at least 2 cm distance between metal objects (like a spoon in a glass) and the oven walls or door to prevent sparks.
- After cooking, allow the food to stand 2–5 minutes for even temperature distribution.

#### After use

- A signal sounds at the end of cooking; the time shows 00:00 min:sec.
- Press **0** to cancel the signal if needed.
- Press **Power** to switch off.
