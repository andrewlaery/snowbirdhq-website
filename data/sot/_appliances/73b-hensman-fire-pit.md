### Outdoor Fire Pit

Outdoor fire pit in the garden — for evening fires only.

- **Firewood** is stacked and available for guest use.
- **Axe** for chopping kindling sits on top of the **garage fridge**.
- **Matches** in a drawer inside the house.
- **Never leave the fire unattended.**
- **Fully extinguish** with water before heading inside — embers can rekindle.
