## Property Orientation

### Internet / Wi-Fi

The Wi-Fi network and password are shown above in the Quick Reference. The Wi-Fi modem is on the desk in the second lounge room.

### Layout

- Main living level with lounge (heat pump), second lounge (gas fire), full kitchen, dining.
- 3 bedrooms + bathrooms.
- Garage accessible from inside the house.

### Bed Configuration

- **3 bedrooms** — 1 king bed (master), 1 queen bed, 1 bunk room with 2 single beds (max 6 guests).
- **Travel cot and highchair** available on request.

### Heating

The home has multiple heating options — see Appliances → Heating for operating details:

- **Mitsubishi Electric heat pump** in the main lounge.
- **Rinnai gas fire** in the second lounge.
- **Portable electric heaters** in each bedroom wardrobe.
- **Heated towel rails** in the bathrooms — please turn off at checkout.

### Hot Water

- **400 L hot water tank** — provides approximately 7 showers per full tank.
- **Reheat time:** approximately 1 hour — stagger showers if possible.

### Tap Water

Tap water is safe to drink throughout the home.

### Garage

- Accessible from inside the house via the internal door.
- **Garage door:** Wall switch inside the garage. If the door reopens after closing, press the button again — it can occasionally play up.
- Contains laundry, cleaning supplies, and space for **ski equipment storage**.
- **Large bins** provided near the entrance for drying ski boots indoors.

### Outdoor

- BBQ available in the garden (see Appliances → Outdoor).
- Garden area available 7am–10pm.

### Cleaning Supplies

Cleaning supplies are under the garage sink (orange tub — includes drain cleaner).

### Utility Infrastructure

Check the [Aurora Energy Queenstown outage page](https://www.aurora.energy/outages) for area-wide power information during any outage.

### Getting Around

- **Bus:** Number 5 bus from Jones Avenue (hourly, 6:05am–10:05pm). Use the Transit app for live timetables.
- **Uber:** Available in Queenstown.
- **Taxis:** QT Taxis 03 450 3000.
- **Queenstown town centre:** short drive.
- **Arrowtown:** ~10 minutes by car.

### Nearby Shops

- **Night n Day Kawarau** — 23 Eleventh Avenue, ~5 min walk (convenience store, open late).
- **Pak'n'Save / Countdown** — Frankton, ~5 min drive.
- **Four Square** — town centre.

### Nearby Restaurants

- **11th Avenue by Franks** — ~5 min walk (03 222 7030).
- **The Hayes** — Lake Hayes Estate, ~15 min walk (03 441 4074).

### Walking & Activities

**Twin Rivers Trail** — 2–3 hour loop walk over the Shotover Bridge, accessible from the local area.

### Mid-stay Clean

Available at additional cost on request — please contact the host to arrange.

### Check-out

- Return keys (house key AND garage fob) to the lockbox.
- Load the dishwasher.
- Dispose of rubbish in the RED/BLUE/YELLOW bins.
- Switch off lights, heated towel rails, gas fire and BBQ.
- Leave bed linens in place and towels in their original rooms.
