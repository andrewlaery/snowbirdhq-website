### Top-Loader Washing Machine

A top-loading washing machine in the bathroom.

- **Washing powder** is provided.
- **Loading**: lift the lid, place clothes in the drum, add powder to the dispenser.
- **Programs**: select via the front dial — Normal / Quick / Delicate. Press start.
- **Drying rack** is provided — set up near the heat pump (turn it on) for faster drying.
- **No dryer in the apartment** — see the shared dryer in the complex's drying room (separate appliance entry).
- **No laundry on the balcony** (body corporate rule).
