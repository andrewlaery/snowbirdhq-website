## Description

Relax in Style: Central 2-Bedroom apartment

Step into modern comfort in the heart of Queenstown. This newly renovated 2-bedroom, 1-bathroom apartment on Shotover Street offers stylish living on the main street of Queenstown CBD. Perfect for those seeking convenience with a touch of luxury in the vibrant heart of QT. Please note as this is in Queenstown city centre & opposite a pub some noise is to be expected from time to time.

Bedrooms: 2
Two bedrooms upstairs.
A cot for infants can be supplied on request.

Designed to provide restful nights for families and groups of friends.

Bathrooms: 1
One bathroom downstairs next to the living & kitchen area.  Equipped with towels, shampoo, conditioner, hand/body wash and toilet paper.

Kitchen and living area:
Open plan kitchen and lounge area with incredible views over Queenstown CBD & the gondola. The lounge area has a comfy sofa, TV and heat pump to keep you toasty.

The kitchen is fully equipped to self cater throughout your stay. We provide basics to get you started; oil, salt, pepper, tea, coffee, sugar and cleaning supplies.

Parking:
Off site parking is available. This is free of charge. As this in the city centre you will need to park in a loading bay & unload luggage then park at an underground parking spot just across the road. Please note the apartment is on the second floor, a set of external steps must be climbed from the carpark. Note the carparks has a restriction of 2.2M in height. Not suitable for camper vans.

Laundry:
A washing machine is available for use throughout your stay.

Surroundings:
Amazing location!!

The apartments are on the main street of Queenstown CBD. Location could not be better!!  The airport is 20minutes drive from the apartment.

Public transport:
Ubers and taxis are readily available if you want to explore the surrounds.

Easily accessible bus route to Frankton/Airport for those who choose not to rent a vehicle, just a few minutes stroll from the apartment.

Full access to the apartment, you will share a set of external steps with the apartment next door. We ask that you carefully read check in instructions to locate the property as it is down an alleyway.

I'll be available via the app for anything needed.

Car parking is NOT on site. It is in an underground carpark across the road. You can park in the loading zone below the apartment for 10 minutes (any longer the council may give you a parking ticket) to drop luggage off. External steps must be climbed to access. As the apartment is in the centre of the city some noise is to be expected. We are opposite an awesome pub (Beech Tree) this shuts down before 11 every evening. Please ensure this meets all guest needs.

Check-Out: Before 10am

- Leave the property in the same condition as when you arrived
- Place all rubbish and recycling in appropriate bins/bags provided
