### Shark TurboBlade Tower Fans (TF202S) x3 — Bedrooms

Bladeless tower fans with **180° oscillation**, **10 speed settings**, twist vents for directional airflow. **Quiet enough for sleep.**

- **Locations**: one in each bedroom (Master, Bedroom 2, Bedroom 3).
- **Controls**: remote control on the bedside table.

#### Standard Use
1. Press **Power** on the remote.
2. Cycle speed with the **speed button** — 10 levels; lowest setting is **whisper-quiet for overnight**.
3. Press **oscillation** to sweep 180° across the room.
4. **Twist the front vents** to aim airflow up, down, left, or right.

#### Tower vs Air Blanket Mode
- Pivot the fan head **horizontal** for "Air Blanket Mode" — wide, low sheet of airflow ideal across a bed.
- Keep **vertical** (Tower Mode) for normal room cooling.

#### Quiet / Overnight
- **Speeds 1–3** are very quiet and suitable for running through the night.
- Turn **oscillation off** for steady airflow in one direction while sleeping.
