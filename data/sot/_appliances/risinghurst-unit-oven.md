### Electric Oven

- **Power switch** above the washing machine must be ON first (shared with the gas hob).
- **Ignition**: hold down the **clock symbol AND the symbol next to it** simultaneously for a few seconds until the display lights up and stops flashing.
- Then set the function and temperature.
- The cooling fan may run for a few minutes after switching off — that's normal.
