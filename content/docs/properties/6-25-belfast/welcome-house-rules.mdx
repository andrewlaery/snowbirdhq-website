# Welcome

### Dear Guest

Thank you for choosing to stay at SkyCrest. We trust your stay here will be more than enjoyable. For your convenience we have prepared a Guidebook including House Rules to assist you during your stay. Should you experience any problems or difficulties with regard to the home during your stay please do not hesitate to contact us via the booking platform. We are here to help!

### The Home & Neighbourhood

SkyCrest is a spacious apartment perched high on Queenstown Hill, boasting breathtaking sweeping views that stretch across the pristine waters of Lake Wakatipu and the dramatic mountain ranges beyond. From this elevated vantage point in a peaceful, secure neighborhood, guests are treated to panoramic vistas that capture the very essence of Queenstown's spectacular alpine landscape.
Just steps from Lake Wakatipu's shoreline, this private retreat provides tranquility while being only a short walk from the town centre's vibrant array of adventures, shopping, dining, nightlife, and transportation options. The magnificent lake and mountain views serve as a constant backdrop, whether you're relaxing in the apartment's luxurious amenities including a spa, sauna, and hot tub, or simply taking in the scenery from your windows.
The nearby lakeside beaches are perfect for families, offering safe swimming spots for children, peaceful places to sunbathe, and stunning views across Lake Wakatipu's crystal-clear waters that mirror the towering peaks surrounding this remarkable destination.

# House Rules

### A Quick Note

- We're delighted to share our home with you in this peaceful residential neighborhood
- To maintain our great relationship with our neighbors and comply with local regulations, we kindly ask that you follow these house guidelines
- Please remove your shoes inside to help keep our home clean and comfortable

### House Items

* Consumable items in the house are provided free of charge on a fair use basis (this includes basic cooking supplies, tea, coffee, laundry powder, dishwasher capsules/powder, soaps & body wash, etc).
* Unused items remain property of the home.

### Security & Responsibility

* Please help us make your stay an enjoyable and memorable one. Please look after your valuables and secure your accommodation. Despite protective measures we are unable to assume any responsibility and will not be liable for any loss or damage to your possessions in or around the property.
* The hosts/owners are not responsible for any accidents, injuries, or illness that occur while on the premises or its facilities, as well as loss of personal belongings or valuables of the guest. This includes adults and children.
* **Note:** During your stay, the house alarm is not in operation.

### Check-In & Check-Out

* Check-In after: 3pm
* Check-Out before: 10am
**Note:** there are multiple parties who rely on these times for cleaning and maintenance. If you have requirements outside of these times, contact the host and we will do our best to accommodate you.

**When checking out:**
* ensure the house (each room) appears as is when you first arrived
* place all rubbish & recycling (including bottles, cardboard and plastics) in the bins &/or bags provided.
* load all dirty plates and glasses into dishwasher. Do not put any dirty plates, glasses, cutlery, etc away (as if clean).
* turn the lights off and any electrical items you have used. Do not switch off any utilities items such as WiFI, modems, etc)
* turn off heated towel rails and the gas fire
* ensure the house is secured (i.e. all windows and doors)

### Access

* Beside the letterboxes are key lockboxes. Use the code provided in your check in documentation to access the lockbox. You will find a FOB and Key. The FOB gives access to the building and front door, the key gives you access to the guest lockup in the garage.
* Front Gate: The gate maybe be locked, use the FOB to gain access.
* Front Door: Use the FOB to access the Front Door. When opening the door, wait 1 second after you use the FOB, then gently pull the door towards you before opening it.
* **FRONT DOOR IMPORTANT NOTE:** Only swipe the FOB once per entry. Multiple swipes of the FOB may trigger the building alarm. If the building alarm is triggered, please swipe the FOB again to disarm.
* Garage Door: Can be opened and closed using the FOB outside the door and the buttons inside the garage (near the building access door).
* Note: The front door Intercom system does not work. Therefore there is no door bell from the building gate, nor does remote access work from the apartment.

### Guests

* The maximum total number of guests is limited to 6.
* The guest responsible for registering the booking must be 25 years old or older.
* All guests staying must be registered with the host beforehand and only registered guests may spend the night.
* Guests are responsible for the actions of any visitors during their stay.
* Any unregistered guest can be asked to leave at any time without cause. 

### Luggage & Equipment
* Small luggage can be stowed in the apartment, larger items can go in garage near the parking bays where there is storage.
* No sports equipment allowed on house (golf, snowboards / skis / boots, bicycles etc) - please store in garage
* There is also an outdoor  clothes rack located in the laundry for additional drying needs

### Visitors & events/parties

* Strictly registered guests only. No unregistered visitors are permitted.
* Parties are NOT allowed.
* No confetti, glitter, or excessive rubbish allowed otherwise an additional cleaning fee will be charged.
* Failure to comply may result in the termination of the contract, additional charges.

### Quiet Hours & Outdoor Spaces

To ensure everyone in our peaceful neighborhood gets a good night's rest:
- Our lovely outdoor spaces are available for your enjoyment between 7am - 10pm
- We kindly ask that you keep noise levels considerate of our neighbors, especially between 10pm - 7am
- For outdoor areas, please refrain from playing music or amplified sound between 8pm - 8am
- After 8pm, we invite you to continue your activities indoors with windows and doors closed

If you experience any noise concerns from nearby properties during quiet hours, you can contact QLDC Noise Control.

These guidelines help maintain the tranquil atmosphere of our residential area and comply with local council requirements. Thank you for helping us be good neighbors!

Note: Under the District Plan, noise limits are:
- Daytime (8am - 8pm): 50dBA LAeq (15 min)
- Nighttime (8pm - 8am): 40dBA LAeq

### Spa, Sauna and Cold Plunge Usage

* Ensure the spa pool cover is always closed when the spa is not being used.
* Please shower before using the spa pool, sauna and cold plunge to ensure cleanliness
* Only chlorine may be added to the spa pool. Do not add any other substances, including bubbles or foaming agents.
* Food and beverages are not permitted in these areas.
* Glass items are strictly prohibited.
* **Important Notice:** The use of the spa pool, sauna and cold plunge is at your own risk. The host is not liable for any injuries, accidents, or damages that may occur during its use. We kindly request that all guests exercise caution, adhere to safety guidelines, and refrain from using the spa pool under the influence of alcohol or substances. It is your responsibility to ensure that the spa pool is used safely and responsibly. By using the spa pool, you acknowledge and accept these terms.

### Parking

We have convenient on-site parking available for up to 1 vehicle in our internal parking space. To keep things running smoothly:

- Only park in the allocated parking space (avoid street parking where you can)
- Please note that bus/coach drop-offs or pickups are not permitted at the property

While we don't have a designated mobility parking space, our driveway is accessible for loading and unloading.

Thank you for helping us be considerate neighbors by following these parking guidelines. If you have any questions about parking arrangements, please don't hesitate to contact us.

### Rubbish & recycling

### Child safety

* Children must be attended to as there are multiple hazards throughout the house for children.

### Damages/breakages & furniture

* Please respect our home as if it is your own.
* Only use appliances, equipment, furniture, etc for their intended uses.

* Any damage to any items at the property, including sofas, chairs, tables, TVs, beds, sheets, towels, pillows, etc., are the responsibility of the renter.

* By accepting this reservation, it is agreed that the House Rules are understood and accepted, all guests are assuming the risk of any harm arising from their use of the premises or others whom they invite to use the premises.
