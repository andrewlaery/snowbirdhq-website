# Welcome

Dear Guest,

Thank you for choosing to stay at Sunny Shotover Suite. We trust your stay here will be more than enjoyable. For your convenience we have prepared this Guidebook to assist you during your stay. Should you experience any problems or difficulties during your stay please do not hesitate to contact us via the booking platform — your hosts (Paddy & Bethan) live in the main house next door and are very accessible.

### The Home & Neighbourhood

Sunny Shotover Suite is a modern one-bedroom unit at 100 Risinghurst Terrace in Lower Shotover, with a sunny aspect and mountain views. The unit is separate from the main house at the same address. Lower Shotover is ~10 minutes by car to Queenstown Airport and ~12 minutes to the town centre.

### Bedrooms & Sleeping

- **Bedroom 1:** King bed (medium-firmness mattress)
- **Maximum occupancy:** 2 guests (strictly enforced — couch is NOT suitable for sleeping)

# House Rules

### House Items

- Consumable items are provided free of charge on a fair-use basis (cooking basics, tea, instant coffee, soaps).
- Two bikes with helmets are available for guest use.

### Security & Responsibility

- Please look after your valuables and secure your accommodation. Hosts are not liable for loss or damage.

### Check-In & Check-Out

- Check-in after 3pm. Check-out before 10am.
- Self check-in via lockbox by the outdoor sink, between the flat and the shed. Code 1001 sent via the booking platform 3 days before arrival.

### Access

- Walk between the flat and the shed — the lockbox is by the outdoor sink.
- **Important:** there is a SECOND lockbox at the main house. Make sure you go to the correct one for the unit (NOT the main house door).
- Find postbox #100 from the street and come up the driveway.
- Late arrivals — let the host know your ETA and they can leave outdoor lights and heating on.

### Quiet Hours

- Please keep noise levels considerate between 10pm and 7am.

### Parking

- One off-street parking space in the driveway only.
- Park to the right of the main house, by the air conditioning unit. Do NOT block shed access.

### Smoking, Vaping, & E-Cigarettes

- Smoking or vaping is NOT permitted inside the unit.

### Pets

- Pets are NOT permitted.

### Damages

- Please respect our home as if it were your own. Notify the hosts immediately of any damage.
