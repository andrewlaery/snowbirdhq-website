### Bosch Rangehood (DHL555BAU)

[User manual (PDF)](https://media3.bsh-group.com/Documents/9001668589_C.pdf)

Bosch extractor hood installed above the cooking surface to remove cooking vapours, steam, and odours. Located in the kitchen above the induction hobs.

#### Starting the hood

Move the slide control to your desired fan setting:

- Setting 1 — low power, quietest, light cooking
- Setting 2 — medium power, regular cooking
- Setting 3 — high power, intensive cooking
- P — intensive mode for very strong odours/steam

#### Light

- Press the light button to turn on the hood's light.
- The light works independently of the fan.

#### Tips

- Turn the hood on before or as you start cooking.
- Choose a fan speed appropriate to your cooking intensity.
- Run for a few minutes after cooking for best results.
- Turn off the fan by sliding to zero; turn off the light with the light button.

#### Maintenance

- Clean the grease filters every ~2 months.
- Don't use the hood without grease filters installed.
- Never use open flames directly under the hood.
