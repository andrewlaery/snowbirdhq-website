### Dishwasher

Built-in dishwasher in the kitchen.

- **Tablets** are in the cupboard above or beside the dishwasher.
- **Start the dishwasher at check-out** (a guest checkout chore — see Property Orientation → Check-out).
- For a normal mixed load, use the standard / Eco programme.
