### Electric Oven

Standard built-in electric oven.

- **Mains switch** is on the **right-hand wall of the kitchen** — if the oven has no power at all (display blank), check this switch first.
- Allow ~10 min to preheat.
- Use the supplied trays + tea towel — oven gloves are in the kitchen drawer.
