### Shared Dryer (Communal Drying Room)

The complex has a **shared dryer** in the communal drying / storage room.

- **Cost**: $4 per load (2 × $2 coins, pushed in to activate).
- **Access**: code is on the back of the apartment keychain.
- **Location**: drying room is in the lower car-park area of the complex.
- **No dryer in the apartment** — this shared one is the only option, or use the in-apartment drying rack with the heat pump on for faster drying.
