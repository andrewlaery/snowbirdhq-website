### Fisher & Paykel Cooktop
Touch-control cooktop in the kitchen.

- **Isolation switch** is on the wall to the **left of the cooktop**.
- Press and **hold the power button** (the rightmost button on the panel). The display lights up with four zeros (one per hotplate).
- Use **+** or **−** to set the desired temperature for each zone.
- To turn off: return the zone to **0**, then press and hold the power button.
