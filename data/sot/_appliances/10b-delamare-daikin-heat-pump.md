### Daikin Heat Pump (Lounge + Bedroom 1)

Two Daikin heat pumps — one in the lounge, one in the master bedroom (Bedroom 1) — same control layout.

- **Lounge remote**: mounted on the wall in the **kitchen**.
- **Bedroom 1 remote**: mounted on the wall **under the unit** in the master bedroom.
- **Recommended setpoint:** **constant 23°C** for comfort + efficiency.
- **In cold weather**: keep doors and windows closed to retain heat. When leaving for any period, close everything so the house holds temperature for your return.
- **Switch off at check-out.**
