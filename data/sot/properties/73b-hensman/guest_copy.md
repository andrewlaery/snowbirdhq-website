# Welcome

Should you experience any problems or difficulties with regard to the home during your stay, please do not hesitate to contact us via the booking platform. We are here to help!

### The Home & Neighbourhood

Lakeview Lodge Lower (73B Hensman Road) is the lower level of a large lodge-style home in a quiet residential neighbourhood with stunning lake and mountain views. The lodge features five bedrooms, an outdoor spa, an outdoor fire pit, and a double garage — ideal for groups and families.

Pete, the owner, lives directly behind the lodge and can assist in person if needed. Bicycles are available in the garage for guest use.

73A Hensman (the upper level) is a separate listing under the same owners, with shared external areas. Please be considerate of any guests staying upstairs.

Queenstown town centre is approximately 20 minutes downhill on foot, or a short taxi/Uber ride (~$10). The nearest supermarket (FreshChoice) is 3 minutes by car. Coronet Peak ski field is the closest, with The Remarkables approximately 15 minutes further.
