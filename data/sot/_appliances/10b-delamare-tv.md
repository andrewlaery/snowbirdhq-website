### TV (Freeview)

Living-room TV with **Freeview** (free-to-air NZ channels) — no Sky Sport or pay TV.

- Power on with the remote.
- Apps are limited to Freeview built-in — there is no streaming stick wired into this TV.
- The **black house manual** on the kitchen table has Freeview channel listings.
