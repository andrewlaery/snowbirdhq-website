### Garden BBQ

A BBQ in the garden.

- **Before lighting**: check the gas bottle is connected and the valve open.
- **Lighting**: open the lid first (never light with lid closed). Set burner to high, press the igniter button.
- **Cooking**: pre-heat with the lid closed for 5–10 minutes; clean the grill before placing food.
- **After use**: clean (wire-brush the grill, scrape the bottom plate), monitor while in use, and ensure the BBQ is **fully extinguished** (burner knobs OFF, then close the bottle valve).
- **Switch off at checkout**.
