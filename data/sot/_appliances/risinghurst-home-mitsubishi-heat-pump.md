### Mitsubishi Electric Heat Pump (Lounge)

Wall-mounted Mitsubishi Electric reverse-cycle heat pump in the main lounge by the patio door — heats and cools.

- **Remote**: kept beside the unit or on the coffee table.
- **Mode buttons**: ☀ sun icon = heat, ❄ snowflake = cool, water-drop = dry, fan icon = fan-only.
- **Fan speed**: separate button on the remote — Auto / Low / Med / High.
- **Setpoint**: 20–22 °C in heating; 24–26 °C in cooling.
- **Reach**: heats the open-plan main living level effectively. Bedrooms have separate portable electric heaters for cold nights.
