### Beko Built-in Electric Oven (OIM 22301 X, 65 L)

A 65-litre built-in electric oven with two pop-out knobs flanking the digital clock display.

- **Knobs**: press to release (they pop out), then turn. **Left knob** = function (bake / fan / grill / etc). **Right knob** = temperature.
- **Clock**: must be set after any power cut, or the oven won't operate. Use the dial buttons by the display to set time.
- **Cooling fan**: keeps running 20–30 min after the oven turns off — that's normal, don't unplug.
- **Trays + racks**: standard Beko side rails support 3–4 levels.
