### Breville Creatista Plus Coffee Machine

Nespresso pod machine with milk frother — kitchen bench.

#### Making Coffee
1. Fill the water tank with fresh water.
2. Insert a Nespresso capsule.
3. Select cup size (espresso or lungo).
4. Press the brew button.

#### Frothing Milk
1. Fill the milk jug to the desired level.
2. Select milk temperature + texture.
3. Place the jug under the steam wand.
4. Press the milk button.

Empty drip tray + capsule bin when full.
