# Welcome

Dear Guest,

Thank you for choosing to stay at Unit 3, Whistler Apartments. We trust your stay here will be more than enjoyable. For your convenience we have prepared this Guidebook to assist you during your stay. Should you experience any problems or difficulties with regard to the apartment during your stay please do not hesitate to contact us via the booking platform. We are here to help!

### The Home & Neighbourhood

Unit 3, Whistler Apartments is a cosy one-bedroom apartment with a balcony and mountain views, ideal for couples or solo travellers. The home is in a central location, a short walk from the town centre with restaurants, shops, and Lake Wakatipu nearby.

### Bedrooms & Sleeping

- **Bedroom 1:** Queen bed
- **Sofa bed in living area** for an additional guest (must be requested in advance, $50 charge)
- **Maximum occupancy:** 2 guests (3 with sofa bed)
- A baby cot is available on request.

# House Rules

### House Items

- Consumable items are provided free of charge on a fair-use basis (cooking basics, tea, coffee, soaps).
- Black house manual is on the kitchen counter — please leave it in place at checkout.

### Security & Responsibility

- Please look after your valuables and secure your accommodation. Hosts are not liable for loss or damage to your possessions.

### Check-In & Check-Out

- Check-in after 3pm. Check-out before 10am.
- Self check-in via the digital lock — code sent via the booking platform 24 hours before arrival.
- Whistler Apartments reception is NOT affiliated with this listing — please contact us via the booking platform for any issues.

### Access

- Digital lock on the front door (installed Feb 2026, replacing the old physical key/lockbox).
- Your access code is sent via the booking platform 24 hours before arrival.

### Quiet Hours

- Please keep noise levels considerate between 10pm and 7am.

### Parking

- Street parking only — no dedicated parking spot.

### Smoking, Vaping, & E-Cigarettes

- Smoking or vaping is NOT permitted inside the apartment or on the balcony.

### Pets

- Pets are NOT permitted.

### Building-Managed Issues

- Issues with the building (hot water, lift, noisy neighbours) are managed by the apartment complex, not the host. Please contact us via the booking platform if you need help reaching them.
