Select a property from the sidebar to view its guest guide.

Each property has four sections:

- **Guest Guide** — landing page with quick links
- **Welcome & House Rules** — what to expect and the house rules
- **User Instructions** — Wi-Fi, appliances, heating, and more
- **Critical & Essential Information** — emergency contacts and safety info
