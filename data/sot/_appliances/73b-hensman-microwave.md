### Microwave

Standalone microwave on the kitchen bench.

- **Power on**: open the door to wake the unit, place the food inside, close the door.
- Set time using the digital pad (or the dial, depending on model).
- Set power level if needed (defaults to high).
- Press **Start**.
- **Use microwave-safe dishes only** — no metal, no foil.
- Wipe the interior after spills.
