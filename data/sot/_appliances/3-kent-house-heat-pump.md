### Mitsubishi Ceiling Heat Pump (Dining)
Ceiling-mounted heat pump in the dining area.

- **Remote control** is on the wall **next to the kitchen bench**.

#### Operating
1. Point the remote at the ceiling unit.
2. Press the **on button**.
3. Press the **mode button** to select **Auto / Heat / Cool / Dry**.
4. Use **up / down arrows** to adjust temperature.
