### Electric Blinds (TV Room)

The large 2-storey blinds in the **TV room** (the gas-fire room with the Frame TV) are **electric / motorised** but operated **manually** via wall switches — no remote, no app, no smart-home automation for these.

- **Two control points**, both wall-mounted **double switches**:
    1. **In the TV room** — to the right of the window under the art.
    2. **In the master bedroom** — controls the same blinds from the other side of the house.
- Press the **up** switch to raise, **down** switch to lower.
- Either switch operates the same blinds — useful when the master-bedroom door is closed at night and you want to drop them without crossing the house.
