### Samsung Smart TV (Lounge)

A Samsung Smart TV in the lounge — screen shows artwork in sleep mode (Frame-style).

- **White remote**: power, navigation, source.
- Press **Home** for the main menu.
- **Streaming services**: Netflix etc. via the Samsung TV app — sign in with your own account; please sign out before leaving so the next guest doesn't see your profile.
- **NZ Freeview**: free over-the-air channels.
