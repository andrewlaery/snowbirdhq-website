### TV with Chromecast

The living-room TV has **Chromecast capability** — cast from your phone or laptop.

- **Cast from a phone**: open the Chromecast-enabled app (Netflix, YouTube, Disney+, Spotify), tap the cast icon, choose the TV.
- **Cast from a laptop (Chrome browser)**: ⋮ menu → Cast → choose the TV.
- **Sign out of any personal accounts** before checking out.
- TV remote on the side table — power on first if the screen is blank.
