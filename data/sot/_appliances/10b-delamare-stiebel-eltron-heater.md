### Stiebel Eltron Panel Heaters (Bedroom 2 + Bedroom 3)

Wall-mounted electric panel heaters in the back two bedrooms.

- **Three-step turn-on** for each panel:
    1. **Plug in** at the wall socket.
    2. **Turn on** the **wall switch**.
    3. **Turn on** the **side switch on the heater** itself.
- Adjust temperature using the **dial on the panel** — slow to warm, gentle radiant heat.
- **Please switch panel heaters OFF when checking out.**
