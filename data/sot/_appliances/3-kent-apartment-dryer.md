### Electrolux Dryer
Electrolux dryer.

#### Operating
1. **Clear the lint filter** before each load.
2. Place items inside.
3. Close the door securely.
4. Press the **power button** (top-left corner of control panel).
5. Select the type of items to dry.
6. Press **Start**.
