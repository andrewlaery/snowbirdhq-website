### Iron and Ironing Board

Iron and ironing board available on the property.

- Stored in the **laundry cupboard** or main entrance hall cupboard.
- Fill the iron's water tank with **clean water** before use.
- Set temperature according to fabric type (low for synthetics, medium for wool, high for cotton/linen).
- Wait for the **ready indicator** before ironing.
- **Empty the water tank** after use to prevent scale build-up.
- **Turn off and unplug** when finished.
