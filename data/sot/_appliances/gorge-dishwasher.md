### Haier Dishwasher

A built-in Haier dishwasher.

- **Tablets**: under the sink — drop one in the dispenser inside the door.
- **Operation**: rotate the program knob to select a cycle, then press the centre button to start.
- **Eco mode** recommended — longest cycle, lowest energy + water.
- **Run before checkout** — please start the dishwasher before you leave (loaded with the last items used).
