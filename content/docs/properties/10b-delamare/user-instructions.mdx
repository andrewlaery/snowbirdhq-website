# User Instructions

### Internet / WiFi

The internet WiFi can be accessed by:
* **Network:** TP-Link_F8D6
* **Password:** provided on the welcome card at check-in

The Wi-Fi modem is next to the dining table. A Wi-Fi booster is located upstairs in the single/king bedroom — please do not unplug it.

### Access & Security

* **Entry:** Lockbox on the wooden fence to the left-hand side as you walk up the steps. Code provided in your booking information. The lockbox contains the front door key and a garage remote fob.
* **Garage Remotes:** There are two garage doors operated by separate remotes. One remote is kept in the house by the black house manual on the kitchen table. To open the garage from inside, use the wall switches at the bottom of the steps leading from the living area to the garage.
* **Flood light switch:** Located in the door leading to the garage — illuminates the driveway area at night.

### Layout

* **Two levels** connected by internal stairs (carpeted, with handrail).
* **Downstairs:** Entry, kitchen, living area, laundry.
* **Upstairs:** Bedrooms and bathroom.

### Bed Configuration

* **Master bedroom:** Queen/king bed with cupboard (stores the travel cot). Vanity with flick mixer. Blinds are operated via a cord mechanism.
* **At least one bedroom** can be configured as a king bed OR two singles — please request your preferred setup before arrival.
* **Travel cot:** Available on request (stored in the master bedroom cupboard or the garage). The cot comes with a mattress protector. Please bring your own cot sheets and linen. Up to two cots can be provided.
* **Highchair:** Available on request (stored in the garage).

### Heating

* **Daikin heat pump (lounge):** Remote is mounted on the wall in the kitchen. We recommend keeping the temperature at a constant 23°C.
* **Master bedroom heat pump:** Same controls as the lounge. Remote is under the unit, mounted on the wall.
* **Panel heaters (each bedroom):** Must be plugged in at the wall socket AND turned on via the wall switch AND the side switch on the heater itself. Adjust the temperature using the dial at the top.
* **Heated towel rails:** In the upstairs bathroom — please turn off at checkout.

### Hot Water

* The house has a **hot water cylinder** (not continuous flow). It comfortably serves 6 guests under normal use, but will run out if all showers, the washing machine, and kitchen taps are used simultaneously.
* **Reheat time:** 40–60 minutes.
* **Tip:** Stagger showers, especially in the morning.

### Kitchen

* **Touch induction hob:** Tap to select a zone, then adjust the temperature using the slider.
* **Oven:** Standard electric. The **timer must be turned on for the oven to heat properly** — this is a common source of "oven not working" reports. If the oven isn't heating, check the timer.
* **Oven mains switch:** In the cubby hole on the right-hand side of the kitchen, by the toaster. If the oven has no power at all, check this switch.
* **Dishwasher:** Eco mode recommended. Tablets are under the kitchen sink.
* **Chest freezer:** Located in the garage for additional frozen storage.
* **Tap water is safe to drink.**

### Bathroom

* Upstairs bathroom with a heated towel rail.
* **Hair dryer** stored under the sink in the upstairs bathroom.
* The flick mixer on the vanity can be stiff — please use a firm but gentle touch.

### Laundry

* **Washing machine** in the laundry room by the kitchen. Laundry powder is provided.
* **No dryer** — outdoor clothes horse to the left of the back door, plus an indoor drying rack is provided.
* **Iron and ironing board** in the master bedroom.

### Living Area

* **TV** with Freeview channels (no Sky Sport or pay TV).
* The **black house manual** on the kitchen table has quick-reference info on Wi-Fi, appliances and property details.

### Outdoor Deck

* The grand deck offers panoramic mountain and lake views.
* Available for guest use 7am–10pm.
* Limited outdoor dining seating — may not seat the full six.

### Garage

* **Two garage doors** with separate remotes (left and right).
* **From inside:** Wall switches at the bottom of the steps from the living area.
* **Size:** Fits small/medium cars, bikes, and ski gear. Larger vehicles (SUVs, 7-seaters, cars with low body kits) will not fit.
* **Chest freezer** for additional frozen storage.
* Salt for de-icing the driveway is in a large white bag inside the garage.
* Please store all shoes and ski gear in the garage — not indoors.

### Utility Infrastructure

* **Fuse box:** In the laundry room next to the kitchen.
* **Flashlight:** Bottom drawer of the kitchen.
* **International electronics:** High-wattage appliances and voltage converters may trip the fuses — please take care.
* Check the Aurora Energy Queenstown outage page for area-wide power information during any outage.

### Getting Around / Transportation

* **Town centre:** ~7 minutes by car.
* **Queenstown Airport:** ~10–15 minutes by car.
* **Bus:** ~15 minute walk to the bus stop on Frankton Road. Use the Transit app for timetables. Walking from the stop back to the property is steep and uphill (~20 minutes).
* **Ride-share:** Uber is available in Queenstown. **QT Taxis:** 03 450 3000.
* **Free parking in town:** Skyline area (90-minute limit), Man Street (no time limit), Camp Street (cheap paid option).
