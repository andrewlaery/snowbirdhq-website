### Electric Oven

Standard built-in electric oven.

- **CRITICAL — timer must be turned ON for the oven to heat properly.** This is the single most common "oven not working" report. If the oven won't heat, set the timer first.
- **Mains switch** is in the **cubby hole on the right-hand side of the kitchen, by the toaster**. If the oven has no power at all (display blank), check this switch.
- Allow ~10 min to preheat to 180°C.
- Use the supplied trays + a tea towel — oven gloves can be in the second drawer.
