### Bosch Rangehood (DIB099950)

[User manual](https://www.bosch-home.co.nz/supportdetail/product/DIB099950/02)

The rangehood buttons are soft-touch — they can be tricky to operate. Pressing harder will not make them respond; instead it can damage the rangehood. When pressing, move your finger lightly on the button to activate it.
