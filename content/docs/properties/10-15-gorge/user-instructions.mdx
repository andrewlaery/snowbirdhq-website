## Property Orientation

### Internet / Wi-Fi

The Wi-Fi network and password are shown above in the Quick Reference. If you have connectivity issues, switch the modem off and on again (unplug for 20 seconds, plug back in).

### Bed Configuration

- Queen bed.
- **Travel cot** available on request. Please notify host before arrival.
- **Highchair** available on request.

### Heating

- **Heat pump** in the living area heats the whole apartment (see Appliances → Heating).
- **Bathroom underfloor heating** + **heated towel rail** — see Appliances.

### Hot Water

- Hot water is managed by the building manager and should be instant.
- If there are issues with hot water supply, call the building manager on **03 409 0394** or use the phone by reception.

### Bathroom

Shower. Heated towel rail (please turn off at checkout). Underfloor heating. Washing machine in the bathroom (see Appliances → Laundry).

### Tap Water

Tap water is safe to drink throughout the apartment.

### Ski, Bike & Luggage Storage

- **Ski storage:** Ski/dryer room below the apartments. Use the ski lock provided.
- **Bike rack:** Outside the drying room in the car park. Use a bike lock.
- **Luggage storage** (pre-check-in / post-check-out): storage room in the garage area below reception. Walk down the steep driveway from reception, turn right into the garage. **Door code: C8743.** Alternative: Bounce.com for luggage storage in town.

### Balcony

- Available for use 7am–10pm.
- **No laundry on the balcony** (body corporate rule).
- **Nothing stored on the balcony** (body corporate rule).

### Utility Infrastructure

Check the [Aurora Energy Queenstown outage page](https://www.aurora.energy/outages) for area-wide power information during any outage.

### Getting Around

- **Bus:** By the Four Square in town, $5 cash or Bee Card (cheaper). Use the Transit app or orc.govt.nz for timetables.
- **Airport bus:** Number 1 bus from opposite Super Liquor on Stanley Street — $4 cash only, goes directly to the airport.
- **Uber** available. **QT Taxis:** 03 450 3000.
- **Queenstown Airport:** ~10–15 minutes by car.

### Nearby Shops

- **Fresh Choice** — ~5 min walk (across the road). Open until 10pm.
- **Raeward Fresh** — ~5 min walk (deli and butchers).
- **Pak'n'Save** and **Countdown** — Frankton, ~20 min drive.
- **Four Square** — in town centre.

### Check-out

- Return the key to the lockbox.
- Load the dishwasher.
- Dispose of rubbish in the bins.
- Switch off lights and heated towel rail.
- Leave bed linens in place and towels in their original rooms.
