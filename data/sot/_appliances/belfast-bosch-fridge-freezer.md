### Bosch Fridge / Freezer (with Filtered Water)

Built-in Bosch fridge / freezer in the kitchen.

#### Filtered Water Dispenser
- The fridge has an **integrated filtered water dispenser**.
- **Use this for drinking water** — the kitchen tap water is not filtered.
- Push your glass against the lever to fill; release to stop.
- A small drip tray catches overflow.

#### Fridge / Freezer Use
- **Fridge**: keep at 3–5°C; check the digital display on the door.
- **Freezer**: -18°C is standard for frozen storage.
- Adjust via the control panel on the inside of the fridge door if needed (please return settings to standard before leaving).
- Allow ~30 min for the cold to recover after long door-opens (e.g. unloading a full grocery shop).

#### Notes
- Please **don't unplug or switch off** the fridge during your stay.
- Wipe up spills promptly to prevent stains and odours.
