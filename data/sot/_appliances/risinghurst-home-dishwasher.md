### Dishwasher

A built-in dishwasher.

- **Tablets** are provided.
- **Loading**: cutlery basket on the lower rack; plates and pots lower; glasses and cups upper.
- **Programs**: select on the front panel — Eco recommended.
- **Run before checkout** — please start the dishwasher before you leave.
