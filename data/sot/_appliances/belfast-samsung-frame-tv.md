### Samsung The Frame TV (QA65LS03FWSXNZ) — Main Living

65" QLED art TV in the main living area — displays artwork when not in active use.

- **Power on**: press the power button on the remote.
- **Watching TV**: standard channel + volume buttons; **HOME** for apps.
- **Art Mode**: press power **once** to switch to Art Mode (low-power artwork display).
- **Full off**: press and **hold** the power button.
- Sign out of any personal streaming accounts before check-out.
