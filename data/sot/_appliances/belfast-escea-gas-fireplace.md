### Escea Gas Fireplace (IB600/IB850)

Built-in gas fire insert — Living Room. Provides ambient heat and atmosphere.

- **Wall timer switch** with **blue light** is on the wall to the **left of the fireplace** — supplies power.
- **Remote** is kept on the **living-room table**.

#### Starting the Fireplace
1. **Press the timer switch** on the wall (blue indicator light comes on).
2. **Press Power** on the remote. Check the digital display shows a temperature — if not, press Power again.
3. The fireplace takes a few seconds to respond and ignite.
4. Adjust flame height using the remote.

#### After Use
- **Turn the fire off via the remote** after use.
