### Touch Induction Hob

Glass-top touch induction cooktop in the kitchen.

- **Selecting a zone**: tap the zone icon, then drag along the slider to set temperature.
- **Turning off a zone**: slide the slider back to 0.
- **Child-safety lock**: press and hold the **key button** to lock the entire control panel; hold it again for **3 seconds** to unlock.
- Use **flat-bottomed magnetic cookware** (induction-compatible).
- **Wipe the surface clean** when cool — small splatters cook on if left.
