### MIDEA Microwave (EM720CL7S)
Bench-top microwave.

- **Isolation switch** is to the **right of the microwave**.
- Push the button in the **bottom-right corner** of the microwave to open the door.
- Close the door, press **TIME**, enter minutes, press **START**.
