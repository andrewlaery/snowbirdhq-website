### Underfloor Heating

The living area has electric underfloor heating that warms the floor surface.

- **Programmed on a timer** to come on in the **evenings and mornings** — no guest action needed for normal use.
- **Slow to warm and slow to cool** — radiant heat is gentle but takes 30–60 min to feel.
- **No bedroom-specific heaters** — underfloor + the gas fireplace are the only heating sources for the lodge.
- If you need it on at a different time, let the host know via the booking platform.
