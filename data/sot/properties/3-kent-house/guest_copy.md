### The Home & Neighbourhood

The upstairs apartment at 3 Kent Street is the larger and more premium of the two units in the home — 4 bedrooms with the best views in the building. An over-engineered 80s build with texture-rich character throughout: wallpapered walls, a large kitchen with pantry, two large living areas. Underfloor heating throughout, set to a constant 20°C.

The garden (downstairs / shared with the lower apartment if let) has fruit trees and direct access to the Queenstown Hill walking track — recommended if you'd like a walk straight from the house.

**Note**: the downstairs apartment may be occupied during your stay (it's a separate listing). Sound carries between the levels — the floor between is concrete but boots and heavy footsteps are audible. Be considerate.
