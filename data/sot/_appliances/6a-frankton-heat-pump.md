### Heat Pump (Heating + Cooling)

Wall-mounted heat pump in the living area — does **both heating and cooling**.

- **Guest-controlled** via the remote (kept on the kitchen counter or in the black booklet).
- **Heat in winter**: set to 21–23°C; **cool in summer**: set to 22–24°C.
- Allow ~10 minutes to reach setpoint.
- **Switch off when checking out** or leaving the apartment for an extended period.
