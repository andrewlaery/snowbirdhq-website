### The Home & Neighbourhood

De La Mare Views is a spacious standalone two-level home in Frankton's quiet, elevated De La Mare neighbourhood. The grand outdoor deck captures panoramic mountain and lake views across Lake Wakatipu. The property sits between Frankton Road and the Five Mile shopping precinct — roughly **7 minutes by car** to Queenstown town centre and a short walk to Frankton's cafes, restaurants, and services. A peaceful residential retreat close to the action but away from it.
