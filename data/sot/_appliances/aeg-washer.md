### AEG Washing Machine

- Turn on with the **On/Off** button.
- Detergent goes in the tray on the left-hand side.
- Select the preferred wash cycle using the wheel.
- Press **Start/Pause**.
- Please leave the washer door open when not in use.
- Laundry powder is provided under the laundry sink.
