## Property Orientation

### Internet / Wi-Fi

- **Network:** TP-Link_F8D6.
- **Password:** provided on the welcome card at check-in.
- The Wi-Fi modem is **next to the dining table**.
- A **Wi-Fi booster** is located **upstairs in the single/king bedroom** — please do not unplug it.

### Layout

- **Two levels** connected by internal stairs (carpeted, with handrail).
- **Downstairs**: entry, kitchen, living area, laundry.
- **Upstairs**: bedrooms and bathroom.

### Bed Configuration

- **3 bedrooms** all upstairs:
    - **Bedroom 1 (master):** queen bed. Cupboard stores the travel cot. Vanity with flick mixer. Blinds on a cord mechanism.
    - **Bedroom 2:** queen bed.
    - **Bedroom 3:** **split-king** — configurable as **1 king OR 2 singles**. Please request your preferred setup before arrival.
- **Travel cot** available on request (stored in the master bedroom cupboard or the garage). Comes with a mattress protector — please bring your own cot sheets and linen. Up to two cots can be provided.
- **Highchair** available on request (stored in the garage).

### Hot Water

- The house has a **hot water cylinder** (not continuous flow). It comfortably serves 6 guests under normal use, but will run out if all showers, the washing machine, and kitchen taps are used simultaneously.
- **Reheat time: 40–60 minutes.**
- **Tip:** stagger showers, especially in the morning.

### Bathroom

- Upstairs bathroom with a **heated towel rail** — please turn off at check-out.
- **Hair dryer** stored under the sink in the upstairs bathroom.
- The flick mixer on the vanity can be **stiff** — use a firm but gentle touch.

### Outdoor Deck

- The grand deck offers panoramic mountain and lake views.
- **Available for guest use 7am–10pm.**
- Limited outdoor dining seating — may not seat the full six.

### Garage

- **Two garage doors** with separate remotes (left and right).
- **From inside**: wall switches at the bottom of the steps from the living area.
- **Size**: fits small/medium cars, bikes, and ski gear. **Larger vehicles (SUVs, 7-seaters, cars with low body kits) will not fit.**
- Salt for de-icing the driveway is in a **large white bag inside the garage**.
- **Please store all shoes and ski gear in the garage** — not indoors.

### Utility Infrastructure

- **Fuse box**: in the laundry room next to the kitchen.
- **Flashlight**: bottom drawer of the kitchen.
- **International electronics**: high-wattage appliances and voltage converters may trip the fuses — please take care.
- Check the [Aurora Energy Queenstown outage page](https://www.aurora.energy/outages) for area-wide power information during any outage.

### Getting Around

- **Town centre:** ~7 minutes by car.
- **Queenstown Airport:** ~10–15 minutes by car.
- **Bus:** ~15-minute walk to the bus stop on Frankton Road. Use the **Transit app** for timetables. Walking from the stop back is **steep and uphill (~20 minutes)**.
- **Ride-share:** Uber available. **QT Taxis:** 03 450 3000.
- **Free parking in town:** Skyline area (90-minute limit), Man Street (no time limit), Camp Street (cheap paid option).

### Nearby

- **Pak'n'Save (Frankton)** — nearest supermarket and best prices.
- **Five Mile shopping area** — short drive, variety of shops and services.
- **Sherwood Hotel & Restaurant** — 554 Frankton Road (03 450 1090).
- **Boatshed Cafe** — 847 Frankton Road (03 441 4146).
- **Altitude Brewing** — 827 Frankton Road (03 442 4507).
- **The FAT** — local pub, a short walk away.
- **Giovis** (Italian, small — book ahead), **Bombay Palace** (Indian), **Hikari** (Japanese) all nearby.

### Mid-stay Clean

- Not included as standard for stays under 7 nights.
- Can be arranged on request for longer stays. Cost: $400–450 per clean.

### Luggage Storage

- The garage can be used for luggage storage before check-in or after check-out — please arrange with the host in advance.

### Check-out

- Return the key to the lockbox.
- Hand-rinse any dishes; start the dishwasher.
- Switch off lights, the heat pumps, the panel heaters, and the heated towel rail.
- Leave bed linens in place and towels in the bathroom.
- Lock all windows and doors.
