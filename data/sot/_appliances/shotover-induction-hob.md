### Touch Induction Hob

A touch-controlled induction cooktop.

- **Cookware must be ferrous** — a magnet should stick to the base. Aluminium / copper / glass pans won't heat.
- **Touch controls**: tap the power button, select a zone, then use the slider / + / − to set heat.
- **Hold the power button** for ~2 seconds to switch the hob off.
- **Detailed instructions** for this hob are in the house manual on the dining table.
