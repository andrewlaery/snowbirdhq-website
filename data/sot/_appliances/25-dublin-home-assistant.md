### Home Assistant Voice ("OK Nabu")

The home runs **Home Assistant** with a voice assistant — wake word **"OK Nabu"**.

#### Quick examples
- **"OK Nabu, turn on the lounge lights"**
- **"OK Nabu, what's the temperature inside?"**
- **"OK Nabu, play music in the kitchen"**
- **"OK Nabu, close the garage door"**

#### How it works
- The voice puck sits on the kitchen bench (or wherever you spot it). Speak from anywhere within ~5m line-of-sight.
- It controls lights, climate, audio, and security devices that have been set up by the host. Not every device in the house is wired in — if a command doesn't work, that device probably isn't on the network.
- **Privacy**: voice processing runs on the home's local server (no cloud) — recordings are not retained.

#### Troubleshooting
- **"OK Nabu" doesn't wake it**: speak slightly louder; wait for the soft chime before issuing the command.
- **"I didn't catch that"**: rephrase more naturally ("turn off the kitchen lights" rather than "lights off please").
- If the voice puck is unresponsive, it may have been unplugged — please contact the host.
